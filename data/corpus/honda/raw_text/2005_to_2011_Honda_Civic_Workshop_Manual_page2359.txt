2008 Honda Civic GX
2006-08 ENGINE Engine Block - Civic (Except Hybrid)
PISTON INSTALLATION
If the Crankshaft is Already Installed
1. Set the crankshaft to bottom dead center (BDC) for each cylinder as its piston
is installed.
2. Remove the connecting rod caps, then install the ring compressor. Check that
the bearing is securely in place.
3. Apply new engine oil to the piston, inside of the ring compressor, and the
cylinder bore, then attach the ring compressor to the piston/connecting rod
assembly.
4. Position the mark (A) to face the cam chain side of the engine.
Fig. 123: Identifying Mark To Face Cam Chain Side Of Engine
Courtesy of AMERICAN HONDA MOTOR CO., INC.
5. Position the piston in the cylinder, and tap it in using the wooden handle of a
hammer (A). Push down on the ring compressor (B) to prevent the rings from
expanding before entering the cylinder bore.
