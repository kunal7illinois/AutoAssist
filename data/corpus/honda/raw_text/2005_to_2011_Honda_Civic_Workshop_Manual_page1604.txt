17. Remove the cylinder head.
CAMSHAFT INSPECTION
1. Remove the camshaft sprocket, refer to the CAMSHAFT SPROCKET
REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION .
2. Remove the rocker arm assembly, refer to the ROCKER ARM ASSEMBLY
REMOVAL .
3. Put the rocker shaft holders on the cylinder head, then tighten the bolts, in
sequence, to the specified torque.
