2008 Honda Civic LX
2006-08 ACCESSORIES & EQUIPMENT Audio System - Civic (Except Hybrid)
SUBWOOFER
1. Remove the rear shelf (see TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION - REAR
SHELF AREA ).
2. Remove the four mounting bolts from the subwoofer (A).
Fig. 143: Identifying Four Mounting Bolts From Subwoofer
Courtesy of AMERICAN HONDA MOTOR CO., INC.
3. Disconnect the 2P connector (B), and remove the subwoofer.
4. Install in the reverse order of removal.
AUXILIARY JACK ASSEMBLY REPLACEMENT
1. With navigation: Remove the navigation unit (see NAVIGATION UNIT
REMOVAL/INSTALLATION ).
Without navigation: Remove the audio unit (see AUDIO UNIT
REMOVAL/INSTALLATION).
2. Carefully pull out the auxiliary jack assembly (A), then disconnect the 5P
connector (B).
