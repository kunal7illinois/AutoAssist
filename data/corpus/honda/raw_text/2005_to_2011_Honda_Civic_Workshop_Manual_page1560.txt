INSTALLATION
1. Remove all of the old liquid gasket from the water outlet mating surfaces,
bolts, and bolt holes.
2. Clean, and dry the water outlet mating surfaces.
3. Apply liquid gasket, P/N 08717-0004,08718-0001, 08718-0002, 08718-0003,
or 08718-0009, evenly to the cylinder head mating surface of the water outlet.
NOTE: Do not install components if too much time has passed
after applying the liquid gasket (for P/N 08718-0002, no
more than 4 minutes, for all others, no more than 5
minutes). Instead, remove the old residue and reapply
the liquid gasket.
