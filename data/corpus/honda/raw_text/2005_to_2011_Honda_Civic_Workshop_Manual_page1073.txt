2006-08 TRANSMISSION Automatic Transmission - Civic (Except Hybrid)
SHIFT LOCK SOLENOID REPLACEMENT
1. Remove the shift lever assembly (see SHIFT LEVER REMOVAL ).
2. Remove the shift lock solenoid connector.
3. Release the shift lock solenoid lock (A), then remove the shift lock solenoid
(B).
Fig. 348: Removing Shift Lock Solenoid Lock
Courtesy of AMERICAN HONDA MOTOR CO., INC.
4. Replace the shift lock solenoid (A), solenoid plunger (B), and plunger spring
(C) assembly.
