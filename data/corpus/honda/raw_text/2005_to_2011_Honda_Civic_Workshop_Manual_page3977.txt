GENERAL INFORMATION Service Reminder Indicators - Honda
OXYGEN SENSOR WARNING LIGHT
1994-95 PASSPORT (DX MODEL)
1. Heated Oxygen Sensor (HO2S) must be replaced every 90,000 miles. When
odometer reaches 90,000 miles, O2S indicator light on dash will illuminate,
and then every subsequent 90,000 miles. After servicing, turn off or reset
warning light.
NOTE: Reset procedure is for first 90,000 mile reset. At every
subsequent 90,000 mile reset, move screw back to
previous hole.
2. To reset warning light, remove instrument cluster. Remove masking tape from
hole "B". See Fig. 6 or Fig. 7 . Remove screw from hole "A" and insert into
hole "B". Apply new masking tape to hole "A".
