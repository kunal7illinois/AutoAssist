VSA OFF SWITCH TEST
1. Remove the driver's dashboard lower cover (see DRIVER's DASHBOARD
LOWER COVER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION ).
2. Push out the VSA OFF switch (A) from the back of the instrument panel.
Fig. 67: Identifying VSA Off Switch (A) From Back Of Instrument Panel
3. Disconnect the VSA OFF switch 5P connector (B).
4. Check for continuity between VSA OFF switch 5P connector terminals No. 1
and No. 2. There should be continuity when the switch is pressed, and no
continuity when the switch is released.
