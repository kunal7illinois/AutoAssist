SRS UNIT REPLACEMENT
REMOVAL
NOTE: If you are disconnecting only SRS unit connector A, skip
step 2.
1. Disconnect the battery negative cable, then wait for 3 minutes before starting
work.
2. Disconnect both seat belt tensioner connectors (see step 7 DISCONNECTING
SYSTEM CONNECTORS) and both seat belt buckle tensioner connectors
(see step 8 DISCONNECTING SYSTEM CONNECTORS).
3. Remove the center console (see CENTER CONSOLE
REMOVAL/INSTALLATION ).
4. Disconnect SRS unit connector A (28P), connector B (28P) and remove the
TORX bolts (C), then pull out the SRS unit.
