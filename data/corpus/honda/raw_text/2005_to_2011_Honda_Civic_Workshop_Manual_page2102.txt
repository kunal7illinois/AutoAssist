Installation
1. Install the new rubber stop (A) and lock washer (B) on the rack shaft. Align the
lock washer tabs (C) with the slots (D) in the rack shaft. Install the rack end (E)
while holding the lock washer in place. Repeat this step for the other side of
the rack shaft.
