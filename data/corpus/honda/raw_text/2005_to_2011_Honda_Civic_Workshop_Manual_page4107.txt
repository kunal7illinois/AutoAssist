Fig. 128: Installing Rear Seat Cushion Cover (4-Door)
REAR SEAT ARMREST COVER REPLACEMENT
FOR SOME MODELS - 4-DOOR
NOTE: Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
1. Remove the armrest from the seat-back (see REAR SEAT ARMREST
REPLACEMENT ).
2. Remove the armrest beverage holder from the armrest (see REAR SEAT
ARMREST BEVERAGE HOLDER REPLACEMENT ).
3. Release the clips (A) and hook strip (B), and pull back the armrest cover (C) all
the way around.
