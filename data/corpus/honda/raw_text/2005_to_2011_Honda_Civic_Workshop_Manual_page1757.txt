2008 Honda Civic GX
2006-08 DRIVELINE/AXLES Driveline/Axle - Civic (All Except Hybrid)
1. Wrap the splines with vinyl tape (A) to prevent damaging to the outboard boot.
Fig. 38: Identifying Clamp Bands And Splines With Vinyl Tape
Courtesy of AMERICAN HONDA MOTOR CO., INC.
2. Install the new ear clamp bands (B) and outboard boot, then remove the vinyl
tape. Be careful not to damage the outboard boot.
3. Install the new stop ring into the driveshaft groove (A).
Fig. 39: Identifying Stop Ring Into Driveshaft Groove
Courtesy of AMERICAN HONDA MOTOR CO., INC.
4. Pack about 35 g (1.2 oz) grease included in the new outboard boot set into the
driveshaft hole in the outboard joint.
NOTE: If you are installing a new outboard joint, the grease is
already installed.
