2008 Honda Civic GX
2006-08 ENGINE Cooling System (R18A1) - Civic (Except Hybrid)
1. Drain the engine coolant (see COOLANT REPLACEMENT ).
2. Remove the air cleaner assembly (see THROTTLE BODY CLEANING ).
3. Remove the air cleaner housing bracket (A), then remove the harness holder
(B) from the cylinder head.
Fig. 24: Identifying Air Cleaner Housing Bracket And Harness Holder
Courtesy of AMERICAN HONDA MOTOR CO., INC.
4. Disconnect the engine coolant temperature (ECT) sensor 1 connector (C).
5. Remove the air cleaner housing bracket (A), upper radiator hose (B), and water
bypass hose (C).
