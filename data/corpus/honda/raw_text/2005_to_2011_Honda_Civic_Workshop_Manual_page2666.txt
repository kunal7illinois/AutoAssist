YES -Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the FTP sensor and the

indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.

YES -Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the FTP sensor and the
PCM. If the PCM was updated, substitute a known-good PCM (see
SUBSTITUTING THE PCM ), then recheck. If the PCM was substituted, go
