2008 Honda Civic LX
2006-08 TRANSMISSION Manual Transmission (SPFM) 5-Speed) - Civic
TRANSMISSION FLUID INSPECTION AND REPLACEMENT
1. Raise the vehicle on the lift.
2. Remove the filler plug (A) and washer (B), check the condition of the fluid,
and make sure it is at the proper level (C).
Fig. 5: Identifying Filler Plug And Washer
Courtesy of AMERICAN HONDA MOTOR CO., INC.
3. If the fluid is dirty, remove the drain plug (A), and drain it.
Fig. 6: Identifying Drain Plug (With Torque Specifications)
Courtesy of AMERICAN HONDA MOTOR CO., INC.
4. Install the drain plug with a new washer, and refill the transmission fluid to the
proper level.
Fluid Capacity
1.4 L (1.5 US qt) at fluid change
1.6 L (1.7 US qt) at overhaul
