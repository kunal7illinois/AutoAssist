ENTERING THE SELF-DIAGNOSTIC FUNCTION (MANUAL METHOD)
Before doing the self-diagnostic function, check the No. 10 (7.5 A) fuse in the
under-dash fuse/relay box and the No. 23 (10 A) fuse in the under-hood fuse/relay
box.
