DISPLAY DAY/NIGHT MODE DOES NOT WORK .
1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
2. Turn the combination light switch ON and LOCK (0) to see if the symptom
can be duplicated.
Is it duplicated?
YES -Go to step 3.
NO -Operation is normal.
3. Turn the combination light switch OFF.
4. Operated the illumination control dial.
Is it normal?
YES -Operation is normal.
NO -Go to step 5.
5. Disconnect and check audio unit connector A (17P) for loose or a poor
connection.
NOTE: Eject all the discs before removing the audio unit to
prevent the CD player's load mechanism.
Reconnect audio unit connector A (20P) and recheck the symptom; does it still
appear?
YES -Go to step 6.
NO -Operation is normal.
