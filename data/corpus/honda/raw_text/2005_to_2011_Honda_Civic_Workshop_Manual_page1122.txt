MAINSHAFT 3RD GEAR AXIAL CLEARANCE INSPECTION
1. Remove the mainshaft transmission housing bearing (see BEARING
REMOVAL ).
2. Install the thrust needle bearing (A), 3rd gear (B), the needle bearing (C), the
thrust needle bearing (D), the 40 x 63 mm thrust washer (E), the 3rd/5th clutch
(F), the 5th gear collar (G), and the transmission housing bearing (H) on the
mainshaft (I). Do not install the O-rings during inspection.
