Speaker check mode
5. Turn off the audio unit.
6. Push and hold the No. 1 and No. 3 buttons. While holding the buttons, push the
VOL push PWR knob to ON. Release the buttons and the speaker check mode
will begin. A low-frequency hum should sound for about one minute. Change
the test speaker by push the SKIP button. If you find a speaker(s) with no
sound, check the speaker and harness connections. If the connections are good,
replace the speaker and retest.
7. The self-diagnostic function ends when the audio unit is turned off, or the
ignition switch to LOCK (0).
ERROR CODES
The audio system displays error codes when a problem is detected with the disc
changer, the disc, the tape player, the XM radio, or the anti-theft code.
