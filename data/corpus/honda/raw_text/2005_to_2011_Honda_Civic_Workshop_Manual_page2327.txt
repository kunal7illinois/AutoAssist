CRANKSHAFT MAIN BEARING REPLACEMENT
Main Bearing Clearance Inspection
1. To check main bearing-to-journal oil clearance, remove the lower block and
bearing halves (see CRANKSHAFT AND PISTON REMOVAL ).
2. Clean each main journal and bearing half with a clean shop towel.
3. Place one strip of plastigage across each main journal.
4. Reinstall the bearings and lower block, then torque the bolts to 29 N.m (3.0
kgf.m, 22 lbf.ft) + 56 Â° in the proper sequence (see step 19 ).
NOTE: Do not rotate the crankshaft during inspection.
5. Remove the lower block and bearings again, and measure the widest part of the
plastigage.
