PARK PIN SWITCH TEST
1. Remove the center console (see CENTER CONSOLE
REMOVAL/INSTALLATION ).
2. Move the shift lever into P, and check for continuity between park pin
switch/A/T gear position indicator panel light connector terminals No. 3 and
No. 4. There should be no continuity.
