cable.
3. Remove the battery.
4. Remove the cowl cover and under-cowl panel (see FRONT GRILLE
COVER REPLACEMENT ).
5. Remove the air cleaner assembly (see THROTTLE BODY CLEANING ).
6. Remove the harness clips (A) and the intake air duct (B), then remove the
battery base (C) with the coolant reservoir (D).
