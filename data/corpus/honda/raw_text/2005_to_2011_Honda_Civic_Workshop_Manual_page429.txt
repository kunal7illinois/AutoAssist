2008 Honda Civic EX
2006-08 RESTRAINTS SRS (Supplemental Restraint System) - Civic (Except Hybrid)
1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery, then wait for 3 minutes before
starting work.
2. Disconnect the appropriate side airbag 2P connector (see step 4
DISCONNECTING SYSTEM CONNECTORS).
3. Remove the front seat assembly (see FRONT SEAT
REMOVAL/INSTALLATION ).
4. Remove the front door sill inner trim:
2-door (see TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION - DOOR AREAS )
(cid:1)
4-door (see FRONT DOOR SILL AREA - 4-DOOR )
(cid:1)
5. Remove the lower B-pillar lower trim panel (see B-PILLAR
UPPER/LOWER TRIM - 4-DOOR ).
6. Disconnect the floor wire harness 4P connector from the side impact sensor
(first).
7. Using a TORX T30 bit, remove the TORX bolt (A), then remove the side
impact sensor (first) (B).
Fig. 335: Identifying Side Impact Sensor And Torx Bolt
Courtesy of AMERICAN HONDA MOTOR CO., INC.
INSTALLATION
1. Install the side impact sensor (first) with a new TORX bolt (A), then connect
the floor wire harness 4P connector (B) to the side impact sensor (first).
