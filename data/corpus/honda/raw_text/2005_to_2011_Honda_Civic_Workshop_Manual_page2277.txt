OIL PAN REMOVAL
1. If the engine is already out of the vehicle, go to step 12.
2. Remove the drive belt (see DRIVE BELT INSPECTION ).
3. Remove the A/C condenser fan shroud (see A/C COMPRESSOR
REPLACEMENT ).
4. Disconnect the A/C compressor clutch connector, then remove the harness
clamp. Remove the A/C compressor without disconnecting the A/C hoses (see
step 39 on ENGINE REMOVAL ).
5. Raise the vehicle on the hoist to full height.
6. Remove the splash shield (see step 23 on ENGINE REMOVAL ).
7. Drain the engine oil (see ENGINE OIL REPLACEMENT ).
