59. Check that the engine/transmission is completely free of vacuum hoses, fuel

60. Slowly lower the engine/transmission about 150 mm (6 in.). Check once again
that all hoses and electrical wiring are disconnected and free from the
engine/transmission, then lower it all the way.
61. Disconnect the chain hoist from the engine/transmission.
62. Raise the vehicle all the way on the hoist, and remove the engine/transmission
from under the vehicle.
