2008 Honda Civic GX
2006-08 ENGINE PERFORMANCE Fuel Supply System - Civic GX
1. Lift the vehicle and support it with jackstands.
2. Close the manual shut-off valve (A).
Fig. 76: Identifying Manual Shut-Off Valve
3. Start the engine, and let it idle. After a few minutes, the engine will stall.
4. Turn the ignition switch OFF, and disconnect the negative cable from the
battery.
5. Connect a ground wire (A) (available from AH special tools department) to the
fuel line to eliminate any static electricity.
Fig. 77: Identifying Ground Wire, Fuel Supply Pipe And Fuel Pressure
Regulator P1
6. Disconnect the fuel supply pipe (B) from fuel pressure regulator P1 (C). Be
careful not to lose the O-rings.
