9. Turn the ignition switch OFF. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery,
then wait for 3 minutes.
10. Disconnect both seat belt tensioner 4P connectors (see step 7
DISCONNECTING SYSTEM CONNECTORS) and the driver's seat belt
buckle tensioner 4P connector (see step 8 DISCONNECTING SYSTEM
CONNECTORS).
11. Disconnect SRS unit connector B (28P) from the SRS unit (see step 9
DISCONNECTING SYSTEM CONNECTORS).
12. Disconnect the simulator lead from the floor wire harness.
13. Reconnect the negative cable to the battery.
14. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
15. Measure the voltage between the No. 9 terminal of SRS unit connector B (28P)
and body ground, and the No. 10 terminal and body ground. There should be
0.5 V or less.
