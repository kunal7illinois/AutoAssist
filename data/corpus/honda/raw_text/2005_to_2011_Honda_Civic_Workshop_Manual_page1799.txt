2008 Honda Civic EX
2006-08 ENGINE PERFORMANCE EGR System (R18A1) - Civic (All Except Hybrid)
NOTE: Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and any on-
board snapshot, and review the general troubleshooting
information (see GENERAL TROUBLESHOOTING
INFORMATION ).
1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
2. Check EGR VLS in the DATA LIST with the HDS. Is 4.88 V or more
indicated?
YES - Go to step 3.
NO - Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor
connections or loose terminals at the EGR valve and the ECM/PCM.
3. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
4. Disconnect the EGR valve 6P connector.
5. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
6. Measure voltage between EGR valve 6P connector terminals No. 2 and No. 3.
