Use this procedure when you need to substitute a known-good PCM during
troubleshooting procedures.
1. Make sure you have the anti-theft code for the audio system or the navigation
system (if equipped), then write down the audio presets.
2. Connect the HDS to the DLC (A) located behind the driver's dashboard lower
cover.
