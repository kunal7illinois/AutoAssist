DTC P1193: FTP SENSOR CIRCUIT HIGH VOLTAGE
NOTE: Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and any on-
board snapshot, and review the general troubleshooting
information (see GENERAL TROUBLESHOOTING
INFORMATION ).
1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
2. Check the FTP SENSOR in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is there about 4.61 V or more?
YES -Go to step 3.
NO -Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor
connections or loose terminals at the FTP sensor and the PCM.
3. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
4. Disconnect the FTP sensor 3P connector.
5. Connect FTP sensor 3P connector terminals No. 1 and No. 3 with a jumper
wire.
