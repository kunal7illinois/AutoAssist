OUTSIDE AIR TEMPERATURE SENSOR TEST
1. Remove the outside air temperature sensor (see OUTSIDE AIR
TEMPERATURE SENSOR REPLACEMENT ).
2. Dip the sensor in ice water, and measure the resistance. Then pour warm water
on the sensor, and check for a change in resistance.
3. Compare the resistance reading between the No. 1 and No. 2 terminals of the
outside air temperature sensor with the specifications shown in Fig. 28 ; the
resistance should be within the specifications.
