2008 Honda Civic LX
2006-08 TRANSMISSION Automatic Transmission - Civic (Except Hybrid)
TORQUE CONVERTER HOUSING
MAINSHAFT BEARING AND OIL SEAL REPLACEMENT
Special Tools Required
Adjustable bearing puller, 25-40 mm 07736-A01000B or 07736-A01000A
(cid:1)
Driver 07749-0010000
(cid:1)
Attachment, 62 x 68 mm 07746-0010500
(cid:1)
Attachment, 72 x 75 mm 07746-0010600
(cid:1)
1. Remove the mainshaft bearing and oil seal using the adjustable bearing puller
and a commercially available 3/8"-16 slide hammer (A).
Fig. 397: Removing Mainshaft Bearing And Oil Seal
Courtesy of AMERICAN HONDA MOTOR CO., INC.
2. Install a new mainshaft bearing until it bottoms in the torque converter housing
using the driver and the 62 x 68 mm attachment.
