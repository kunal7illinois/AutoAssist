2008 Honda Civic GX
2006-08 BRAKES VSA System - Civic (All Except Hybrid)
1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
2. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
3. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), then turn it ON (II) again.
4. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC 86-01 indicated?
YES -Go to step 5.
NO -Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for loose
terminals between ECM connector A (44P) and the VSA modulator-control
unit 37P connector. Refer to INTERMITTENT FAILURES
TROUBLESHOOTING (see ).
5. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
6. Short the SCS line with the HDS.
7. Disconnect ECM connector A (44P).
8. Disconnect the HDS from the data link connector (DLC).
9. Disconnect the gauge control module (tach) 36P connector.
10. Disconnect SRS unit connector A (28P).
11. With EPS: Disconnect EPS control unit connector D (28P).
12. With TPMS: Disconnect the TPMS control unit 20P connector.
13. Disconnect the yaw rate-lateral acceleration sensor 5P connector.
14. Disconnect the VSA modulator-control unit 37P connector.
15. Check for continuity between VSA modulator-control unit 37P connector
terminals No. 1 and No. 15 and body ground individually.
