INSTALLATION
1. Install the SRS unit (A) with new TORX bolts (B), then connect the connectors
(C) to the SRS unit; push them into position until they click.
NOTE: Be sure the SRS unit is sitting squarely against it's
bracket before torquing the TORX bolt.
