2008 Honda Civic GX
2006-08 ENGINE Engine Block - Civic (Except Hybrid)
OIL PAN INSTALLATION
1. Remove all of the old liquid gasket from the oil pan mating surfaces, bolts, and
bolt holes.
2. Clean, and dry the oil pan mating surfaces.
3. Apply liquid gasket, P/N 08717-00004, 08718-0001, 08718-0002, 08718-0003,
or 08718-0009, evenly to the engine block mating surface of the oil pan.
NOTE: Do not install components if too much time has passed
after applying the liquid gasket (for P/N 08718-0002, no
more than 4 minutes, for all others, no more than 5
minutes). Instead, remove the old residue and reapply
the liquid gasket.
