2006-08 ACCESSORIES & EQUIPMENT Glass - Civic (All Except Hybrid)
Use seat covers to avoid damaging any surface.

1. Remove the A-pillar trim (see TRIM REMOVAL/INSTALLATION -
PILLAR AREAS ).
2. From inside the vehicle, use a knife (A) to cut through the front corner glass
adhesive (B) all the way around. Apply protective tape along the edge of the
entire front corner glass opening flange.
Fig. 18: Cutting Through Front Corner Glass Adhesive Using Knife
3. From outside the vehicle, pry the front corner glass clips (A) and the front
corner trim clips (B), then carefully remove the glass (C) and trim (D) together.
The trim has not be attached to the glass.
