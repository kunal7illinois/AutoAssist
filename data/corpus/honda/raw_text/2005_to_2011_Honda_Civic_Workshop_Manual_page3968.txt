1998-2001 PRELUDE
1. At each 7500 mile service interval, the MAINTENANCE REQUIRED light
will change from Green to Yellow. If service is not performed (and light is not
reset), the MAINTENANCE REQUIRED light will change from Yellow to
Red.
2. When service has been completed, reset MAINTENANCE REQUIRED
reminder light. To reset reminder light, turn ignition off. Insert ignition key in
