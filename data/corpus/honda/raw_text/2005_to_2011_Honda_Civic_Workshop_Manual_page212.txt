2008 Honda Civic EX
2006-08 RESTRAINTS SRS (Supplemental Restraint System) - Civic (Except Hybrid)
1. Clear the DTC memory (see CLEAR THE DTC MEMORY WITH THE
HDS).
2. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and check that the SRS indicator comes on
for about 6 seconds and then goes off.
Does the SRS indicator stay on, and is DTC 28-1x indicated?
YES -Go to step 3.
NO -Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Go to
TROUBLESHOOTING INTERMITTENT FAILURES . If another DTC is
indicated, troubleshoot the DTC.
3. Turn the ignition switch OFF. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery,
then wait for 3 minutes.
4. Disconnect the floor wire harness 4P connector (A) from the front passenger's
seat belt buckle tensioner connector (B).
Fig. 114: Identifying Floor Wire Harness 4P And Front Passenger's Seat
Belt Buckle Tensioner Connectors
Courtesy of AMERICAN HONDA MOTOR CO., INC.
5. Connect the SRS inflator simulator (2 ohms connectors) and simulator lead K
to the floor wire harness connector.
6. Reconnect the negative cable to the battery.
7. Clear the DTC memory.
8. Read the DTC (see CLEAR THE DTC MEMORY WITH THE HDS).
