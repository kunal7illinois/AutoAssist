2008 Honda Civic EX
2006-08 ACCESSORIES & EQUIPMENT Consoles - Civic (All Except Hybrid)
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the armrest and related parts.
1. Remove the center console armrest (see CENTER CONSOLE ARMREST
REPLACEMENT ).
2. Using a T15 TORX bit, remove the screws. Slide the armrest liner (A)
downward to release the hooks (B), then remove the armrest liner and armrest
spring (C).
Fig. 18: Removing Armrest Liner And Spring
3. Using a T15 TORX bit, remove the screws.
Fig. 19: Removing Armrest Screw
4. Slide the armrest (A) all the way forward, using a T15 TORX bit, remove the
screws, then remove the armrest backbone (B).
