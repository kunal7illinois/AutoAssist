2008 Honda Civic EX
2006-08 ACCESSORIES & EQUIPMENT Wipers/Washers - Civic (All Except Hybrid)
Wiper arms stop position
1. When the wiper arms stop at the automatic stop position, confirm that they are
at the standard position.
2-door
a: Position at about 1.1 in. (29 mm) from the top of cowl cover (A)
b: Position at about 4.1 in. (106 mm) from the top of cowl cover (A)
4-door
a: Position at about 0.9 in. (23 mm) from the top of cowl cover (A)
b: Position at about 3.8 in. (96 mm) from the top of cowl cover (A)
Washer nozzle position
2. When you turn on the washers, confirm 50% or more of the washer fluid lands
within the spray area. If the spray area is not within the standard positions,
adjust the nozzles.
