REAR SEAT SIDE BOLSTER COVER REPLACEMENT
NOTE: Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
1. Remove the seat side bolster (see SEAT SIDE BOLSTER - 4-DOOR ).
2. Release all the hook strips (A), and fold back the seat side bolster cover (B),
and release the cover from the hooks (C).
Fig. 121: Releasing Rear Seat Side Bolster Cover From Hooks
3. Install the cover in the reverse order of removal, and to prevent wrinkles when
installing a side bolster cover, make sure the material is stretched evenly over
the pad before securing the hook strips and staples.
