NOTE:
Always use Honda Long Life Antifreeze/Coolant
(cid:1)
Type 2 (P/N OL999-9001). Using a non-Honda
coolant can result in corrosion, causing the cooling
system to malfunction or fail.
Honda Long Life Antifreeze/Coolant Type 2 is a
(cid:1)
mixture of 50 % antifreeze and 50 % water. Do not
add water.
