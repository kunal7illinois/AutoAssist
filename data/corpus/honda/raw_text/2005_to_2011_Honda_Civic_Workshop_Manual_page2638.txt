6. Monitor the OBD STATUS for DTC P0191 in the DTCs MENU with the
HDS.
Does the screen indicate FAILED?
YES -Go to step 7.
NO -If the screen indicates PASSED, intermittent failure, the system is OK at
this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the fuel rail
pressure sensor and the PCM. If the screen indicates EXECUTING, keep
driving until a result comes on. If the screen indicates OUT OF CONDITION,
go to step 4 and recheck.
7. Start the engine. Hold the engine speed at 3,000 rpm without load (in Park or
