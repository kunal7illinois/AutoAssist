RACK GUIDE ADJUSTMENT
Special Tools Required
Locknut wrench, 40 mm 07MAA-SL00100 or 07916-SA50001
(cid:1)
Locknut wrench, 43 mm 07MAA-SL0020A
(cid:1)
HYDRAULIC POWER STEERING TYPE
1. Set the wheels in the straight ahead position.
2. Loosen the rack guide screw locknut (A) with the locknut wrench, then remove
the rack guide screw (B).
Fig. 27: Identifying Rack Guide Screw Locknut, Locknut Wrench & Rack
Guide Screw
3. Remove the old sealant from the rack guide screw (A), and apply new sealant
(Three Bond 1215 or Loctite 5699) to the middle of the threads (B). Loosely
install the rack guide screw on the steering gearbox.
