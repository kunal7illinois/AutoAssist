11. Inspect A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve A (see A/T CLUTCH

Does A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve A operate properly?
YES -Repair the hydraulic system related with shift valve A, or replace the

12. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
13. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
14. Test-drive the vehicle with the shift lever in D, and let the transmission shift
through all five gears.
15. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0). Repeat the test-drive in step 14, then go
to step 16.
16. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0747 indicated?
YES -Go to step 9.
NO -Go to step 17.
17. Monitor the OBD STATUS for P0747 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the HDS indicate PASSED?
YES -Troubleshooting is complete. If any other DTCs were indicated in step
16, go to the INDICATED DTC'S TROUBLESHOOTING.
NO -If the HDS indicates FAILED, go to step 9. If the HDS indicates NOT
COMPLETED, go to step 14.
