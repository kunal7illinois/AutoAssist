DTC 107-22: CENTRAL PROCESSING UNIT (CPU) INTERNAL CIRCUIT MALFUNCTION
1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0) to cool the VSA modulator-control unit,
and wait 1 hour or more.
2. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
3. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
4. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), then turn it ON (II) again.
5. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC 107-22 indicated?
YES -Replace the VSA modulator-control unit (see VSA MODULATOR-
CONTROL UNIT REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION ).
NO -The system is OK at this time.
