DRIVE BELT INSPECTION
1. Inspect the belt for cracks and damage. If the belt is cracked or damaged,
replace it.
2. Check that the auto-tensioner indicator (A) is within the standard range (B) as
shown. If it is out of the standard range, replace the drive belt (see DRIVE
BELT INSPECTION ).
