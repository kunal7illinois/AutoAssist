3. Check for abnormal noise from the tensioner pulley. If you hear abnormal

TENSIONER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION ).
4. Remove the drive belt (see DRIVE BELT INSPECTION ).
5. Set a long-handled, boxed-end wrench on the drive belt auto-tensioner from
above the engine. Slowly turn the wrench in the direction shown. Check that
the tensioner moves smoothly and without any abnormal noise. If the tensioner
does not move smoothly, or you hear abnormal noise, replace the auto-
tensioner (see DRIVE BELT AUTO-TENSIONER
REMOVAL/INSTALLATION ).
NOTE: This is a hydraulic type auto-tensioner; you must turn
the wrench slowly.
