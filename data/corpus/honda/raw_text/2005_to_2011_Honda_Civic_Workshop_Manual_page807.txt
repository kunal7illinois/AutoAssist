7. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).

9. Test-drive the vehicle in 2nd gear with the shift lever in D at speeds over 7
mph (12 km/h) and the engine speed above 1,000 rpm, for at least 12 seconds.
10. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0). Repeat the test-drive in step 9, then go
to step 11.
11. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
