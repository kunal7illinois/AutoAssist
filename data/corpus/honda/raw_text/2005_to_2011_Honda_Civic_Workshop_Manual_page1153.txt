12. For the 1st, 3rd, and 5th clutches: Press the clutch compressor attachment
down with 150-160 N (15-16 kgf, 33-35 lbf) (B) using a force gauge, and read
the dial indicator (C). The dial indicator reads the clearance (D) between the
clutch end-plate and top disc (E). Take measurements in at least three places,
and use the average as the actual clearance.
13. For 2nd and 4th clutches: Press the clutch compressor attachment down with
39 N (4 kgf, 9 lbf) (B) using a force gauge, and read the dial indicator (C). The
dial indicator reads the clearance (D) between the clutch end-plate and top disc
(E). Take measurements in at least three places, and use the average as the
actual clearance.
Clearance between Clutch End-Plate and Top Disc Service Limit:
1st Clutch: 1.62-1.82 mm (0.064-0.072 in.)
2nd Clutch: 0.7-0.9 mm (0.028-0.035 in.)
3rd Clutch: 0.93-1.13 mm (0.037-0.044 in.)
4th Clutch: 0.7-0.9 mm (0.028-0.035 in.)
5th Clutch: 0.93-1.13 mm (0.037-0.044 in.)
