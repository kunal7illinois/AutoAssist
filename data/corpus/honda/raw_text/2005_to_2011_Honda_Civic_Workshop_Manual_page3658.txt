Gray Tone
This screen looks for problems with contrast. You should be able to see the changes
from bar to bar across the scale. If you can't see the shade changes from bar to bar,
replace the navigation unit.
Fig. 44: Diagnostic Menu Screen Display - Gray Tone
Courtesy of AMERICAN HONDA MOTOR CO., INC.
White Raster
This diagnostic screen checks for pixels that may be dead (off). The entire display
must be white. If pixels are dead, replace the navigation unit.
