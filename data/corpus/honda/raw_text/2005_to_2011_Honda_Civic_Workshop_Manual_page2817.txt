DTC B1183: GAUGE CONTROL MODULE LOST COMMUNICATION WITH THE EPS CONTROL
UNIT (EPS MESSAGE)
1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
2. Turn the ignition switch OFF, and then back ON (II).
3. Wait for 6 seconds or more.
4. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC B1183 indicated?
YES - Go to step 5.
NO - Intermittent failure. The gauge control module (tach) is OK at this time.
Check for loose or poor connections.
5. Check for DTCs with the HDS.

YES - Troubleshoot DTC B1178.
NO - Go to step 6.
