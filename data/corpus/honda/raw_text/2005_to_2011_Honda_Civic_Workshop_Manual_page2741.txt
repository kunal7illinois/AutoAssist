Is there continuity?
YES - Go to step 14.
NO - Repair open in the wire between the gauge control module (tach) (signal
line) and the fuel gauge sending unit, then go to step 24.
14. Remove the jumper wire from the fuel tank unit 4P connector.
15. Remove the fuel tank unit (see FUEL TANK UNIT
REMOVAL/INSTALLATION ).
16. Test the fuel gauge sending unit (see FUEL GAUGE SENDING UNIT
TEST ).
Is the fuel gauge sending unit OK?
YES - Go to step 17.
NO - Replace the fuel gauge sending unit (see FUEL PUMP/FUEL GAUGE
SENDING UNIT REPLACEMENT ), then go to step 23.
17. Connect the fuel tank unit 4P connector.
18. Reconnect the gauge control module (tach) 36P connector.
19. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
20. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
