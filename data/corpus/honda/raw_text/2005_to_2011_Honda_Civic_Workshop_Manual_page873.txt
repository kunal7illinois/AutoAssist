Does the HDS indicate FAILED?
YES -Go to step 8.
NO -If the HDS indicates PASSED, intermittent failure, the system is OK at
this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals between A/T clutch
pressure control solenoid valve B and the PCM. If the HDS indicates NOT
COMPLETED, go to step 5.
