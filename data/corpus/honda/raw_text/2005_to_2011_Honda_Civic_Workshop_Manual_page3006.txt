MAX COOL POSITION FUNCTION
When the mode control button is in the MAX A/C position, the HVAC control unit
will automatically select the recirculation mode and turn the A/C on. If the
recirculation switch is pressed when in MAX A/C, MAX A/C turns off. If A/C is
pressed when in MAX A/C, the A/C turns off.
