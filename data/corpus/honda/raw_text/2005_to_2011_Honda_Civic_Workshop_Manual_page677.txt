Power Flow
P Position
Hydraulic pressure is not applied to the clutches. Power is not transmitted to the
countershaft. The countershaft is locked by the park pawl interlocking the park
gear.
N Position
Engine power transmitted from the torque converter drives the mainshaft idler gear,
the idler gear shaft idler gear, and the secondary shaft idler gear, but hydraulic
pressure is not applied to the clutches. Power is not transmitted to the countershaft.
