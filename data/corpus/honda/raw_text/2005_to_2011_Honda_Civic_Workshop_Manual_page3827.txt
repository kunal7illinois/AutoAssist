31. Set the 11 vanes (C) in the grooves in the rotor. Make sure that the round ends
(D) of the vanes are in contact with the sliding surface of the cam ring.
32. Place the side plate (A) on the cam ring, and align the roll pin set hole (B) with
the roll pin (C).
Fig. 32: Identifying Align Roll Pin Set Hole With Roll Pin
33. Coat the new O-ring (D) with power steering fluid, then position it into the
groove on the side plate.
34. Install the pump housing (A) over the cover assembly (B).
