COUNTERSHAFT REVERSE SELECTOR HUB REMOVAL
1. Remove the reverse selector hub from the countershaft by hand. If the reverse
selector hub (A) cannot be removed by hand, it is press-fitted to the
countershaft (B), and remove it from the countershaft with a press. Place 4th-
5th gear (C) on press bases (D), and place a shaft protector (E) between the
countershaft and press to prevent damaging the countershaft.
NOTE: Some reverse selector hubs are not press-fitted, and
can be removed without using a press.
