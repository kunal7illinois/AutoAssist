Connector Terminal No. 2 And Body Ground
Is there continuity?
YES - Repair short to body ground in the wire between the gauge control
module (tach) and the brake fluid level switch.
NO - Go to step 12.
12. Reconnect the gauge control module (tach) 36P connector.
13. Disconnect the parking brake switch IP connector.
14. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
Does the brake system indicator go off?
YES - Replace the parking brake switch (see PARKING BRAKE CABLE
REPLACEMENT ).
NO - Go to step 15.
15. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
16. Disconnect the gauge control module (tach) 36P connector.
17. Check for continuity between parking brake switch 1P connector terminal No.
1 and body ground.
