Does the brake system indicator go off?
YES -Replace the parking brake switch (see PARKING BRAKE CABLE
REPLACEMENT ).
NO -Go to step 8.
8. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
9. Disconnect the gauge control module (tach) 36P connector.
10. Check for continuity between parking brake switch 1P connector terminal No.
1 and body ground.
Fig. 59: Checking Continuity Between Parking Brake Switch 1P
Connector Terminal No. 1 And Body Ground
Is there continuity?
YES -Repair short to body ground in the wire between the gauge control
module (tach) and the parking brake switch.
NO -Check for loose terminals in the gauge control module (tach) 36P
connector. If necessary, substitute a known-good gauge control module (tach),
then go to step 1 and recheck. If it is OK, replace the original gauge control
module (tach) (see REPLACEMENT ).
