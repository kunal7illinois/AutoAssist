NOTE: Use fender covers to avoid damaging painted surfaces.
1. Make sure you have the anti-theft code for the audio system or the navigation
system (if equipped), then write down the audio presets.
2. Remove the cowl cover and under-cowl panel (see COWL COVER
REPLACEMENT ).
3. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery, then disconnect the positive
cable.
4. Remove the battery.
5. Remove the air cleaner assembly (see AIR CLEANER ELEMENT
INSPECTION/REPLACEMENT ).
6. Remove the harness clips (A) and the intake air duct (B), then remove the
battery base (C) with the coolant reservoir (D).

(B) to avoid bending the clutch line.
