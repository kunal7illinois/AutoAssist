DTC INDICATOR 3: A PROBLEM IN THE AIR MIX CONTROL LINKAGE, DOOR, OR MOTOR
1. Clear the DTC (see CLEAR THE DTCS ).
2. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), and then ON (II).
3. Do the self-diagnostic with the heater control panel (see HOW TO USE THE
SELF-DIAGNOSTIC FUNCTION ).
4. Check for DTCs.
Is DTC 3 indicated?
YES -Go to step 5.
NO -Intermittent failure.
5. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
6. Test the air mix control motor (see AIR MIX CONTROL MOTOR TEST ).
Is the air mix control motor OK?
YES -Substitute a known-good heater control panel, and recheck. If the
symptom/indication goes away, replace the original heater control panel.
NO -Replace the air mix control motor (see AIR MIX CONTROL MOTOR
TEST ), or repair the air mix control linkage or door.
