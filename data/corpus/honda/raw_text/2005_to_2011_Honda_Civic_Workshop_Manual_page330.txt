Does the SRS indicator stay on, and is DTC 81-79 indicated?
YES -Go to step 3.
NO -Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Go to
TROUBLESHOOTING INTERMITTENT FAILURES. If another DTC is
indicated, troubleshoot the DTC.
3. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
4. Make sure nothing is on the front passenger's seat.
5. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and check that the SRS indicator comes on
for about 6 seconds and then goes off.
Does the SRS indicator go off?
YES -The system is OK.
NO -Remove the front passenger's seat assembly (see FRONT SEAT
REMOVAL/INSTALLATION ) and the front passenger's weight sensors
(see FRONT PASSENGER'S WEIGHT SENSOR REPLACEMENT), then
reinstall them. Calibrate the ODS unit (see ODS UNIT CALIBRATION). If
DTC 81-79 is indicated repeatedly, replace the front passenger's weight
sensors. If the DTC is still present, replace the SRS unit (see SRS UNIT
REPLACEMENT).
