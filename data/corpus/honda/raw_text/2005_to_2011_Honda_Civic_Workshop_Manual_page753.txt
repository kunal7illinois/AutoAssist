56. Monitor the OBD STATUS for P0705 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Does the HDS indicate PASSED?
YES -Troubleshooting is complete. If any other DTCs were indicated in step
55, go to the INDICATED DTC'S TROUBLESHOOTING.
NO -If the HDS indicates FAILED, check for intermittent short to body ground
in the wire between the transmission range switch and the PCM, then go to step
1. If the HDS indicates NOT COMPLETED, go to step 53.
