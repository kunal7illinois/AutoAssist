D, D3 Position
In D and D3, the optimum gear is automatically selected from 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and 5th
gears in D; 1st, 2nd, and 3rd gears in D3 according to conditions such as the
balance between the throttle opening (engine loading) and the vehicle speed.
