2008 Honda Civic LX
2006-08 TRANSMISSION Automatic Transmission - Civic (Except Hybrid)
1. Install the mainshaft holder onto the mainshaft.
Fig. 491: Identifying Mainshaft Holder Onto Mainshaft
Courtesy of AMERICAN HONDA MOTOR CO., INC.
2. Lubricate the following parts with ATF:
Splines and threads of the mainshaft.
(cid:1)
Splines of the mainshaft idler gear.
(cid:1)
Old conical spring washer and old locknut.
(cid:1)
3. Install the mainshaft idler gear (A), the old conical spring washer (B), and the
old locknut (C) on the mainshaft (D), and tighten the locknut to 216 N.m (22.0
kgf.m, 159 lbf.ft).
NOTE:
Do not tap the idler gear to install.
(cid:1)
Use a torque wrench to tighten the locknut. Do not
(cid:1)
use an impact wrench.
