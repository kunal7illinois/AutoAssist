COUNTERSHAFT REVERSE SELECTOR HUB INSTALLATION
Special Tools Required
Driver, 40 mm I.D. 07746-0030100
1. Install 1st gear, 2nd gear, 3rd gear, the 33.2 x 40 x 26 mm collar, the 29 mm
cotters, and the 33 x 45 x 7.8 mm collar on the countershaft, and secure them
with the snap ring.
2. Install the needle bearings and 4th-5th gear.
3. Slide the reverse selector hub (A) over the countershaft (B), then press it in
place using the 40 mm I.D. driver and a press.
NOTE: Some reverse selector hubs are not press-fitted, and
can be installed without using the special tool and a
press.
