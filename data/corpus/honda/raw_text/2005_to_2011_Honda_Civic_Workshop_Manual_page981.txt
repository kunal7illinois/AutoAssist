3RD CLUTCH TRANSMISSION FLUID PRESSURE SWITCH REPLACEMENT
1. Raise the vehicle on a lift, or apply the parking brake, block the rear wheels,
and raise the front of the vehicle. Make sure it is securely supported.
2. Remove the splash shield.
3. Disconnect the 3rd clutch transmission fluid pressure switch connector, then
remove the 3rd clutch transmission fluid pressure switch (A).
