2008 Honda Civic EX
2006-08 STEERING EPS System - Civic (All Except Hybrid & Si)
1. Remove the boot bands (A) and discard them. Remove the tie-rod clips (B),
and pull the boots away from the ends of the steering gearbox.
Fig. 111: Identifying Boot Bands
2. Unbend the lock washer (A).
Fig. 112: Identifying Unbend Lock Washer (A)
3. Unscrew both rack ends (A) with a wrench. Be careful not to damage the rack
shaft surface with the wrench. Remove the lock washer (B) and rubber stop (C)
and discard them.
NOTE: Hold the flat surfaces of the rack shaft on the pinion
shaft side with another wrench.
