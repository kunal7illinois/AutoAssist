DISASSEMBLY
1. Remove the cap (A) from the top of the damper.
Fig. 41: Identifying Damper Cap
2. Compress the damper spring, then remove the self-locking nut (A) using the
strut nut adapter (B) while holding the damper shaft with a hex wrench (C). Do
not compress the spring more than necessary to remove the nut.
Fig. 42: Identifying Compress Damper Spring
3. Release the pressure from the strut spring compressor, then disassemble the
damper as shown in the Exploded View.
