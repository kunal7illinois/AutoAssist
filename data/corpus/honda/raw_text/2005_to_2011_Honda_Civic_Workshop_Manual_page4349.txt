DTC 27-23: STEERING ANGLE SENSOR COUNTER MALFUNCTION
1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
2. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
3. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), then turn it ON (II) again.
4. Turn the steering wheel from lock to lock.
5. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC 27-23 indicated?
YES -Replace the steering angle sensor (see STEERING ANGLE SENSOR
REPLACEMENT ).
NO -Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.
