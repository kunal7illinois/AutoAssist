2008 Honda Civic GX
2006-08 BRAKES ABS System - Civic (All Except Hybrid)
REMOVAL - EXCEPT SI MODEL
1. Disconnect the ABS modulator-control unit 25P connector (A) by pulling up
the lock (B); the connector disconnects itself.
Fig. 38: Removal And Installation Of ABS Modulator-Control Unit (With
Specifications)
2. Disconnect the six brake lines from the ABS modulator-control unit.
NOTE: Brake lines are connected to the master cylinder (C)
and to the right-front (D), the left-rear (E), the right-rear
(F) and the left-front (G) brake system.
3. Remove the ABS modulator-control unit (H) with the bracket (I) from the
body.
