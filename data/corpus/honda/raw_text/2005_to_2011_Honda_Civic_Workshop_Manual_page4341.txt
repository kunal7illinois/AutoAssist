DTC 27-11: STEERING ANGLE SENSOR DIAG SIGNAL ERROR (INITIAL); DTC 27-26:
STEERING ANGLE SENSOR DIAG SIGNAL ERROR (MAIN)
1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
2. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
3. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), then turn it ON (II) again.
4. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC 27-11, or 27-26 indicated?
YES -Go to step 5.
NO -Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for loose
terminals between the steering angle sensor 5P connector and the VSA
modulator-control unit 37P connector. Refer to INTERMITTENT
FAILURES TROUBLESHOOTING (see ).
5. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
6. Disconnect the steering angle sensor 5P connector.
7. Disconnect the VSA modulator-control unit 37P connector.
