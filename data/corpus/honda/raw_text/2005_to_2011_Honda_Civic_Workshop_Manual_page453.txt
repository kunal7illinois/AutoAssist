SYSTEM DESCRIPTION
OVERVIEW
The audio unit acts as the processor for all audio functions. Select audio functions
from the audio unit, the audio remote,(on the steering wheel), or by using the
navigation voice control system. The audio display provides the current audio
status. For vehicles with navigation, additional audio information is available by
touching the audio button on the navigation audio screen. (See the owner's manual
and the navigation system manual for more details.)
The XM receiver passes its signal to the audio unit. In addition, it communicates
with the audio unit via the GA-Net bus. Any open connections in the GA-Net bus
circuit causes audio and navigation functions to appear inoperative.
