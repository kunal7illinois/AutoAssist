Demo Mode
This screen allows the navigation system to drive a route, when the vehicle is
stationary. Typical applications include auto shows, and other events. This feature
allows a visitor to enter a destination, and see the system drive to the destination.
No speed signal is needed.
To initiate the mode select ON.
(cid:1)
Changing the speed rate in ms (milliseconds) is optional, and represents the
(cid:1)
time between updates of the VP (vehicle position) movement.
When you increase the rate, the VP slows down because it is updated
(cid:2)
(moved) at a slower rate.
When you decrease the rate, VP is faster because it is updated (moved)
(cid:2)
more frequently.
1500 ms is VP at its slowest in demo mode.
(cid:2)
150 ms is VP at its fastest in demo mode (Default).
(cid:2)
At key off, the setting automatically returns to the default of Off.
