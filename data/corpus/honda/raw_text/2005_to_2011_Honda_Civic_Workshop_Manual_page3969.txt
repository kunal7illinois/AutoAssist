2000-05 INSIGHT
1. When distance driven since maintenance required indicator was reset is 6000
miles, the MAINT REQ'D reminder light will start to blink. After exceeding
7500 miles without having scheduled maintenance performed and MAINT
REQ'D reminder light reset, light will remain on until it is reset.
2. To reset the light, turn ignition switch to OFF position. Press and hold TRIP
button, located on lower right side of instrument cluster. While still holding
TRIP button, turn ignition switch to ON position with engine off. Hold button
for about 10 seconds until indicator resets.
