DEFOGGER WIRE REPAIR
NOTE: To make an effective repair, the broken section must be no
longer than 1 inch (2.5 mm).
1. Lightly rub the area around the broken section (A) with fine steel wool, then
clean it with isopropyl alcohol.
Fig. 4: Identifying Defogger Wire Broken Section
2. Carefully mask above and below the broken portion of the defogger wire (B)
with cellophane tape (C).
3. Using a small brush, apply a heavy coat of silver conductive paint
(commercially available) (A) extending about 1/8 " on both sides of the break.
Allow 25 minutes to dry.
