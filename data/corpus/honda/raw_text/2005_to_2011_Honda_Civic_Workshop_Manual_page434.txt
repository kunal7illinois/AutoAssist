2008 Honda Civic EX
2006-08 RESTRAINTS SRS (Supplemental Restraint System) - Civic (Except Hybrid)
1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery, then wait for 3 minutes before
starting work.
2. Remove the front passenger's seat assembly section (see FRONT SEAT
REMOVAL/INSTALLATION ).
3. Remove the cushion cover/pad from the seat cushion frame (see FRONT
SEAT CUSHION COVER REPLACEMENT ).
4. Using a TORX E18 socket, remove the TORX nuts (A) attaching the seat track
(B) to the front passenger's seat slide assembly including all four front
passenger's weight sensors (C).
Fig. 341: Identifying Front Passenger's Weight Sensors, Connectors, Seat
Track And Torx Nuts
Courtesy of AMERICAN HONDA MOTOR CO., INC.
5. Disconnect the sensor connectors (D) from the front passenger's weight sensors
(E), then remove the front passenger's weight sensors.
Installation
NOTE: Be sure to install the harness wires so they are not pinched
or interfering with other parts.
1. Install the new front passenger's seat slide assembly including all four front
passenger's weight sensors under the seat track.
