DTC P0496: EVAP SYSTEM HIGH PURGE FLOW DETECTED
NOTE: Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and any on-
board snapshot, and review the general troubleshooting
information (see GENERAL TROUBLESHOOTING
INFORMATION ).
1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
2. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
3. Do the EVAP FUNCTION TEST in the INSPECTION MENU with the HDS.
Is the result OK?
YES - Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor
connections or loose terminals at the FTP sensor, the EVAP canister purge
valve, the EVAP canister vent shut valve, and the ECM/PCM.
NO - Go to step 4.
4. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
5. Replace the EVAP canister purge valve (see EVAP CANISTER PURGE
VALVE REPLACEMENT ).
