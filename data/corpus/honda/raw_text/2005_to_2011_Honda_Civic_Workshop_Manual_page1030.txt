COOLANT REPLACEMENT ).
20. Start the engine in P or N, and warm it up to normal operating temperature (the
radiator fan comes on). Turn off the engine, and recheck the ATF level (see
ATF LEVEL CHECK ).
21. Enter the anti-theft code for the audio system or the navigation system (if
equipped), then enter the audio presets. Set the clock (on vehicles without
navigation).
