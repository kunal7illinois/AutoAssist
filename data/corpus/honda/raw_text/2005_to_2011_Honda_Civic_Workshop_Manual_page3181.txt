INTERIOR LIGHT SWITCH TEST/REPLACEMENT
WITH MOONROOF
NOTE: The interior light switch is built into the moonroof switch,
and it switches the front individual map lights between the
OFF and DOOR positions.
1. Remove the front individual map lights (see FRONT INDIVIDUAL MAP
LIGHT TEST/REPLACEMENT ).
2. Disconnect the moonroof switch 10P connector (A) and map light 3P
connector (B).
Fig. 10: Identifying Moonroof Switch 10P Connector And Map Light 3P
Connector
Courtesy of AMERICAN HONDA MOTOR CO., INC.
3. Remove the moonroof switch (C).
4. At the moonroof switch 10P connector, check for continuity between the No. 1
and No. 8 terminals.
There should be continuity when the interior light switch is in the DOOR
(cid:1)
position.
There should be no continuity when the interior light switch is in the OFF
(cid:1)
position.
