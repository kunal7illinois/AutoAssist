GPS Send Time
This screen is for factory use only. It allows adjustment of the GPS time. This
display updates in realtime.
GPS Time is the time as received from the GPS satellites. It is in Greenwich
(cid:1)
Mean Time (GMT).
Date, Hour, Minute, and Set should not be used.
