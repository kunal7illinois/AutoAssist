NAVIGATION FUNCTION
The navigation system is composed of the navigation unit, the ECM/PCM (vehicle
speed signal), the GPS antenna, microphone, voice control switch, XM receiver,
and the climate control unit.
These units communicate with each other on the GA-Net bus.
