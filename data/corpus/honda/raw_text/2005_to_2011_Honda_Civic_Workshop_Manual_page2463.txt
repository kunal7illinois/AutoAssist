Is the fuel fill cap seal missing or damaged, is the fuel fill pipe damaged, or is
the tether cord caught under the cap?
YES - Replace the fuel fill cap or the fuel fill pipe, then go to step 19.
NO - Go to step 3.
3. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
4. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
5. Do the EVAP FUNCTION TEST in the INSPECTION MENU with the HDS.
Is the result OK?
YES - Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor
connections or loose terminals at the FTP sensor, or the EVAP canister vent
shut valve and the ECM/PCM.
NO - Go to step 6.
6. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
7. Remove the EVAP canister vent shut valve from the EVAP canister (see
EVAP CANISTER VENT SHUT VALVE REPLACEMENT ).
8. Connect the 2P connector to the EVAP canister vent shut valve.
9. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
