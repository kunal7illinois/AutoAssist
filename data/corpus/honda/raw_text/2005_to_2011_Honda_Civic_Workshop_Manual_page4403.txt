2008 Honda Civic GX
2006-08 BRAKES VSA System - Civic (All Except Hybrid)
Removal
1. Disconnect the VSA modulator-control unit 37P connector (A) by pushing the
lock (B) and pulling down the lever (C); the connector disconnects it self.
Fig. 70: Identifying VSA Modulator-Control Unit Components (With
Torque Specifications)
2. Disconnect the six brake lines from the VSA modulator-control unit.
NOTE: Brake lines are connected to the master cylinder (D)
and to the right-front (E), the left-rear (F), the right-rear
(G), and the left-front (H) brake systems.
3. Remove the VSA modulator-control unit (I) with the bracket (J) from the body.
4. Remove the VSA modulator-control unit from the bracket.
