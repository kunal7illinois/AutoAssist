D Position: Driving in 3rd gear
The PCM turns shift solenoid valve CON, and shift solenoid valve A and B remain
OFF. Shift solenoid valve C pressure (SC) is applied to the right side of shift valve
C and cut valve B. Shift valve C is then moved to the left side to releasing the 2nd
clutch pressure. The 3rd clutch keeps on engaging with the A/T clutch pressure
control solenoid valve C pressure mode.
