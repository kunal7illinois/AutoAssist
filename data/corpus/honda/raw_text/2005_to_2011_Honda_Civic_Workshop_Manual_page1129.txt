SECONDARY SHAFT DISASSEMBLY, INSPECTION, AND REASSEMBLY
1. Inspect the thrust needle bearing and the needle bearing for galling and rough
movement.
Fig. 417: Exploded View Of Secondary Shaft

2. Inspect the splines for excessive wear and damage.
3. Check the shaft bearing surface for scoring and excessive wear.
4. Before installing the O-rings, wrap the shaft splines with tape to prevent O-ring
damage.
