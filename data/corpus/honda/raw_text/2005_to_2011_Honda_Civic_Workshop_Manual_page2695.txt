BETWEEN THE ENGINE AND THE MANUAL SHUT-OFF VALVE
This procedure will allow you to safely work on any part of the fuel system
downstream of the manual shut-off valve, such as fuel pressure regulator P1 or the
fuel injectors.
1. Lift the vehicle, and support it with jackstands.
2. Close the manual shut-off valve (A).

3. Start the engine, and let it idle. After a few minutes, the engine will stall.
