Loosening cylinder head cover
– Loosen cylinder head cover bolts in the sequence 1 ... 6.
Tightening sequence for cylinder head cover
– Renew bolts.
1. Fit bolts in the sequence -1 ... 6- and hand-tighten in several
stages.
2. Tighten bolts in the sequence -1 ... 6- to 8 Nm using torque
wrench.
3. Turn 90° further in the sequence -1 ... 6- using a rigid
wrench.
Note
Take care to keep cylinder head cover straight.
