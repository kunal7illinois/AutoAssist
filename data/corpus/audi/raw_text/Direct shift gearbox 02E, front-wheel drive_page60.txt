6 Transporting the direct shift gearbox
Special tools and workshop equipment required
♦ Workshop hoist -VAS 6100-
♦ Retainer -T10013-
Procedure
Use retainer -T10013- to transport the direct shift gearbox and
when setting up gearbox support -3282- .
– Remove threaded pin from retainer -T10013- (relea s e retain‐
ing ring).
– Secure retainer -T10013- to gearbox lifting eye with bolt
(M8x40) -1- and washer (outside diameter 30 mm).
– Hook workshop hoist -VAS 6100- onto retainer -T10013- .
Caution
The gearbox oil cooler can be damaged when the gearbox is
transported.
♦ Guide gearbox as required when transporting.
