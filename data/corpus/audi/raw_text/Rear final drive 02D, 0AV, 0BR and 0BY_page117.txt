Removing
Renewing the oil seal (right-side) is the same procedure as re‐
newing the oil seal (left-side).
– Remove drive shaft ⇒ Rep. Gr. 42 ; Servicing rear drive
shafts .
– Place drip tray under the rear final drive.
Removing flange shaft (left-side)
– Screw a nut M8 -arrow- onto thread of puller -T10037- until it
is flush with end of thread.
– Remove flange shaft using puller -T10037 - .
Removing flange shaft (right-side)
– Screw a nut M8 -arrow- onto thread of puller -T 1 0037- until it
is flush with end of thread.
– Remove flange shaft using puller -T10037 - .
Both sides (continued)
– Lever out flange shaft oil seal using oil seal extractor lever -
VW 681- .
Installing
– Lightly lubricate outside circumference of new oil seal and
drive in onto stop using thrust piece -T10049- . Take care to
keep seal straight when installing.
– Pack space between sealing lip and dust lip half full with seal‐
ing grease -G 052 128 A1- .
