2.24.2 4-Cylinder, Gasoline Engine, 1.8 TFSI,
2.0 TFSI
– Remove the noise insulation. Refer to
⇒ “2.16 Engine Compartment Front Noise Insulation Cover,
Removing and Installing“, page 28 .
– Loosen oil filter with Hazet Tension Band -2171-1- or Oil Filter
Wrench -3417- and remove.
Note
Follow all waste disposal regulations!
– Open oil drain plug or extract engine oil.
– Clean oil filter sealing surface on engine.
– Lubricate rubber seal lightly with oil.
– Install new filter and tighten.
– Install oil drain plug with new gasket.
– Fill with engine oil, specifications, refer to ⇒ Fluid Capacity
Tables; Rep. Gr. 03 .
Tightening Specifications Nm
Oil filter 22
Oil drain plug 30
