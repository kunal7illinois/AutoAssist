Installing
• Clutch pedal in “released” position.
– Install retaining clip -2- on operating rod -1- for clutch master
cylinder.
– Insert a spacer -A- between clutch pedal and stop, and press
clutch pedal forward.
♦ Length of spacer = approx. 40 mm (e.g. 1/2” socket)
– Lock clutch master cylinder on mounting bracket
-arrows 1 and 2-.
– Press operating rod -1- for clutch master cylinder in direction
of -arrow- so that retaining clip -2- snaps into place in clutch
pedal.
– Install mounting bracket ⇒ page 18 .
