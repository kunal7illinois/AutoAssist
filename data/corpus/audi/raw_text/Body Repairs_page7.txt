1 General notes
This Manual describes only selected repair operations. A de‐
scription is given where the repair method differs from the original
manufacturing process.
Where the repair operation is not described: Separate the original
joint and repair using a replacement part.
If no description is given for any sub-parts supplied, these should
be matched up and butt welded with an SG continuous seam.
Please refer to the information in the Workshop Manual ⇒ General
Information; Body Repairs, General Body Repairs .
