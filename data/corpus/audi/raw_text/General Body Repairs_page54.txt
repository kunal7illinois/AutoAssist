18 Safety notes
Always use an extractor when performing welding and grinding
operations.
Welding and grinding must never be performed simultaneously in
the same working area.
Working area must be cleaned at regular intervals as necessary
to remove dust.
Compressed air must NOT be used to blow out dust deposits.
The extraction system must be cleaned at regular intervals.
In addition, the relevant accident prevention and trade association
regulations must be observed.
