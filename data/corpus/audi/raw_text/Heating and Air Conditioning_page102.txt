7.8 Removing and installing dust and pollen
filter
Note
♦ Replacement interval for dust and pollen filter ⇒ Maintenance
tables
♦ There are different versions of the dust and pollen filter without
and with activated charcoal element ⇒ Electronic parts cata‐
logue . The Audi TT with air conditioner is currently fitted with
a dust and pollen filter with activated charcoal element. Vehi‐
cles with no air conditioner (heater only) feature a dust and
pollen filter with no activated charcoal element.
♦ Clean the area around the dust and pollen filter in the slot for
the air conditioner unit (heater) before fitting a new filter.
♦ It may be necessary to remove the driving instructor's pedals
on driving school vehicles (depending on version, the driving
instructor's pedals may be provided with service disconnection
points) ⇒ Heed fitting instructions for driving school equip‐
ment .
