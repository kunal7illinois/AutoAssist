9.2 Removing and installing knee restraint
(dash panel trim, driver side)
Removing
– Remove light switch ⇒ Rep. gr. 96 .
– Remove dash panel end trim ⇒ page 146 .
– Unscrew bolts -1- (2x) and -2- (5x).
– Use a small screwdriver to detach top edge of knee restraint
-3- from dash panel trim -arrow-.
– Remove knee restraint.
Installing
Install in reverse order of removal.
Tightening torque ⇒ page 102
