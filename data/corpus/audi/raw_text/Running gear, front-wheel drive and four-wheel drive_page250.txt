4.3 Explanatory notes on weight codes
used in production (PR numbers)
The PR number on the vehicle data sticker indicates the type of
running gear (for front axle and rear axle) fitted in the vehicle.
The vehicle data sticker can be found in the spare wheel well and
in the Service Schedule.
