1.7 Removing and installing trim (bottom)
for steering column switch
Removing
– Remove upper trim for steering column switch ⇒ page 7 .
– Unscrew bolts -5- and remove handle -4- for adjusting steering
column.
– Starting from straight ahead position, turn steering wheel 90°
and remove bolts -1- and -2-.
Note
If bolts -1- and -2- are not accessible, steering wheel must be
removed before performing remaining work steps ⇒ Rep. gr. 48 .
– Remove bolt -6- and detach lower trim -3- for steering column
switch.
Installing
Install in reverse order of removal.
