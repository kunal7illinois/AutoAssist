5.9 Checking operation of automatic anti-
dazzle interior mirror
The test can only be carried out if the following conditions are met:
• Interior mirror is fitted.
• Ignition is switched on.
• Reverse gear is not engaged.
• Automatic anti-dazzle function is switched on, indicator lamp
must be lit.
