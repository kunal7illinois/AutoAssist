Assembly mounting on gearbox side: tightening sequence
– Fit gearbox support -A- between gearbox and support arm of
gearbox mounting.
– Bolt gearbox support to gearbox.
– Pull gearbox up to support arm of gearbox mounting using
spindle of support bracket -10 - 222 A- .
– Screw in bolts -2- initially hand-tight.
Caution
The threads in the gearbox support can be damaged if the bolts
are not inserted straight.
♦ Before fitting the bolts -2-, the gearbox support and the
support arm of the gearbox mounting must be aligned ab‐
solutely parallel. If necessary, push the gearbox up at the
rear with a trolley jack.
– Only tighten bolts after subframe has been installed ⇒ Rep.
Gr. 40 and assembly mountings aligned ⇒ Rep. Gr. 10 .
