37 Fitting solid rivets
– Match up new parts, apply adhesive, position new part on body
and secure in place.
– Use riveting tool to punch flanges.
Piercing and stamping are performed in one operation.
Note
Set riveting tool to material thickness for all operations. Insert
suitable clamping pins in punched holes to stop flanges coming
apart after punching.
– Insert solid rivet and use riveting tool to set closing head.
Appropriate inserts for the riveting tool are available for the vari‐
ous rivet diameters.
Note
In contrast to series production, all riveted joints also have to be
bonded when performing repairs in the workshop. Refer to section
on Bonded joint. Solid rivets are made of aluminium.
2 types of solid rivets are available:
Solid rivet ∅ 4 x 8 for joining 2 panels
Solid rivet ∅ 4 x 12 for joining 3 panels
