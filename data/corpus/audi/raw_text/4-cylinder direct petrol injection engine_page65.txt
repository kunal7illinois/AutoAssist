Removing
– Remove sump with oil pump ⇒ page 120 .
– Remove front sealing flange ⇒ page 44 .
– Pull chain sprocket off crankshaft with puller -2- (Kukko 44-1
or similar). Use special tool 40-105 to protect end of crank‐
shaft.
Installing
Installation is carried out in the reverse order; note the following:
WARNING
Wear protective gloves.
– Heat chain sprocket in oven for approx. 15 minutes to 220°C.
Note
Installation position: wide collar on sprocket facing towards en‐
gine,
– Fit chain sprocket on end of crankshaft using pliers, and press
onto crankshaft as far as the stop using drift sleeve -30 - 100- .
– Install sump with oil pump ⇒ page 120 .
