B - Bolt without washer
C - Bolt with captive washer

78.6 mm) must also be fitted after installing the gearbox hous‐
ing -2- ⇒ page 167 .
– Install circlip -1- for input shaft grooved ball bearing.
– Remove support bridge -30 - 211 A- for input shaft.
