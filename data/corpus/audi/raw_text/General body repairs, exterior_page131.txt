Note
♦ The convertible roof clamping device -VAS 6586- can be se‐
cured to the scissor-type assembly platform -VAS 6131 A- or
on a workbench via the holes in the cross bar.
♦ For the convertible roof to be in the correct installation position,
it must be secured to the mounting plates via the correspond‐
ing holes in the main mountings and the threaded holes “A, B,
C, D and E” in the mounting plates ⇒ page 126 .
♦ Before you do so, loosely secure the mounting plates to the
main mountings with bolts.
♦ Insert the convertible roof together with the mounting plates in
th e convertible roof clamping device -VAS 6586- and tighten
th e bolts at the main mountings.
♦ To make sure that the hydraulic pump functions correctly, put
it into the half shells on the cross bar together with the reservoir
and the hydraulic pump motor.
