7.6 Removing and installing left footwell
vent temperature sender -G261-
Note
Vehicles with no air conditioner (heater only) are not fitted with a
left footwell vent temperature sender -G261- (depending on the
version, there is no mounting hole in the air duct or this is sealed
with a plug).
– Remove driver's storage compartment. ⇒ General body re‐
pairs, interior; Rep. Gr. 68
– Remove left footwell vent -A- (driver's side). ⇒ General body
repairs, interior; Rep. Gr. 68
– Reach through the opening between the air conditioner unit
on the left and the central tube (dash panel cross-member) or
the support at the air conditioner unit on the left and turn the
sender -B- to a position in which the connector -A- can be un‐
plugged.
– Unplug the connector -A- (e.g. using pointed nose pliers).
– Turn sender -B- through 90° and pull it out of air conditioner
unit.
– On installation, pay attention to correct positioning of seal
-C-.
Note
♦ If it is not possible to turn the sender -B- or if the sender -B- is
inaccessible on account of other components, these are to be
removed ⇒ General body repairs, interior; Rep. Gr. 70 .
♦ Illustration shows sender -B- with dash panel removed. With
the dash panel in position, the sender -B- can be seen from
underneath with the footwell vent removed (the part of the
sender with the temperature sensor can be seen in the air
duct).
