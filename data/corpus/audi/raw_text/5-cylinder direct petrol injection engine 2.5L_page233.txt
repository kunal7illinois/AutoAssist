1.5 Checking exhaust system for leaks
Procedure
– Start the engine and run at idling speed.
– Plug tailpipes during leak test (e.g. with cloth or plugs).
– Listen for noise at connections between cylinder head/exhaust
manifold with turbocharger, exhaust manifold with turbocharg‐
er/starter catalytic converter etc. to locate any leaks.
– Rectify any leaks that are found.
