Installing adapter for mirror base
– The corresponding adapter -2- is supplied loose together with
the new mounting plate -1-; the adapter must first be fitted onto
the mirror base -3-.
– With the mounting plate angled forwards, press it onto the
adapter and then pivot it rearwards into its normal position.
– The adapter will then engage in the mounting plate.
Removing mirror housing
– Unscrew bolts -arrows- from underside of housing and from
mounting plate
– Take mounting plate out of housing.
