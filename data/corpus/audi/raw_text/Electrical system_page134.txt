11.5 Removing and installing steering angle
sender -G85-
Removing
– Remove steering column trim ⇒ Rep. gr. 68 .
– Remove return ring with slip ring ⇒ page 125 .
– Detach steering angle sender -G85- -arrow-.
Installing
Installation is carried out in the reverse order; note the following:
– Perform calibration of steering angle sender -G85- in „Guided
Fault Finding“ or „Guided Functions“ mode after renewing
steering angle sender ⇒ Vehicle diagnostic, testing and infor‐
mation system VAS 5051.
