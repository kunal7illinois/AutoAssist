3.3 Checking exhaust gas recirculation
cooler change-over
Special tools and workshop equipment required
♦ Hand vacuum pump -VAS 6213-
Procedure
– Detach heat shield sleeve.
– Disconnect vacuum hose from vacuum unit and connect hand
vacuum pump -VAS 6213- in its place.
– Operate hand vacuum pump to produce a vacuum.
• The linkage of the exhaust gas recirculation cooler change-
over should move -arrows-.
If the linkage does not move or only moves jerkily:
♦ The vacuum unit is defective.
♦ The change-over flap for exhaust gas recirculation is sticking.
Note
Secure the heat insulation sleeve in the original position when
installing.
