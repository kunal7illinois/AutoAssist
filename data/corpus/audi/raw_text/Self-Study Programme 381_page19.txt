Sensor unit G419
The sensor unit comprises senders G200 (lateral
acceleration sender), G202 (yaw rate sender) and, on
models with quattro four-wheel drive, G251 (longitu-
dinal acceleration sender) . The sensor unit has
been adopted from the Audi A3. The service proce-
dures for calibrating the sensors are the same as for
the Audi A3.
