3.9 Renewing front turn signal bulb -M5- / -
M7-
Removing
– Remove headlight ⇒ page 84 .
– Detach cover -1- for turn signal from headlight housing
-arrow A-.
– Turn bulb holder -2- anti-clockwise -arrow B- and pull it out of
housing.
– Screw bulb out of bulb holder.
Installing
Installation is carried out in the reverse order; note the following:
– Insert new bulb in bulb holder, taking care not to touch glass
part of bulb with bare hands.
– Close housing cover.
– Install headlight ⇒ page 84 .
