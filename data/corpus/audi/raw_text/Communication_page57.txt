4.11 Removing and installing aerial filter -
R87- (USA vehicles only)
The aerial filter -R87- is installed on vehicles up to 10/06.
The aerial filter -R87- is located in the insert in the luggage com‐
partment (left-side) below the satellite radio -R146- .
– Switch off all electrical equipment.
– Remove ignition key.
Removing
– Open luggage compartment.
– Take out luggage compartment floor.
– Remove satellite radio -R146- from insert in luggage compart‐
ment and place it to one side.
– Pry aerial filter -R87- -1- out of luggage compartment insert.
– Unplug electrical connectors -2- and -3- at aerial filter -R87-
-1-.
Installing
– Installation is performed in reverse sequence.
