18 Removing and installing rear final
drive (Audi TT RS)
Special tools and workshop equipment required
♦ Engine and gearbox jack -V.A.G 1383 A-
Removing
– Remove spare wheel (if fitted).
– Remove spare wheel well or foam inserts.
– Remove rubber plugs or tape -arrows A- in luggage compart‐
ment floor.
– Working through these apertures, unscrew bolts -arrow B-.
– Disconnect exhaust system at clamps -1- and -2-.
Caution
The flexible joint in the starter catalytic converter can be dam‐
aged.
♦ The flexible joint must not be bent further than 10°.
– Tie up catalytic converters on underbody.
– Remove rear section of exhaust system ⇒ Rep. Gr. 26 .
– Remove rear flexible coupling ⇒ page 48 .
– Tie propshaft up to underbody on the side.
TT Roadster:
– Remove bolts -1- and -3- and detach diagonal struts -2-.
