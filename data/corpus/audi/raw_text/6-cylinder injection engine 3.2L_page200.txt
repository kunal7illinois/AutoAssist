3.8 Removing and installing secondary air
pump
Removing
– Disconnect air hose -arrow-.
– Move hose going to secondary air pump clear.
– Release fasteners -1 ... 4- and remove centre noise insulation.
– Detach electrical connector -3- at secondary air pump motor -
V101- and move wire clear.
– On vehicles with direct shift gearbox: Remove bolt -2- securing
bracket for coolant pipe going to gearbox oil cooler.
– Remove bolts -1-.
– Loosen bolt -4- and remove secondary air pump with bracket.
Installing
• Tightening torque ⇒ page 184 .
Install in reverse order.
