41 Joddling
– Joddle using a suitable joddling tool.
Note
In the case of separating cuts allow 22 mm for overlap. Place
additional thickness of same material behind joint if joddling is not
possible.
Provide upper panel with 45° bevel and file down edges to permit
subsequent filling of separating cut.
A special metal filler is available for this purpose. Refer to ETKA.
Note
The filler is applied to the bright aluminium. The filler hardens at
120° C.
