1.2 Removing and installing air pipes and
hoses with plug-in connectors
Removing
Proceed as follows:
– Release tab -A- on plug-in connector by pulling retaining clip
-arrow-.
– Disconnect air pipes and hoses by hand (do not use a tool).
Installing
Note
♦ Renew seal if damaged.
♦ Hose connections and air pipes and hoses must be free of oil
and grease before assembly.
♦ Make sure that the seal is correctly seated in the groove on
the complete circumference of the air pipe/hose.
♦ Apply a thin coating of silicon-free lubricant to the sealing area
and the seal.
– Release retaining clip (position -1-).
– Push air pipe/hose in as far as stop and press retaining clip to
lock -2-.
– Press air pipe/hose again and pull again to check that plug-in
connector is correctly engaged.
