Installing
Installation is carried out in reverse sequence; note the following:
– Secure gear selector cable and gate selector cable to selector
housing with securing clips -1 ... 4-.
Vehicles with vehicle ID No. from 8J-7-013001 onwards:
– Press gear selector cable and gate selector cable onto pins.
Continued for all vehicles:
– Fit new gasket and secure new floor plate to selector mecha‐
nism by bending over tabs -arrows-.
– Install selector mechanism ⇒ page 64 .
– Adjust selector mechanism ⇒ page 79 .
