2.3 Removing and installing cylinder head
cover
Removing
Proceed as follows:
– Remove engine cover panel -arrows-.
– Unscrew bolts -arrows- and press toothed belt cover (top)
slightly to one side.
– Remove ignition coils ⇒ Rep. Gr. 28 .
– Remove bolt -arrow-.
– Loosen hose clips -1- and -3- and move air pipe clear to one
side (hose -2- remains connected).
– Disconnect vacuum hose -1- from cylinder head cover.
– Remove bolts -arrows- and detach pressure control valve for
crankcase breather system.
