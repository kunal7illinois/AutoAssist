Cylinder head machining limit
– Machining of the cylinder head (surface grinding) is only per‐
missible down to the minimum dimension a.
♦ Minimum dimension a = 139.20 mm
Tightening sequence for retaining frame
– Proceed as follows:
1. Fit bolts in the sequence -1 ... 6- and hand-tighten in several
stages.
2. Tighten bolts in the sequence -1 ... 6- to 8 Nm using torque
wrench.
3. Turn 90° (1/4 turn) further in the sequence -1 ... 6- using a
rigid wrench.
Note
Take care to keep retaining frame straight.
