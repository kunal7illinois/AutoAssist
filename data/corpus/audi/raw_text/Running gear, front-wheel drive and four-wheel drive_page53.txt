– „Running gear (Rep. Gr. 01; 40 - 49)“
– „14 - Wheel damping electronics“

– 14 - Code control unit (Rep. Gr. 40)
or
– 14 - Replace control unit (Rep. Gr. 40)
or
– 14 - Re-adapt reference position (default position) (Rep. Gr.
40)
