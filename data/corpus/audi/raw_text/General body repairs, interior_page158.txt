2.3 Removing and installing dash panel end
trim
Removing
Note
Removal and installation are identical on the left and right.
– Use a screwdriver to prise off dash panel end trim -arrow-.
Installing
Installation is carried out in the reverse order; note the following:
– Press in the dash panel end trim until the catches fully engage.
