Preparations

– Turn light switch to „dipped beam“ position – not to „Auto“ po‐
sition.
– Versions equipped with halogen headlights: Set knurled wheel
for headlight range control to position „0“.
– Versions equipped with gas discharge headlights: For prepar‐
ing headlight adjustment, select „Basic setting“ function in
„Guided Functions“ mode ⇒ Vehicle diagnostic, testing and
information system VAS 5051.
