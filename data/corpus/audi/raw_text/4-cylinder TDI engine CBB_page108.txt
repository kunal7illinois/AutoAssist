Cylinder head - tightening torque and sequence
– Renew cylinder head bolts.
– Tighten bolts in the sequence -1 ... 10- in 4 stages as follows:
1. Tighten to 30 Nm.
2. Tighten to 50 Nm.
3. Turn 90° further.
4. Turn 90° further.
