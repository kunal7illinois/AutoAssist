Removing
– With ignition switched off, disconnect earth cable at battery
⇒ page 4 .
– Remove air cleaner housing ⇒ Rep. gr. 24 .
– Unplug connector -4- by sliding locking element to rear and
pressing down release catch.
– Fold back protective sleeve -3- and detach B+ wire -item 1-

– Remove centre hex stud -2-.
– Remove nut -6- and detach bracket -5- for earth cable.
– Remove nut -1- and move clear earth cable -3-.
– Remove centre hex stud -2- and lift out starter -4- to front.
