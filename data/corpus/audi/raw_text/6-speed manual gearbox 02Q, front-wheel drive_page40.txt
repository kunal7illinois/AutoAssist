5.1 Removing and installing clutch slave
cylinder together with release bearing
Note
Slave cylinder and release bearing are one unit and can only be
renewed together.
Removing
• Gearbox removed ⇒ page 82 .
– Release retaining clip -2- with a screwdriver and pull bleeder
connection -4- off slave cylinder -1-.
– Remove bolts -arrows-.
– Take off slave cylinder together with release bearing -A-.
Installing
• Tightening torque ⇒ page 35
Installation is carried out in reverse sequence; note the following:
Caution
Tighten securing bolts for slave cylinder in small steps. Other‐
wise mounting lugs -arrows- can be damaged.
– Secure clutch slave cylinder with release bearing -arrows-.
– Check O-ring -3- for damage and renew if necessary.
– Press bleeder connection -4- onto connection on clutch slave
cylinder -1- so that retaining clip -2- snaps into place.
– Pull on bleeder connection to check it is secure.
– Bleed clutch system ⇒ page 31 .
