Tyre pressure monitoring system (US spec)
Overview
The Audi TT for the North American market uses an
improved version of the tyre pressure monitoring
system as featured previously in the Audi A6 (USA)
.It is a direct measuring system with tyre pressure
sensors in the wheels.
