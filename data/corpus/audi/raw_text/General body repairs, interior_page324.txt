3.26 Removing and installing trim with Easy
Entry handle (Coupé only) up to 10.2006
Note
♦ The trim and handle can be removed and installed with the
front seat in position.
♦ If the trim and handle are removed, both components will be
damaged irreparably.
Removing
– Destroy trim -1- and attachment piece -3- of handle -2-.
– Take care not to damage cover.
– Make sure no plastic remnants have dropped into the cover or
between the padding and the retainer for the Easy Entry fa‐
cility.
– Detach destroyed trim -1- with Easy Entry handle -2- from front
seat.
