42 Placing additional thickness of same
material behind joint
The additional thickness of same material -C- is made from rem‐
nants of new part -B- or old part -A-. Also place additional thick‐
ness of same material behind joint at beaded panel edges. The
material is cut for small cross-sections or large panel edges.
– Bevel both panels to an angle of 45°. Round off outer edge
(radius = R3) and chamfer inner edge.
There must be a gap of 3 to 4 mm between panel tips.
