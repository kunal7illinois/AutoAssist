Caution
Make sure lubrication system is not clogged by excess sealant.
♦ The bead of sealant must not be thicker than specified.
– Apply beads of sealant -1, 2, 3- onto clean sealing surface of
sump (top section) as shown in illustration.
• Thickness of sealant bead: 1.5 ... 2.0 mm
Note
The sump (top section) must be installed within 5 minutes after
applying the sealant.
– Fit sump (top section) and screw in bolts -13, 15, 16, 17- by
hand until they make contact.
– Wipe off surplus sealant at the positions marked with
-arrows-.
– Loosen bolts slightly.
– Secure plates -2036/1- to 20 Nm at cylinder block as shown
in illustration.
– Press sump (top section) -A- firmly onto plates.
– Tighten bolts for sump (top section) ⇒ page 136 .
• The sump (top section) must remain in contact with plates.
Remaining installation steps are carried out in reverse sequence;
note the following:
– Install sump (bottom section) ⇒ page 137 .
