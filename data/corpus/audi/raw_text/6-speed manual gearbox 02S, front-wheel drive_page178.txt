Installing
– Lightly oil outer circumference of new oil seal.
– Pack space between sealing lip and dust lip -arrow- half full
with sealing grease -G 052 128 A1- .
– Drive in new seal onto stop (take care to keep seal straight).
– Install flange shaft.
– Press flange shaft in against spring on gearbox housing and
secure with countersunk bolt. Tightening torque
⇒ Item 12 (page 180) .
– Bolt drive shaft to flange shaft ⇒ Rep. Gr. 40 .
– Check oil level in gearbox and top up with gear oil if necessary
⇒ page 95 .
– Install wheel housing liner (front left) -arrows-.
– Install noise insulation (centre) ⇒ Rep. Gr. 66 .
– Fit wheel.
