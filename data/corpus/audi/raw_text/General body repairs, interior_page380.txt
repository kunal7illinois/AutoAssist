4.6 Removing and installing backrest ad‐
juster knob
Removing
– Push side trim -2- slightly towards backrest.
– Use a screwdriver to lift retaining catch -1- up slightly and dis‐
engage.
– Turn backrest adjuster knob -3- and disengage another re‐
taining catch.
– Remove backrest adjuster knob.
Installing
Installation is carried out in the reverse order; note the following:
– Push on adjuster knob until it engages audibly.
Note
If backrest adjuster knob has come loose on adjusting shaft,
backrest adjuster knob must be renewed.
4.7 Removing and installing seat height ad‐
juster
Removing
– Pull seat height adjuster upwards and hold it.
– Use a narrow-bladed screwdriver to press retaining clip -1- in‐
wards -arrow A- and pull seat height adjuster -2- off lever
-arrow B-.
