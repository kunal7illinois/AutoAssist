3.2 Removing and installing steering col‐
umn
Removing
The replacement steering column is only supplied as a complete
unit. Repair is not possible.
The steering lock housing can be transferred from the old unit ⇒
Rep. gr. 94 .
WARNING
Observe the following points before working on the electrical
system and removing the steering wheel:
♦ Unscrew earth strap from battery ⇒ Rep. gr. 27 .
♦ Make sure wheels are in straight-ahead position.
Failure to comply with these precautions may lead to subse‐
quent failure of the airbag system.
– Turn wheels to straight ahead position.
– Pull down lever beneath steering column.
– Push steering column down as far as possible and pull out.
– Remove airbag unit ⇒ Rep. gr. 69 .
– Remove steering wheel ⇒ page 253 .
– Remove trim for steering column switch ⇒ Rep. gr. 68 .
– Remove storage compartment on driver's side. ⇒ Rep. gr. 68
– If fitted, remove knee airbag (driver's side) ⇒ Rep. gr. 69 .
– Remove steering column switch ⇒ Rep. gr. 94 .
