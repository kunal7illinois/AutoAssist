– Detach securing clip -1- for gear selector cable and pull cable
off pin.

– Detach securing clip -2- for gate selector cable from gate relay
lever and pull cable off pin.

• To prevent damage to gate selector cable, cable end-piece
must be detached from gate selector cable before removing
relay lever.
– Pull locking mechanism forwards in direction of -arrow 1- onto
stop and then turn to left in direction of -arrow 2- to lock.
– Then press gate relay lever forwards (in direction of
-arrow 3-).
• Detach cable end-piece only when gate relay lever is removed
