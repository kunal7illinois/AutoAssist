38 Fitting punch rivets
– Match up new parts, apply adhesive, position new part on body
and secure in place.
– When using punch rivets it is not necessary to drill or punch
holes.
– Position Rechargeable riveter -VAS 5279 A- and fit rivet.
2 types of punch rivets are available:
4D0 803217 N
4D0 803217 Q
Note
In contrast to series production, all riveted joints also have to be
bonded when performing repairs in the workshop. Refer to section
on Bonded joint. Punch rivets are made of aluminium.
