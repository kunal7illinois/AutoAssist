Loosening bolt securing drive shaft to wheel hub
– To avoid damage to wheel bearing, do not slacken off bolt se‐
curing drive shaft to wheel hub further than 90° with vehicle
still standing on its wheels.
– Raise vehicle so that wheels are off the ground.
– Have a second mechanic press the brake pedal.
– Remove bolt -arrow-.

– Renew bolt -arrow-.
