10.4 Description of repair work
• Repairs must be performed in an area protected from direct
sunlight.
• The repair area must be approx. room temperature.
• The work area must be protected from moisture.
– Loosen point of impact with hard metal scriber but do not in‐
crease size or remove pieces of broken glass.
– Remove any moisture which may have ingressed using the
suction cup, assisted by a hot-air blower from vehicle interior.
Terminate repair work if moisture cannot be removed.
– Fit mirror -1- from inside and align in such a way that you have
a good view of the damaged area.
