2.4 Removing and installing wiper arms
Special tools and workshop equipment required
♦ Tool kit for wiper arms -T10369-
Removing
Note
If the wiper motor needs to be run during repair work, the bonnet
must be closed (voltage supply to wiper motor is cut off when
bonnet is open).
– Switch on ignition.
– Actuate touch wipe function and allow wiper arms to move to
end position.
– Switch off ignition and take out ignition key.
– Pry cover caps off wiper arms with a screwdriver.
– Loosen nuts -arrows- one turn.
