Proceed as follows:
• Engine cold.

-G62- .
Note

♦ Disregard item -2-.
– Pull off retaining clip and detach coolant temperature sender
-G62- .
Installing
Installation is carried out in the reverse order; note the following:
Note
♦ Fit new O-ring.
♦ Insert new coolant temperature sender -G62- immediately into
connection to avoid loss of coolant.
