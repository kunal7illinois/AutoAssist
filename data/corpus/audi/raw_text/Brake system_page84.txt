6.2 Removing and installing rear brake pads
Note
Mark brake pads when removing them if they are to be reinstalled.
Reinstall in their original position to prevent uneven braking.
Special tools and workshop equipment required
♦ Torque wrench -V.A.G 1331-
Removing
– Raise vehicle.
– Remove wheels.
Do not detach handbrake cable from brake caliper.
– Detach brake caliper housing (counterhold at guide pin).
– Remove brake caliper housing from brake disc.
– Secure brake caliper housing with wire such that the weight of
the caliper does not damage the brake hose.
WARNING
Do not kink or bend brake lines and brake hose.
