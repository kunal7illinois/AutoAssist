5.13 Removing and installing trim (left-side/
right-side)
Special tools and workshop equipment required
♦ Removal wedge -3409-
Note
Removal and installation of trim (left-side) are described for 1/2
section of backrest (left-side). The removal and installation pro‐
cedures are similar for th e trim (right-side).
Removing
– Starting at the bottom rear right, unclip trim -1- using removal
wedge -3409- .
– Press release mechanism button -2- as far forwards as it will
go in the direction of travel and hold it in this position.
– Prise trim -1- off release mechanism using removal wedge
-3409- .
– Detach trim -1- towards rear.
