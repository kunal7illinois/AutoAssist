Installation is carried out in reverse sequence; note the following:
– Secure gearbox mounting -A-.
Caution
Screw in bolts straight when fitting as otherwise the threads in
the support arm of the gearbox mounting will be damaged.
♦ Before fitting the bolts -arrows-, the gearbox support and
the support arm of the gearbox mounting must be aligned
absolutely parallel. If necessary, push the gearbox up at
the rear with a trolley jack.
– Pull gearbox up with spindle on support bracket until gearbox
support contacts support arm of gearbox mounting.
– Adjust assembly mountings ⇒ Rep. Gr. 10 .
– Secure support arm of gearbox mounting to gearbox support.
– Detach support bracket -10 - 222 A- from engine.
