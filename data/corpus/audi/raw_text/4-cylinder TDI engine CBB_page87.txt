4.2 Measuring piston projection at “TDC”
Note
♦ Piston projection at “TDC” must be measured when installing
new pistons or a short engine.
♦ If the measured values for piston projection are not the same
for all pistons, use the highest value to determine the correct
gasket size.
♦ Depending upon piston projection, install the corresponding
cylinder head gasket according to the table below.
