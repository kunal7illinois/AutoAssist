Removing spindle cover
– Move seat to service position ⇒ page 274 .
– Use removal wedge -3409- to prise spindle cover -1- off seat
rail cover -3- and seat longitudinal adjustment spindle -2-.
Installing spindle cover
Install in reverse order of removal. Observe the following:
– Make sure the spindle cover is properly engaged.
