Caution
Electronic components of selector mechanism can be dam‐
aged by electrostatic discharge.
♦ Do not touch printed circuit -B- with bare hands.
– Touch vehicle earth with your hand (without gloves) to dis‐
charge any static electricity.
– Remove bolts -arrows-.
– Unclip retaining tabs on both sides and detach detent plate
-A- from shift unit.
– Shift selector lever to position “S”.
– Pull down locking plate -1- for selector lever cable and remove.
– Pull plastic retaining spring -2- forwards slightly and press piv‐
ot pin -3- upwards only as far as necessary -arrow-.
Note
Ensure that pin is not pressed out completely.
– Pull selector lever cable -4- out of shift unit.
Note
Do not bend or kink the selector lever cable.
