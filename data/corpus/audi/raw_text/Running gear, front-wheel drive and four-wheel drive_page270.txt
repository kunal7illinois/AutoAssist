4.1 Differences between steering boxes
(generations 2 and 3)
A generation 2 steering box was fitted in the Audi TT 2007 ► at
the start of production. This was superseded in Model Year 2008
by a generation 3 steering box; for correct version refer to ⇒
Electronic parts catalogue „ETKA“ .
Vehicles fitted with a generation 2 steering box can be converted
to generation 3.
When replacing a generation 2 steering box with a generation 3
steering box, the electrical wiring ⇒ page 266 must also be re‐
placed.
When fitted, the type of steering box can be identified from the
number of securing bolts.
