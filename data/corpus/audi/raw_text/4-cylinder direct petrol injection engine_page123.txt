Installing
• Tightening torques ⇒ page 98
– Do not lubricate sealing lip of oil seal.
– Fit guide sleeve -T10071/1- from assembly tool -T10071- onto
camshaft journal.
– Push oil seal over guide sleeve onto shaft.
– Detach guide sleeve.
– Press in oil seal all the way using guide sleeve -T10071/3- and
bolt -T10071/4- from assembly tool -T10071- .
– Install camshaft sprocket.
♦ Check installation position: The thinner web of the camshaft
sprocket faces outwards -arrows- and the marking for No. 1
cylinder TDC is visible.
