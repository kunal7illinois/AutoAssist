4.1 Preparations
Gear oil for direct shift gearbox ⇒ Electronic parts catalogue
– Remove air cleaner housing ⇒ Rep. Gr. 24 .
– Remove noise insulation (centre) -1- ⇒ Rep. Gr. 66 .
Clean gearbox.
Caution
A pivot pin for the selector forks in the gearbox must be re‐
moved in order to drain the gear oil.
The selector shaft must be locked to prevent the position of the
selector forks from being changed, e.g. due to accidental op‐
eration of the selector mechanism.
Lock the selector shaft as follows:
– Press selector shaft down (direction of -arrow 1-).
– While pressing selector shaft down, move angled rod -A- up‐
wards in direction of -arrow 2-. At the same time press angled
rod carefully into selector shaft until it engages.
4.2 Draining off gear oil
Use a clean container (capacity 3.0 ltr.) with a graduated scale to
collect gear oil.
