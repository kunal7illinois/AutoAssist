Removing
Note
The cover -2- and the dished spring -5- are removed together
when removing the control unit -1-. The pressure sensor -4- and
the valve -3- are removed separately.
– Switch off ignition.
– Unplug connectors -1- and -2- at top of control unit.
– Position drip tray under final drive.
– Remove bolts -arrows-.
Make sure that no parts drop to the ground when removing the
control unit.
– Carefully detach control unit -A-.
– Carefully take hold of coupling opening control valve -N373-
-A- at metal housing -arrow- using pliers, and pull out.
– Remove pressure sensor -B-.
