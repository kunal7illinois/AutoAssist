ening torque and sequence

2 - Tighten bolts -2- to 40 Nm.
3 - Tighten bolts -1- to 40 Nm.
Bracket -A- for bevel box for vehicles with 3.2 ltr. MPI engine:
tightening torque and sequence
– Tighten bolts in 4 stages as follows:
1 - Screw in bolts -1- and -2- hand-tight.
2 - Pre-tighten bolts -1- to 3 Nm.
3 - Tighten bolts -2- to 35 Nm.
4 - Tighten bolts -1- to 45 Nm.
