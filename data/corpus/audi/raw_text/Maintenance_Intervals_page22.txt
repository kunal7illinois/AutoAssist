Maintenance Intervals - Edition 08.2011
Ribbed V-belt - Check condition and replace if necessary. Check
tension of belt drive with a manual tensioner and retension if nec‐
essary (2.0T, V6 FSI, V6 TFSI (rear most belt), 4.2L FSI, and 5.2L
FSI engines only).
Ribbed V-belt - Replace only the V-belt for the compressor drive
(3.0 TFSI only)
Spark plugs - Replace* (A4 2.0L TFSI and A5 2.0L TFSI only).
* Spark plug replacement first at 55,000 km or 3 years, whichever
occurs first. Thereafter every 60,000 km or 3 years, whichever
occurs first.
Perform at 175,000 km in addition to the major maintenance serv‐
ice.
Timing belt - Replace. Check condition of timing belt tensioning
system, dampening pulleys, and idler pulleys and replace if nec‐
essary (Audi TTS only).
Perform at 205,000 km in addition to the major maintenance serv‐
ice and thereafter every 30,000 km.
Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) – Check ash loading according to
manufacturer work procedure; replace* if necessary. (Audi Q7
3.0L TDI and A3 2.0L TDI).
If DPF replacement is not necessary, perform check every 30,000
km thereafter until replacement becomes necessary. Subsequent
DPF replacement interval is a minimum of 200,000 km, thereafter.
Perform at 205,000 km in addition to the major maintenance serv‐
ice.
Timing belt - Replace. Check condition of timing belt tensioning
system, dampening pulleys, and idler pulleys and replace if nec‐
essary (A3 2.0L TDI only)
