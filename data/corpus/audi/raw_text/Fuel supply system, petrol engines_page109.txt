9.7 Removing and installing fuel system di‐
agnostic pump -V144-
Removing
– Remove rear left wheel housing liner ⇒ Rep. gr. 66 .
– Disconnect breather line -2- at diagnostic pump and detach
from bracket -arrow-.
– Unplug electrical connector -3-.
– Press release tab and disconnect breather pipe -5-.
– Unscrew nuts -1- and -4-.
– Detach diagnostic pump with bracket.
Installing
• Tightening torque ⇒ page 98
Installation is carried out in the reverse order; note the following:
• The breather line -5- should engage audibly.
– Install rear left wheel housing liner ⇒ Rep. gr. 66 .
