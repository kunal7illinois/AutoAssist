Cutting
Only perform cutting operations using a body saw or metal cut‐
ters.
The tooth pitch for aluminium saw blades is coarser than for sheet
steel.
Only use cutting wheels in conjunction with cleaning block DA 009
802.
Note
Always use an extractor.
WARNING
Separating cuts must only be made as specified in the “Body
repairs” workshop manual.
