Installing gearbox selector lever
– When installing gearbox selector lever, make sure that the gap
in the splines -arrow A- aligns with the wider spline -arrow B-
on the selector shaft.
Installation position: gearbox selector lever/gate relay lever
1 - Gearbox selector lever with damper weight
2 - Gate relay lever locates in guide rail of gearbox selector lever
via slide block -arrow-
Identification of cable end-pieces
The holes in the cable end-pieces have different diameters.
Cable end-piece for: Dimension “a”
-1- Gear selector cable to gearbox selector lever 8.5 mm
-2- Gate selector cable to gate relay lever 10 mm
