Attaching air conditioner compressor to body
If the air conditioner compressor is removed without opening the
refrigerant circuit, the air conditioner compressor is to be attached
to the body using a piece of welding wire for example.
In doing so, take care not to strain the refrigerant hoses at the air
conditioner compressor.
