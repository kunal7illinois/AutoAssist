Procedure
Proceed as follows:
• Turbocharger removed ⇒ page 182
• Tightening torques ⇒ page 179
– Disconnect pressure hose -arrow-.
– Detach locking plate -2- for turbocharger linkage.
Note
Disregard -items 1 and 3-.
– Slacken nut -2-.
– Adjust bypass flap via nut -3-.
• It must be possible to still just turn the bypass flap by hand.
– Hand-tighten nut -2-.
– Connect hand vacuum pump -VAS 6213- and pressure control
valve -VAS 6342- to vacuum unit and connection “II” of turbo‐
charger tester -V.A.G 1397A- , as shown in illustration.
– Close pressure control valve -VAS 6342- .
• Lever -1- should be horizontal.
– Move adjuster ring -1- on hand vacuum pump to position
“pressure” -arrow-.
– Switch on turbocharger tester -V.A.G 1397A- and set sliding
switch to position “II”.
