2.4 Removing and installing engine cover
panel with air filter element - engines
with code letters BWA and BPY
The air filter element is integrated in the engine cover panel.
Removing engine cover panel
– Unplug electrical connector -3- at air mass meter -G70- .
– Open clamps -1 and 2- and disconnect air intake hose from
air mass meter.
– Detach air intake connection at lock carrier -5-.
– Release spring-type clip -4- and detach air intake hose from
engine cover panel.
– Carefully pull engine cover panel off positions -1 - 2 - 3 - 4-
one after the other.
Caution
Always observe sequence (risk of breaking engine cover pan‐
el).
– Cover open intake hose with a clean cloth.
Removing air filter element
– Remove all bolts -arrows-.
– Unbolt heat shield.
