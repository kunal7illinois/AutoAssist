– First unscrew filter housing -1- approx. 7 turns.
– Wait approx. 10 seconds.
This will enable the oil in the filter housing to drain back into the
gearbox.
– Now detach filter housing (but not before).
– Renew heat shield -2- if missing or damaged.
Installing
Installation is carried out in reverse sequence; note the following:
– Thorough clean off any oil that has spilled on gearbox.
– Install gear oil filter with collar -arrow- facing downwards.
– Screw in filter housing and tighten to 20 Nm.
– Check gear oil level and top up as required ⇒ page 63 .
