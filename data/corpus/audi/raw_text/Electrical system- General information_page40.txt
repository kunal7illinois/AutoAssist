Procedure
The following test can only be performed if the transport mode is
activated in the battery monitor control unit -J367- or the energy
management control unit -J644- . If the transport mode is activa‐
ted later, for example when storing used vehicles, the SOC can
only be determined after the systems have switched to „data bus
rest mode“ once.
– Open driver's door.
– Switch on ignition.
– Read SOC in % off trip recorder.
Note
Depending on version, the value can be preceded by „TRA“.
• Specification: 50 % or above, charge state of battery is OK.
– If charge state of battery is below 50 %, battery must be charg‐
ed ⇒ page 35 .
– Current draw must be measured after a charging period of
5 minutes ⇒ page 30 .
– If charge state of battery is 0 %, battery is totally discharged
⇒ page 38 .
