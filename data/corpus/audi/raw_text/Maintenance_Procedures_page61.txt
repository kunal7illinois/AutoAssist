2.46 Clock, Setting
Radio Clock
Note
The clock can function as a radio-controlled quartz clock as well
as a normal quartz clock.
If an acceptable radio clock signal is received, clock automatically
switches to “radio-controlled quartz clock“ operating mode. The
radio clock reception symbol (radio tower radiating waves) ap‐
pears in display. If the clock does not receive a valid signal, it
automatically switches to “quartz clock“ mode after 3 days and
radio clock reception symbol goes out.
