9.5 Repairing holes (up to 30 mm diameter)
– First clean and dry the damaged panel.
– Using abrasive paper (120 grade), sand down the edges of the
hole to a funnel shape (a = 10 - 20 mm).
– Spray on a thin coat of primer D 195 150 A1 and allow to dry
for 10 minutes.
– Roughen the repaired surface with sandpaper (120 grade).
– Then clean the repaired area with cleaning fluid D 195 850 A1.
Allow to dry for 5 minutes.
– Spray on a thin coat of primer D 195 150 A1 and allow to dry
for 10 minutes.
