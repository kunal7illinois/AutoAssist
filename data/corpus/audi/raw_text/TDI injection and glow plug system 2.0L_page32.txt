Installing
Installation is carried out in the reverse order; note the following:
– Renew seals and/or gaskets.
– Tighten bolts securing intake manifold in diagonal sequence
starting from outside and working inwards.

Note

♦ Make sure that the seal is correctly seated in the groove on
the complete circumference of the air pipe/hose.
♦ Apply a thin coating of silicon-free lubricant to the sealing area
and the seal.
