Maintenance Intervals - Edition 08.2011
Doors - Lubricate door check straps and hood latch (except Audi
A8).
Ribbed V-belt - Check condition and replace if necessary. Check
tension of belt drive with a manual tensioner and retension if nec‐
essary (2.0T, V6 FSI, V6 TFSI (rear most belt), 4.2L FSI, and 5.2L
FSI engines only).
Ribbed V-belt - Replace only the V-belt for the compressor drive
(3.0 TFSI only)
Spark plugs - Replace* ( A4 2.0L TFSI, A5 2.0L TFSI, TTS only)
* Spark plug replacement first at 35,000 miles (55,000 km) or 3
years, whichever occurs first. Thereafter every 40,000 miles
(60,000 km) or 3 years, whichever occurs first.
Perform at 110,000 miles (175,000 km).
Timing belt - Replace. Check condition of timing belt tensioning
system, dampening pulleys, and idler pulleys and replace if nec‐
essary (Audi TT 2.0 TFSI with front wheel drive only and TTS).
Perform at 125, 0 00 miles (200,000 km) in addition to minor main‐
tenance service and thereafter every 20,000 miles (30,000 km).
Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) – Check ash loading according to
manufacturer work procedure; replace* if necessary. (Audi Q7
3.0L TDI and A3 2.0L TDI only)
*If DPF replacement is not necessary, perform check every
20,000 miles (30,000 km) thereafter until replacement becomes
necessary. Subsequent DPF replacement interval is a minimum
of 120,000 miles (200,000 km), thereafter.
Perform at 130,000 miles (205,000 km).
Timing belt - Replace. Check condition of timing belt tensioning
system, dampening pulleys, and idler pulleys and replace if nec‐
essary (A3 2.0L TDI only)
