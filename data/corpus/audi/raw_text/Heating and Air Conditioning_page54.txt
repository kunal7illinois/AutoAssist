At the start of production, the Audi TT is only fitted with “Denso”
air conditioner compressors of type “6 SEU 14”. Depending on
the engine and production period, air conditioner compressors of
other makes (e.g. “Sanden”, type “PXE 16” or “ZJX” for vehicles

4.1 Preparation
– Remove noise insulation. ⇒ General body repairs, exterior;
Rep. Gr. 66
– Slacken off and detach the poly V-belt(s). ⇒ Engine, Mechan‐
ical Components; Rep. Gr. 13
Note
♦ The air conditioner compressor can be detached from the
holder and re-attached without opening up the refrigerant
pipes ⇒ page 40 .
♦ Before removal, mark direction of rotation of poly V-belt with
chalk or felt-tipped pen. Running a used belt in the opposite
direction could destroy it.
♦ Vehicles with a 5-cyl. engine have 2 poly V-belts attached to
the air conditioner compressor. The air conditioner compres‐
sor is therefore provided with a double-belt pulley.
– If applicable, detach the air conditioner compressor from the
engine ⇒ page 40 (for vehicles with 4 or 6-cyl. engine) or
⇒ page 43 (for vehicles with 5-cyl. engine).
