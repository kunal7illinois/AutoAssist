Removing
• Engine cold.
– Release and detach relay lever -arrow-.
– Remove bolts -1- and -2-.
– Detach vacuum unit for intake manifold flaps from intake mani‐
fold and move clear to one side (vacuum hose -3- remains
connected).
– Unplug electrical connector -arrow- at coolant temperature
sender -G62- using assembly tool -T10118- .
– Pull retaining clip for coolant temperature sender -G62- up‐
wards.
– Remove coolant temperature sender -G62- out of thermostat
housing.
