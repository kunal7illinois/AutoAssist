Type Plate – Location
The type plate - A - is fastened behind the right strut tower
on the plenum chamber.
Vehicle Data Sticker
➡
The vehicle data sticker is located in the left rear of the vehicle in
the spare wheel well under the foam storage tray. The vehicle data
sticker can also be found in the service schedule
for the customer.
