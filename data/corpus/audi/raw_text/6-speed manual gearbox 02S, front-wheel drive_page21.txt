– Fit retaining clip -2- on operating rod -1- for clutch master cyl‐
inder.
– Press retaining clip into mounting on clutch pedal so that it
snaps into place audibly.
– Fit over-centre spring -2- into mounting bracket from above
while holding end of spring in correct position with assembly
tool -T10178- .
• Socket -arrow- of over-centre spring must be in vertical posi‐
tion.
– Fit actuator on clutch pedal -A- into corresponding socket in
over-centre spring -arrow-.
– Press top of clutch pedal forwards against spring pressure of
over-centre spring, push through bolt and tighten self-locking
nut.
Note
It is easier to press the pedal against the spring if you pull back
the bottom of the pedal carefully as you bring the top of the pedal
into position.
