Removing
– Use a small screwdriver to carefully prise out the selector lever
boot at the side -arrow 1-.
– Lift off the selector lever boot on both sides -arrows 2 and 3-.
– Use removal wedge -3409- to prise centre console trim out of
centre console -arrows-.
– Unplug electrical connectors.
Installing
Installation is carried out in the reverse order; note the following:
– Make sure trim engages audibly in centre console.
