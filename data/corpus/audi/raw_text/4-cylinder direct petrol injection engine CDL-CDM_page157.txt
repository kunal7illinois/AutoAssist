♦ Oil pressure tester -V.A.G 1342-
Procedure
Proceed as follows:
• Oil level OK
• Engine oil temperature approx. 80 °C
– Remove oil pressure switch ⇒ page 147 .
– Screw oil pressure switch into oil pressure tester -
V.A.G 1342- .
– Screw oil pressure tester -V.A.G 1342- into bore for oil pres‐
sure switch in oil filter bracket.
– Start engine.
• Oil pressure at idling speed: at least 1.2 bar.
• Oil pressure at 2000 rpm: at least 2.7 bar.
– Install oil pressure switch ⇒ page 147 .
