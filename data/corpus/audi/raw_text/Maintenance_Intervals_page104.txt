Service at 75,000 miles or 1 Year after Last Service for Audi RS6
ONLY
• Engine Oil / Oil Filter - Change oil and replace filter.
• Engine - Check for leaks
• Cooling system - Check coolant level and add coolant if nec‐
essary.
• Exhaust system - Check for damage and leaks.
• Engine on-board diagnostics - Check fault memory
• Doors - Lubricate door hinge mechanisms
• Battery - Check electrolyte level and add if necessary.
• Windshield wiper and washer / Headlight washer - Check ad‐
justment and function; add fluid if necessary.
• Tires and spare wheel - Check for wear and damage. Check
tire pressure. Check renewal date of tire repair set (where ap‐
plicable)
• Service reminder indicator display - Reset display.
• Brake system - Check for damage and leaks, thickness of
pads, and brake fluid level.
• Drive shafts - Check boots.
• Lights - Check all lights. Check headlight adjustment
• Front axle - Check for excessive play. Check dust seals on ball
joints and tie rod ends.
• Automatic transmission and final drive - Check for leaks.
• Dust and pollen filter - Replace filter.
• Inner cooler hoses - Clean internally
• Power steering fluid - Check fluid level, add if necessary
• Air filter - Clean housing and replace filter
• Inner cooler - Clean externally
• Radiator - Clean externally
• Condensor - Clean externally
• Fuel filter - Replace
• Spark plugs - Replace
• Timing belt drive - Replace belt, tensioner assembly and idler
pulley
• Ribbed belt - Replace
• Road test
