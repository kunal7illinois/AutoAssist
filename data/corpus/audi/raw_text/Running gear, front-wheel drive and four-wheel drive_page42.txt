Removing
– Remove wheel.
– On vehicles with vehicle level sender, unscrew nut from link‐
age at wishbone.
– Mark positions of nuts -arrows- with felt pen.
– Unscrew nuts -arrows-.
– Disengage wishbone from swivel joint.
– Apply ball joint puller 3287 A to swivel joint as shown.
– Press swivel joint out of wheel bearing housing.
WARNING
When the swivel joint is pressed out, it will release suddenly
from the wheel bearing housing and could cause injury.
