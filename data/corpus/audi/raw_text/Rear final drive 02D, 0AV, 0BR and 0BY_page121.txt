Checking oil level
– Position drip tray -VAS 6208- under final drive.
– To check oil level, unscrew oil filler plug -arrow-. (For illustra‐
tion purposes here shown with final drive removed.)
Oil level is correct if Haldex coupling is filled up to bottom lip of
filler hole or no more than 3 mm below bottom lip of filler hole.
– If necessary, top up with high-performance oil for Haldex cou‐
pling ⇒ “21.2 Topping up oil in Haldex coupling”, page 117 .
