General body repairs, interior - Edition 07.2010
Removing
– Remove cover and padding from seat pan ⇒ page 401 .
– Detach cover -1- as far as possible from padding -2-.
– Use pliers -4- to pinch off upholstery clips -3-.
– Vehicles from 09.2009 onwards: Pull seat padding out of fabric
strap -arrow- on cover.
– Detach cover from padding.
Installing
Install in reverse order of removal. Observe the following:
– Use new upholstery clips.
– Use upholstery clip pliers -V.A.G 1634- to fasten upholstery
clips.
– Fit upholstery clips in their original positions.
