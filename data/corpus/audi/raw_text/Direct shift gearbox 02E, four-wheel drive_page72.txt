Audi TT 2007 ➤
Direct shift gearbox 02E, four-wheel drive - Edition 08.2008
– With the ignition switched off, disconnect battery earth cable
⇒ Rep. Gr. 27 .
– Remove air cleaner housing (top and bottom sections) ⇒ Rep.
Gr. 24 .
– Remove bolts -arrows- and detach bracket for air cleaner
housing.
– Detach locking plate -C-.
– Press securing clip -A- upwards and detach.
– Press selector lever cable off selector shaft lever -arrow- and
pull out of support bracket -D-.
Note
♦ Do not bend or kink the selector lever cable.
♦ -Items 1 and B- can be disregarded.
– Cut open cable tie -arrow- for dust boot.
– Unplug electrical connector -2-.
– Push back dust boot -1- and disconnect B+ wire from starter
solenoid.
– Disconnect earth wire -3-.
Caution
The control unit (mechatronic unit) can be damaged by elec‐
trostatic discharge.
♦ Do not touch contact pins in gearbox connector with bare
hands.
– Touch vehicle earth with your hand (without gloves) to dis‐
charge any static electricity.
– Turn fastener anti-clockwise and unplug electrical connector
-4- on gearbox.
