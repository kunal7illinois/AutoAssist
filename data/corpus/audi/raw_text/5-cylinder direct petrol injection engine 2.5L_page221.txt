Note
♦ Renew gaskets, seals and O-rings.
♦ Fill turbocharger with engine oil at connection for oil supply
pipe.
♦ Hose connections and air pipes and hoses must be free of oil
and grease before assembly.
♦ Secure all hose connections with the correct type of hose clips
(same as original equipment) ⇒ Electronic parts catalogue .
♦ After installing the turbocharger, allow the engine to idle for
approx. 1 minute without pressing the accelerator to ensure
that the turbocharger is supplied with oil.

Note
If turbocharger is renewed, it is important to check that the addi‐
tional coolant pump relay -J496- , the continued coolant circula‐
tion pump -V51- and the solenoid for coolant circuit -N492- are
working properly. The continued coolant circulation pump -V51-
is included in the fault fin d ing programme for the additional cool‐
ant pump relay -J496- ; there is a separate fault finding
programme for the solenoid for coolant circuit -N492- .
