– Ensure correct insertion of the circlip -A-. The bevelled side
-C- faces away from the air conditioner compressor (flat side

– Clean the thread of the compressor shaft before attaching the
drive plate.
Note
The thread of the new drive plate is pre-greased at the factory
with a pre-determined quantity of a specific grease.
– For fitting the drive plate, moisten the rubber elements -D-
slightly with tyre assembly paste or soap solution for example
to provide lubrication.
– Insert rubber elements -D- into pulley -B- as shown.
– Press the rubber element of the drive plate -C- into the pulley
-E- until the drive plate -C- makes contact with the thread of
the shaft of the air conditioner compressor -B-.
– Screw the drive plate -C- onto the compressor shaft -B- by
turning it in the direction opposite to the arrow -arrow-.
– Hold the air conditioner compressor drive shaft -B- with a com‐
mercially available hexagon socket wrench (7 mm) for exam‐
ple and turn the drive plate -C- with the pulley -E- in the
direction opposite to the arrow -arrow- (tightening torque 30
Nm).
