Audi TT 2007 ➤
Brake system - Edition 04.2009
– Use release tool -T 40024- to separate brake pedal from brake
servo.
– Position release tool -T 40024- and press handles together
while simultaneously pulling the brake pedal towards driver's
seat. (The brake pedal is thereby detached from ball head of
push rod).
Installing
Clip brake pedal t o ball head of brake servo push rod.
– Hold ball head of push rod in front of mounting and push brake
pedal in direction of brake servo so that the ball head audibly
locks into place.
– Install storage compartment on driver's side: ⇒ Rep. Gr. 68
WARNING
Before driving vehicle for the first time, make sure function of
brakes is OK.
