2.32 Tire Pressure Monitoring Display,
Adapting
Note
After each change involving the tires, e.g. tire pressure or replac‐
ing or exchanging tires, the system must be programmed.
– With ignition on and vehicle standing, press tire pressure mon‐
itoring button -arrow- until tire pressure display blinks several
times in instrument cluster and a signal is heard.
– Once the signal is heard, the TPMS has been adapted.
