Removing gearbox mounting:
– Open retainers for wiring duct -arrows- and press electrical
wiring to the side.
– Unclip wiring duct.
– Remove bolts -1- and detach gearbox mounting -A-.
Installing
Installation is carried out in reverse sequence; note the following:
• Tightening torques ⇒ page 83
– Secure gearbox mounting -A-.
If gearbox support -A- has been removed:
– Install gearbox support -A-.
