2.4 Checking of electrical components ac‐
tuated by the air conditioner
Note
Various electrical components in the vehicle (e.g. the heated rear
window -Z1- and the heated seats), which do not form part of the
air conditioner, are actuated by the air conditioner operating unit,
Climatronic control unit -J255- . Electrical checking of these com‐
ponents is to be performed as described in the air conditioner
guided fault-finding routine ⇒ "Guided fault-finding" function of
vehicle diagnostic, testing and information system VAS 5051 .
