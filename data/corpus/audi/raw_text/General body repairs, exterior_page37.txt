2.9 Removing and installing gas strut and
releasing gas from gas strut
Note
Different gas struts are installed; the procedures for removal also
differ.
Removing gas strut with steel spring
WARNING
Risk of accident.
♦ Before removing a gas strut, support bonnet or secure it
in position.
– Disengage gas strut for bonnet; to do so, lift retaining spring
-2- -arrow- with a small screwdriver -1- and detach gas strut
-3- from ball stud.
Removing gas strut with plastic clip
– Using commercially available long-nose pliers, compress
plastic clip -1- as shown and pull gas strut -2- off ball stud.
Installing
Installation is carried out in the reverse order; note the following:
• The cylinder end of the gas strut must be installed on the bon‐
net side.
– Press gas strut onto ball stud until it engages audibly.
