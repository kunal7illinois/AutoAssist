Note
You must ensure that the bushes slide freely, as the clamping
pressure of a stiff bush will be insufficient even when the correct
tightening torque is applied.

for securing bolts slightly.
– Install poly V-belt tensioner for alternator and coolant pump ⇒
Rep. gr. 13 .
– Install coolant pipe (right-side) ⇒ Rep. gr. 19 .
