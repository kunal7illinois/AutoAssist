Note
♦ If the headlight retaining tabs have broken off, they can be
replaced by installing the repair kit. In this case, it is not nec‐
essary to renew the complete headlight unit.
♦ Different repair kits are available for both left and right side
headlights ⇒ Electronic parts catalogue .
