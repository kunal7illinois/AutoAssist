2 Roof side member - Renewal
WARNING
Always observe safety precautions.
Safety notes ⇒ General Information; Body Repairs, General Body
Repairs ; Safety notes .
The repair “Foam-filling roof side member” must be entered in the
Service record section of the Service Schedule under Workshop
remarks.
2.1 Tools
WARNING
When renewing the roof side member, a structural foam must
be applied on vehicles up to chassis number
TRUZZZ8JZ81044000 to provide reinforcement for a roof car‐
rier.
It is important to keep exactly to the specified sequence.
Note
♦ After applying the foam fill, the vehicle must be left standing
on a flat surface for 24 hours at a room temperature of 15° C
to allow the epoxy hard foam to harden ( curing time).
♦ Do not continue working on the vehicle until the curing time
has elapsed.
Special tools and workshop equipment required
♦ Shielded arc welding equipment 250A -VAS 6388-
