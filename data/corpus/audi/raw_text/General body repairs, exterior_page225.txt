4.4 Removing and installing radiator grille
Removing
– Remove number plate holder ⇒ page 218 .
– Remove bumper ⇒ page 171 .
– Remove bolt -2-.
– Release retaining clip -1- along bolts and press radiator grille
slightly out of bumper.
– Carefully press securing tab -3- upwards and release retaining
tab -4-; at the same time disengage radiator grille from mount‐
ing -5- -arrow-.
– Detach radiator grille -6- from bumper.
Installing
• Tightening torques ⇒ page 217
Installation is carried out in the reverse order; note the following:
– Press radiator grille on on until it engages completely.
– Install bumper ⇒ page 171 .
– Install number plate holder ⇒ page 218 .
