4.8 Adjusting camber at rear wheels
– Unscrew nut -A- on bolted joint between upper transverse link
and subframe, and loosely screw on new nut.
– Adjust camber by turning eccentric bolt with 18 mm socket
from shock absorber set -T10001- .
Note
The maximum adjustment range is 90° to the left or right of the
centre position.
– Tighten nut -A- using ring spanner -T10179- (special tool).
– Tightening torques ⇒ page 123
– Use special tool as follows.
– Check camber value again after tightening nut -A-
