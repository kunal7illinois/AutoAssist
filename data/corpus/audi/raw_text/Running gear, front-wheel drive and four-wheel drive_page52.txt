4.2 Removing and installing electronically
controlled damping control unit -J250-
Removing
The electronically controlled damping control unit -J250- is loca‐
ted under the front right seat.
– Remove front right seat ⇒ Rep. gr. 72 .
– Using a carpet knife (commercially available type), cut out
carpeting at the markings shown.
– Fold up carpeting and unclip control unit -2- -arrows-
– Unplug connector -1- and take out control unit -2-.
Installing
Installation is performed in reverse sequence; note the following:
– Reference position of suspension must be re-adapted if elec‐
tronically controlled damping control unit -J250- is renewed.
– Install carpet frame for access to control unit ⇒ Rep. gr. 70 .
– Re-adapt reference position (default position) ⇒ Vehicle diag‐
nostic, testing and information system VAS 5051.
