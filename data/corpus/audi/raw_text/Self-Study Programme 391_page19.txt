The spline shaft acts as the driving shaft between

Pull out the spline shaft downwards using the
crank.
In place of the spline shaft, insert the crank, spline
head first, as far as it will go. To open the
convertible top lock, turn the crank clockwise.
Now press the convertible top down onto the
windscreen frame by hand. To close and fully lock
the convertible top lock, turn the crank
anticlockwise.
