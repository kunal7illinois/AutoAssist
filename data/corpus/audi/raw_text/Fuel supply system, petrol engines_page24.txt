– Lower fuel tank using engine and gearbox jack -V.A.G 1383

Note
Illustration shows electrical connector on flange in installation po‐
sition.
– Unplug electrical connector -arrow- at flange.
– Remove fuel tank downwards (turning it sideways as re‐
quired).
Installing
• Tightening torques ⇒ page 14
Installation is carried out in the reverse order; note the following:
– Position fuel tank with securing strap on underbody, using en‐
gine and gearbox jack -V.A.G 1383 A- .
– When installing fuel tank, make sure that the fuel filler neck is
correctly inserted into the opening on the body.
– Secure fuel tank by first tightening bolts at points -2- and -4-,
then at points -1- and -3-.
