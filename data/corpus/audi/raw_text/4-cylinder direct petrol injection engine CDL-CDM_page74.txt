♦ If large quantities of metal shavings or abrasion are found
when performing engine repairs, this may be an indication of
damage to the crankshaft or conrod bearings. To prevent fur‐
ther damage, the following steps are required after completion
of repair work: clean the oil galleries carefully and renew the
oil spray jets, oil cooler and oil filter.
