Removing
– Remove steering box ⇒ page 267 .
– Clean steering box in area of adjuster screw.
– Clamp steering box as illustrated with 2 bolts (M10 x 60).
– Measure torque required to turn steering.
– First turn steering to full right lock (as seen in direction of trav‐
el).
– Then turn steering evenly back to full left lock using friction
gauge, e.g. -VAS 6222- . Read off torque required to turn
steering.
