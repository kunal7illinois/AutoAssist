• Oil pressure at idling speed: 1.2 ... 2.1 bar.
• Oil pressure at 2000 rpm: 1.6 ... 2.1 bar.
• Oil pressure at 3700 rpm: 3.0 ... 4.0 bar.
Note
During the running-in period, the oil pressure at 2000 rpm can be
between 3.0 and 4.0 bar.
