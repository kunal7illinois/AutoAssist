Adjusting
• Mounting gearbox on assembly stand ⇒ page 125 .
• Sealing surfaces of clutch and gearbox housings must be free
of sealant.
• When taking measurements, install only the shaft to be meas‐
ured.
– Press tapered roller bearing outer race with shim (thickness
1.70 mm) into gearbox housing. Support gearbox housing with
thrust piece -2050- directly below bearing mounting.
– Fit complete 1st to 4th gear output shaft into clutch housing.
– Fit gearbox housing and tighten bolts -A- and -B- diagonally to
correct torque.
