5.33 Removing and installing rear cross pan‐
el trim
Special tools and workshop equipment required
♦ Removal wedge -3409-
Removing
– Fold the entire luggage compartment floor covering forwards.
– Take floor covering out of luggage compartment.
– Unclip rear cross panel trim -1- vertically upwards using re‐
moval wedge -3409- .
Installing
Install in reverse order of removal. Observe the following:
Note
Check the clips for damage or deformation and renew if neces‐
sary.
– Insert rear cross panel trim -1- so that retainers (left and right)
engage in pins -3- and eyelets -4-.
– Fold lip of rear lid seal -2- over rear cross panel trim -1-.
