Note
♦ Make sure that the door is properly sealed when it is installed;
otherwise the crash sensor (pressure sensor) may not function
correctly.
♦ Make sure that all components (e.g. cover panel, loudspeaker,
door trim) are fitted correctly.
♦ The door trim clips provide a seal for the system. Renew door
trim clips if necessary.
♦ Make sure electrical connectors are fitted correctly (as far as
stop) and engage audibly.
– Switch on ignition.
WARNING
The battery must be connected with the ignition switched on.
If pyrotechnic components (e.g. airbag or belt tensioner) are
inexpertly repaired, this may result in unwanted triggering after
connecting the battery. There must not be anyone in the vehi‐
cle while you are connecting the battery.
– Connect battery earth wire with ignition switched on ⇒ Elec‐
trical system; Rep. gr. 27 .
Note

you must interrogate, erase and then re-interrogate the fault
memory ⇒ Vehicle diagnostic, testing and information system
