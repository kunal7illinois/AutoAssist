Installing
Installation is performed in reverse sequence; note the following:
Do not tighten bolts securing trailing arm to wheel bearing housing
until all other suspension components (especially spring and
shock absorber) on that side are fitted. The wheel suspension
must be in extended position before securing. The trailing arm
and the wheel bearing housing will only then move into the re‐
quired position -arrows-.
Installation position: Bolt connection between trailing arm and
wheel bearing housing
