14.3 Removing and installing iPod holder -
R192-
Special tools and workshop equipment required
♦ Removal wedge -3409-
– Switch off all electrical equipment.
– Remove ignition key.
Removing
– Open glove box.
– Remove iPod -1- from iPod holder -R192- .
– Using removal wedge -3409- , carefully lever iPod holder -
R192- out of its retainers -arrows-.
– Pull iPod holder -R192- out of glove box and unplug electrical
connector.
Installing
– Installation is performed in reverse sequence.
