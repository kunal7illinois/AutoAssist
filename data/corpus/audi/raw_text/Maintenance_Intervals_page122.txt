Service at 60,000 miles or 1 Year after Last Service
♦ Engine Oil – change and replace filter
♦ Engine - Check for leaks
♦ Cooling System – check level, add if necessary
♦ Exhaust System – check for damage, leaks
♦ Engine On Board Diagnostics – check fault memory
♦ Doors – lubricate doors, check straps
♦ Battery – check electrolyte level and add if needed
♦ Wiper/Washer – check adjustment and function, add fluid if
necessary
♦ Automatic Shift Lock - Check
♦ Manual Transmission – check shift and interlock (where ap‐
plicable)
♦ Tires and Spare – check condition and pressure
♦ Service Reminder Display – reset
♦ Brake System – check
♦ Drive Shaft Boots – check
♦ Road Test
♦ Lights – check
♦ Front Axle – check dust seals on ball joints and tie rod ends
♦ Haldex Clutch - Change oil and filter (TT only)
♦ Automatic Transmission – check for leaks
♦ Manual Transmission and Final Drive – check for leaks (where
applicable)
♦ Dust and Pollen Filter – replace
♦ Headlamps - inspect, adjust if necessary
♦ Sliding Roof - clean, lubricate guide rails (A4, A6.S6/allroad,
A8/S8 only)
