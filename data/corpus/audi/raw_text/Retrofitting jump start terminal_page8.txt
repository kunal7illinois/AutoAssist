Fitting instructions: Retrofitting jump start terminal in engine compartment - Edition 07.2007
– Centre-punch the marked location for the hole -arrow-.
– Pre-drill using a ∅ 5 mm bit.
– Then use a ∅ 9 mm bit to drill the final hole.
– Remove swarf with a vacuum cleaner.
– Insert pop rivet bolt -N 908 985 02- into hole and secure rivet
using special pop rivet pliers -V.A.G 1753A- .
– Secure slave start socket (earth stud) -4F0 972 501- -item 2-
on pop rivet bolt -1- (9 Nm).
– Mark the location for the holes for the slave start socket -U6-
on the left suspension turret as follows:
• Hole -2- must be located on a level with the centre of the egg-
shaped indentation -3-.
• The distance -a- from the edge of the indentation to the hole
must be 30 mm.
– Hold the slave start socket -U6- with the front mounting eye
horizontally over the marking -2-.
– Mark the location for the hole -1- for the rear mounting eye of
the slave start socket -U6- on the suspension turret.
