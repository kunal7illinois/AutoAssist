3.3 Checking combination valve for secon‐
dary air system for proper operation and
leakage - engine code BUB
Procedure
• Vacuum hoses and hose connections do not leak.
• Vacuum hoses are not clogged.
– Disconnect hose -arrow- and attach test hose to hose leading
to combination valve for secondary air system.
– Blow lightly into test hose with your mouth (do not use com‐
pressed air).
• The combination valve for secondary air system should be
closed; it should not be possible to blow through the hose.
– Blow into test hose with your mouth using more pressure (do
not use compressed air).
• The combination valve for secondary air system should open;
it should now be possible to blow through the hose.
– Renew combination valve for secondary air if you cannot de‐
termine the switching point ⇒ page 189 .
3.4 Removing and installing combination
valve for secondary air system - engine
code BUB
Removing
– Remove throttle valve module -J338- ⇒ Rep. Gr. 24 .
– Disconnect hose -2- from combination valve for secondary air
system.
– Unscrew bolts -1- and detach combination valve for secondary
air system.
Installing
• Tightening torque ⇒ page 184 .
Installation is carried out in the reverse order; note the following:
Note
Renew seals and/or gaskets.
– Install throttle valve module -J338- ⇒ Rep. Gr. 24 .
