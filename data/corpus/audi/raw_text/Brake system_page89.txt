6.4 Detaching and attaching handbrake ca‐
ble from/to brake caliper
Removing
– Press brake lever -2- in direction of arrow and disengage
handbrake cable -3-.
– Compress retaining tabs -1- and pull out handbrake cable
through guide.
Installing
– Insert handbrake cable through guide
– Compress retaining tabs -1- and engage. Make sure that both
retaining tabs are engaged.
– Press brake lever -2- in direction of arrow and attach hand‐
brake cable -3-.
