12.1 Removing and installing four-wheel
drive control unit -J492-
Special tools and workshop equipment required
♦ Allen key (4 mm)
♦ Torque wrench (2 ... 10 Nm) -V.A.G 1783-
♦ Drip tray for workshop hoist -VAS 6208-
– Switch off ignition.
– Unplug connectors -1- and -2- at top of control unit.
– Position drip tray under final drive.
Note
It is advisable to have a workshop trolley with tools and cleaning
cloths within easy reach so that removed parts can be put down
safely.
