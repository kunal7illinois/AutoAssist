Checking gear lever setting
• With the gearbox in neutral, the gear lever should rest in the
3rd/4th gear gate.
– Depress clutch.
– Select all gears several times. Pay particular attention to the
operation of the reverse gear locking mechanism.
• The gear lever should return by itself from the reverse gear
gate to the 3rd/4th gear gate.
If the gear lever sticks or baulks repeatedly when engaging a
gear, perform the adjustment procedure again.
