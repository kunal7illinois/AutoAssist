2.2 Removing and installing radiator outlet
coolant temperature sender -G83-
Removing
Proceed as follows:
• Engine cold.
– Open filler cap on coolant expansion tank briefly to dissipate
residual pressure in cooling system.
– Remove front noise insulation -1- ⇒ Rep. Gr. 66 .
– Unplug electrical connector -2- at radiator outlet coolant tem‐
perature sender -G83- .
Note
Place a cloth underneath to catch escaping coolant.
– Pull out retaining clip -1- and detach radiator outlet coolant
temperature sender -G83- .
Installing
Installation is carried out in the reverse order; note the following:
Note
♦ Fit new O-ring.
♦ Insert new radiator outlet coolant temperature sender -G83-
immediately into connection to avoid loss of coolant.
– Install noise insulation ⇒ Rep. Gr. 66 .
– Check coolant level ⇒ page 159 .
