WARNING
♦ Do not under any circumstances allow brake fluid to come
into contact with fluids that contain mineral oils (e.g. oil,
petrol, cleaning agents). Mineral oils damage the plugs
and seals in the brake system.
♦ Brake fluid is poisonous and must NOT be sucked through
a hose with the mouth. On account of its corrosive effect
it must also not be allowed to come into contact with paint‐
work.
♦ Always observe the relevant environmental regulations for
disposal.
