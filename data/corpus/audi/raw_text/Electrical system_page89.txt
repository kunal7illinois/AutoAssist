2.9 Converting headlights for driving on the
left or right side of the road
When travelling in countries where the vehicle is to be driven on
the other side of the road, both headlights must be adjusted to
prevent dazzling by the asymmetric dipped beam.
Procedure
– Remove headlight ⇒ page 76 .
– Remove screw -2- (if fitted).
– Release retaining clip -arrow- and detach housing cover -1-
for dipped beam headlight/daytime running light from head‐
light housing.
– Check position of adjustment lever -1- at both headlights.
• Lever left = Driving on left
• Lever right = Driving on right
– If necessary, swivel lever until it engages in desired position
