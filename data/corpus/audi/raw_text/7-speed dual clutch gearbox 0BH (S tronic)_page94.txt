– Remove oil pan ⇒ page 86 .
Caution
The mechatronic unit can be damaged by electrostatic dis‐
charge.
♦ Do not touch the contact pins in the connector with bare
hands.
– Touch an earthed metal object, such as a heater radiator or
lifting platform with your hand (without gloves) to discharge
static electricity.
Caution
The electrical wiring can be damaged.
♦ Do not pull on the electrical wiring.
♦ Work very carefully and do not use force. The catch on
connector -2- can break off. If this happens, the mecha‐
tronic unit for dual clutch gearbox -J743- must be re‐
newed.
– Using a small screwdriver, carefully release connector -1- for
gearbox input speed sender -G182- and clutch temperature
sender -G509- and unplug connector.
Note
-G182- and -G509- will have to be renewed if the electrical wiring
is damaged.
