1.3 Removing and installing dash panel trim
(driver side)
Removing
– Remove light switch ⇒ Rep. gr. 96 .
– Remove dash panel end trim ⇒ page 146 .
– Remove bolts -1- and -2-.
– Apply small screwdriver to top edge of dash panel trim (driver
side) -3- and unclip it from dash panel -arrow-.
– Unclip 16-pin connector -T16- .
– Detach dash panel trim (driver side).
Installing
Install in reverse order of removal.
