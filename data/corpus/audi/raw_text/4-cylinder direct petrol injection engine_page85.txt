Loosening cylinder head bolts
– Loosen cylinder head bolts in the sequence indicated and re‐
move.
Tightening sequence for cylinder head
– Then tighten cylinder head bolts in the sequence -1 ... 10- as
follows:
1. Tighten with torque wrench initially to 40 Nm.
2. Turn 90° (1/4 turn) further using a rigid wrench.
3. Turn 90° (1/4 turn) further using a rigid wrench.
Checking cylinder head for distortion
– Use straight edge and feeler gauge to measure for distortion
at several points.
♦ Max. permissible distortion: 0.05 mm
