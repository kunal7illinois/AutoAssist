11 Steering column switch module
General notes
♦ The steering column switch module consists of the return ring
with slip ring, steering angle sender -G85- , steering column
switches (turn signal switch, wiper switch, cruise control
switch), steering column electronics control unit -J527- and the
ignition/starter switch.
♦ Fault finding is performed via „Guided Fault Finding“ ⇒ Vehicle
diagnostic, testing and information system VAS 5051.
