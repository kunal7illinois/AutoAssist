6 Servicing rear brakes
Note
♦ Use the brake filling and bleeding equipment -VAS 5234- or
the upgrade kit and extraction unit -V.A.G 1869/4- to extract
brake fluid from brake fluid reservoir.
♦ Before removing brake caliper or disconnecting brake hose
from brake caliper, insert brake pedal actuator -V.A.G 1869/2-
to prevent brake fluid reservoir from emptying completely.
WARNING
After installing the brake pads, depress pedal firmly several
times with the vehicle stationary, so that the brake pads are
properly seated in their normal operating position.
