3.5.39 Camshaft Adjustment Valve 1, Exhaust
The following procedure is used to diagnose Camshaft Adjust‐
ment Valve 1 (exhaust) -N318- which is controlled by Engine
Control Module (ECM) - J623- .
Special tools and workshop equipment required

Test requirements
• Fuse -SC7- OK.
• Engine Control Module (ECM) Power Supply Relay -J271-
OK.
• Battery voltage at least 12.5 volts.
• All electrical cons u mers such as, lights and rear window de‐

• Vehicles with automatic transmission, shift selector lever into
