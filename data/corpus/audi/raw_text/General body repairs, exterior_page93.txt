Setting with respect to side window
– Insert the template as shown in the door seal -3- between the
mounting rail -4- of the side window -1- and the door window
-2-.
– The door window must be in the area of the small groove.
Gap widths
The gap widths can be checked using setting gauge -3371- .
• Dimension -A- = 3.5 mm ± 0.5 mm
• Dimension -B- = 3.5 mm ± 0.5 mm
• Dimension -C- = 0 mm ... –1 mm
• Dimension -D- = 0 mm ... +1 mm
Adjusting door at striker plate
– Loosen bolts -2-.
– Move striker plate -1- until door shell is flush with body contour.
– Tighten bolts to 20 Nm.
Note
The striker plate must only be adjusted towards the centre of the
vehicle and not in height, as otherwise the lock could become
damaged.
