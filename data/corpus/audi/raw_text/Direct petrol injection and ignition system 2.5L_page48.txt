♦ When installing, it is important to re-attach the Lambda probe
wiring at the same locations to prevent it from coming into
contact with the exhaust pipe.

and grease before assembly.
♦ Do not use any lubricants containing silicone when assem‐
bling.

(same as original equipment) ⇒ Electronic parts catalogue .

at their connections, spray rust remover onto the worm thread
