2.5 Checking oil pressure
Special tools and workshop equipment required
♦ Oil pressure tester -V.A.G 1342-
Procedure
• Oil level OK
• Engine oil temperature approx. 80 °C.
– Remove oil pressure switch -F1- ⇒ page 156 .
– Connect oil pressure tester -V.A.G 1342- to threaded hole for
oil pressure switch -F1- .
– Screw a used oil pressure switch into threaded hole on oil
pressure tester -V.A.G 1342- to seal hole.
– Start engine.
• Oil pressure at idling speed: at least 0.8 bar.
• Oil pressure at 2000 rpm: at least 2.0 bar.
– Install oil pressure switch -F1- ⇒ page 156 .
