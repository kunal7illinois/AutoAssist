13.3 Removing and installing CD changer -
R41-
Special tools and workshop equipment required
♦ Radio release tool -T10057-
– Switch off all electrical equipment.
– Remove ignition key.
Removing
– Open glove box.
– Take out CDs that are still in CD changer -R41- .
– Insert two radio release tools -T10057- into slots on CD chang‐
er -R41- until they engage. Pointed ends of grips must face
outwards.
– Grasp hold of grips on radio release tool -T10057- and pull CD
changer -R41- out of mounting frame.
– Unplug electrical connector at CD changer -R41- .
– Press release tabs on CD changer -R41- and remove radio
release tool -T10057- .
Installing
– Plug in electrical connector.
– Slide CD changer -R41- into mounting frame until it engages.
