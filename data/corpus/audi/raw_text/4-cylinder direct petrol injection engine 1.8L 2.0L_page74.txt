– Bolt dial gauge -VAS 6079- with universal dial gauge bracket
-VW 387- onto cylinder block and set it against crank web.
– Press crankshaft against dial gauge by hand and set gauge to

• New: 0.07 ... 0.23 mm.
• Wear limit: 0.30 mm.
