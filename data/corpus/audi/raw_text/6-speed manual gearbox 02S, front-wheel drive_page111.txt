5.5.1 Dismantling
– Mount gearbox on assembly stand.
– Drain off gear oil.
– Remove clutch release lever and release bearing ⇒ page 33 .
– Remove guide sleeve ⇒ page 33 .
– Unbolt gearbox housing cover -arrow-.
– Take off cover. If necessary carefully lever up all round and
alternating between sides on the protruding flanges, being
careful not to damage sealing surfaces.
