– Remove gearbox selector lever ⇒ Item 2 (page 54) .
– Carefully lever out selector shaft oil seal.
Installing
– Lightly oil outer circumference of new oil seal and half-fill
space between sealing lips -arrow- with grease for radial oil
seals -G 052 128 A1- .
– Drive in new oil seal to stop, taking care to keep oil seal
straight.
• Lettering on seal must face outwards (to pressing tool).
– Install gearbox selector lever ⇒ Item 2 (page 54) .
– Install gate relay lever together with cable end-piece
⇒ page 56 .
– Check gearbox oil ⇒ page 85 .
