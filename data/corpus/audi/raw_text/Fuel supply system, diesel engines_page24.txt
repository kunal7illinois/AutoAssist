– With fuel tank removed, mark fuel supply line -1- and fuel re‐

TT Coupé:
WARNING
Large amounts of fuel can escape.
♦ The fuel tank must not be more than1 /3 full when remov‐
ing the fuel delivery unit, otherwise a large amount of fuel
will escape.
All vehicles (continued):
– Unscrew locking ring using wrench -T10202- .
– Take flange (right-side) and seal of fuel delivery unit out of
opening in fuel tank and move clear, leaving pipes connected.
– In opening in fuel tank, press release tab to disconnect supply
pipe -1- to suction-jet pump.
– Detach fuel supply pipe -2- from fuel delivery unit.
– Remove fuel delivery unit.
Note
♦ When removing fuel delivery unit, make sure you do not bend
float arm of fuel gauge sender -G- .
♦ Keep in mind that the fuel delivery unit still contains fuel.
