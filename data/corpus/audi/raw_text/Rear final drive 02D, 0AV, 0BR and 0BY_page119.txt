Caution
Different types of high-performance oil for Haldex coupling
♦ The rear final drive units “02D/0AV” and “0BR/0BY” have
different Haldex couplings and therefore different types of
oil for the Haldex coupling.
♦ The two different types of oil for the Haldex couplings must
not be interchanged or mixed together.
