2.14 Removing and installing centre console
(front)
Removing
– Remove centre console side trim panels ⇒ page 17 .
– Remove centre console trim: vehicles with manual gearbox
⇒ page 17 , vehicles with dual clutch gearbox ⇒ page 18 .
– Remove grip moulding for handbrake lever ⇒ page 21 .
– Remove ashtray ⇒ page 21 .
– Unscrew bolts -1 ... 10-.
Note
The illustration shows a vehicle with dual clutch gearbox.
– Apply handbrake as firmly as possible.
– Pull the rear centre console upwards -arrow A- and guide it
past the handbrake lever. To do so, slide the centre console
forwards as far as possible -arrow B-.
– Remove centre console to the rear over selector lever or gear
lever.
