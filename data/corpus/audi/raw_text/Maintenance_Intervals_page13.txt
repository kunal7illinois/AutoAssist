Perform after the first 205,000 km or 195,000 km after a Diesel
Particulate Filter (DPF) replacement
Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) – Check ash loading according to
manufacturer work procedure; replace* if necessary. (Audi Q7
3.0L TDI and A3 2.0L TDI).
If DPF replacement is not necessary, perform check every 30,000
km thereafter until replacement becomes necessary. Subsequent
DPF replacement interval is a minimum of 200,000 km, thereafter.
Perform every 205,000 km.
Timing belt - Replace. Check condition of timing belt tensioning
system, dampening pulleys, and idler pulleys and replace if nec‐
essary (A3 2.0L TDI only).
