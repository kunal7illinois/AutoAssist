3.5.30 Catalytic Converter, Checking
Test requirements
• Battery voltage at least 12.5 volts.
• Oxygen Sensors OK.
• No leaks or damage to exhaust system.
Note
If ANY Fuel Trim, misfire, or sensor faults are set, repair those
faults BEFORE replacing the Catalytic Converter. Failure to do so
will damage the replacement converter.
