10.1 MY 2003 Maintenance Intervals - USA
Note
♦ Every two years regardless of mileage: Brake fluid - Replace
fluid (all vehicles).
♦ Every two years regardless of mileage: Cloth top - Check
function and rollover protection of with cloth top down (Audi
A4 Cabriolet only)
Service at 5,000 miles or 1 Year after Last Service for Audi RS6
ONLY
♦ Engine Oil – change and replace filter
♦ Wiper/Washer – check adjustment and function, add fluid if
necessary
♦ Tires and Spare – check condition and pressure. Rotate tires.
♦ Service Reminder Display – reset
♦ Brake System – check for damage and leaks. Check pad
thickness and brake fluid level.
Service at 10,000 miles or 1 Year after Last Service
♦ Engine Oil – change and replace filter
♦ Cooling System – check level, add if necessary
♦ Exhaust System – check for damage, leaks
♦ Engine On Board Diagnostics – check fault memory
♦ Doors – lubricate doors, check straps
♦ Battery – check electrolyte level and add if needed
♦ Wiper/Washer – check adjustment and function, add fluid if
necessary
♦ Automatic Shift Lock - Check
♦ Manual Transmission – check shift and interlock (if applicable)
♦ Tires and Spare – check condition and pressure
♦ Service Reminder Display – reset
♦ Brake System – check
♦ Drive Shaft Boots – check
♦ Road Test
♦ Lights – check
Service at 15,000 miles or 1 Year after Last Service for Audi RS6
ONLY
• Engine Oil / Oil Filter - Change oil and replace filter.
• Engine - Check for leaks
• Cooling system - Check coolant level and add coolant if nec‐
essary.
