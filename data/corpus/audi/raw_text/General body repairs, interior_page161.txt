2.9 Removing and installing mounting frame
for radio / navigation system
Removing
– Remove instrument cluster ⇒ Rep. gr. 90 .
– Remove glove box ⇒ page 12 .
– Remove radio / navigation system ⇒ Rep. gr. 91 .
– Remove trim cover for centre console ⇒ page 147 .
– Unscrew side bolts -1- and -2- for centre console.
– Remove bolts -arrows-.
– Turn mounting frame -1- 90° in direction of -arrow-, slightly
pressing mounting frame retainers to side.
– Take out mounting frame.
Installing
Install in reverse order of removal.
Tightening torque ⇒ page 144

position.
