61 – Convertible roof, hardtop, canopy
1 Repairing convertible roof (soft top)
1.1 Safety precautions
Observe the following precautions to avoid possible injury and/or
damage to the convertible roof:
♦ Always apply the handbrake.
♦ Switch on the ignition and leave it on while opening / closing
the convertible roof.
♦ Remove any objects from the convertible roof storage com‐
partment to avoid damage to the convertible roof or the rear
window.
