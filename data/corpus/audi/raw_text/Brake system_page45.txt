7.2 Basic setting of inlet valves and sepa‐
rating valves (Mark 60EC)
The basic setting of the inlet valves should only be performed if
the ABS control unit -J104- has been renewed.
To do so, use the vehicle diagnosis, testing and information sys‐
tem -VAS 505x- or -VAS 5052- .
Note
If the basic setting of the inlet valves has to be performed, you
must then perform the basic setting of the separating valves.
