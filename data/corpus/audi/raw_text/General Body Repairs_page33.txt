9.2 Repairing dents
– First clean and dry the damaged panel.
– Now apply heat to the affected area using a hot air blower until
the dent can be pressed out with a suitable tool.
– Lightly sand down the dented area with abrasive paper (120
grain).
– Then clean the repaired area with cleaning fluid D 195 850 A1.
Allow to dry for 5 minutes.
– Spray on a thin coat of primer D 195 150 A1 and allow to dry
for 10 minutes.
