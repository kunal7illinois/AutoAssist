Procedure
• Gearbox in vehicle
– Remove flange shaft (left-side) ⇒ page 250 .
– Pull out flange shaft oil seal using extractor tool -T20143/2- .
– Lightly oil outer circumference of new oil seal.
– Drive in new oil seal onto stop (take care to keep oil seal
straight).
– Pack space between sealing lip and dust lip -arrow- half full
with sealing grease -G 052 128 A1- .
– Install flange shaft (left-side) ⇒ page 250 .
– Check gear oil level in manual gearbox and top up as required
⇒ page 138 .
