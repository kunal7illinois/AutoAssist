Installing
Install in reverse order of removal. Observe the following:
– First engage retaining tab -4- of trim -3- in opening -arrow- of
release mechanism -1-.
– Press button -2- of release mechanism button -1- as far for‐
wards as it will go (in direction of travel) and hold it in this
position.
– Press down trim and engage it with release mechanism.
– Make sure that all retaining tabs are engaged.
– Make sure that opening in cover is properly covered and that
there are no creases in cover in this area.
