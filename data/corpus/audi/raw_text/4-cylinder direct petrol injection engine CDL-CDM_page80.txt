Removing
Proceed as follows:
– Remove sump ⇒ page 131 .
– Unscrew bolts -arrows- and remove toothed belt cover (top
section).
Caution
Irreparable damage can be caused if the toothed belt slips.
♦ Turn crankshaft only in direction of engine rotation.
– Rotate crankshaft by t urning bolt for toothed belt sprocket until
camshaft sprocket is positioned at “TDC”.
• Marking on camshaft sprocket must face arrow on toothed belt
cover -top arrow-.
• Notch on vibration damper must face arrow on toothed belt
cover -bottom arrow-.
