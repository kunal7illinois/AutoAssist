Sump - tightening torque and sequence
– Tighten bolts securing sump in three stages as follows:
1. Tighten bolts -1 ... 20- securing sump to cylinder block in
diagonal sequence initially to 5 Nm.
2. Tighten bolts securing sump to gearbox -arrows- to 40 Nm.
3. Tighten bolts -1 ... 20- securing sump to cylinder block in
diagonal sequence to 15 Nm.
