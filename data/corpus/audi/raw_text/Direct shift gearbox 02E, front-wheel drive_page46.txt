3.7 Removing and installing selector lever
lock solenoid -N110-
The selector lever lock solenoid -N110- is integrated in the shift
unit; it cannot be renewed separately.
Note
The shift unit has to be renewed if the selector lever lock solenoid
-N110- is defective ⇒ page 33 .
