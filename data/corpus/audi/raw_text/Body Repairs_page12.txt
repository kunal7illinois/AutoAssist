4 General notes on repairs to alumini‐
um parts
The repair methods required for the purely aluminium body parts
are similar to the established procedures for the other Audi mod‐
els with aluminium body.
New joining technique
Flow-drill screws
Flow-drill screws can be easily released in the workshop and re‐
placed with new screws. Oversize screws are available for dam‐
aged threads. New panels have to be pre-drilled to take these
screws.
