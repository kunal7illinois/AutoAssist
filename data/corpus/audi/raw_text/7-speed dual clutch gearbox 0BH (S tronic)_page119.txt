separate oil filling.

3.1 Overview of tightening torques
A - Oil drain plug
• Tightening torque: 15 Nm
B - Oil filler plug
• Tightening torque: 15 Nm
• Always renew plugs -A- and -B-.
3.2 Checking gear oil level
Special tools and workshop equipment required
♦ Used oil collection and extraction unit -V.A.G 1782-
♦ Safety goggles
Note
♦ Observe the general repair instructions ⇒ page 10 .
♦ Observe rules for cleanliness when working on the gearbox
⇒ page 12 .
♦ Comply with notes on dual clutch gearbox 0BH „Disposal reg‐
ulations for oils“ ⇒ page 6 .
Procedure
• Vehicle must be absolutely horizontal (on a four-pillar lifting
platform or over an inspection pit).
• Selector lever in position „P“, handbrake applied.
• Engine not running.
