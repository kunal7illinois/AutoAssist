5 Checking oil level in manual gearbox
Special tools and workshop equipment required
♦ Torque wrench -V.A.G 1331-
♦ Multi-point bit -3357-
or
♦ 17 mm socket (hexagon key)
• Vehicle must be horizontal when checking oil level.
– Raise vehicle.
– Remove noise insulation (centre) by releasing fasteners
-1 ... 4-.
– Remove oil filler plug -arrow-.
• Specification: oil level up to bottom lip of filler hole
– Top up with gear oil if necessary; specification ⇒ page 1 .
– Screw in oil filler plug -arrow- with new seal.
– Tighten oil filler plug to specified torque.
