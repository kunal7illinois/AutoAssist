23A-26 AUTOMATIC TRANSAXLE
AUTOMATIC TRANSAXLE DIAGNOSIS
TORQUE CONVERTER STALL TEST JUDGEMENT
RESULTS
1. Stall speed is too high in "D" range only
• Malfunction of torque converter (Slippage on the splines of
the torque converter and the input shaft)
• Low line pressure
• Low-reverse brake slippage and malfunction of one-way
clutch
2. Stall speed is too high in "D" range only
• Underdrive clutch slippage
3. Stall speed is too high in "R" range only
• Reverse clutch slippage
4. Stall speed too low in both "D" and "R" ranges
• Malfunction of torque converter (Slippage of the one-way
clutch)
• Insufficient engine output
HYDRAULIC PRESSURE TESTS
M1231005500165
Check the A/T fluid level and temperature. Check engine
coolant temperature.
• A/T fluid level: "HOT" mark on the dipstick
• A/T fluid temperature: 70 − 80°C (158 − 176°F)
• Engine coolant temperature: 80 − 100°C (176 − 212°F)
CAUTION
The fluid temperature should be within 70 − 80 °C (158 −
176°F) during the test.
