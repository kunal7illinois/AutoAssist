>>E<< SYNCHRONIZER RING INSTALLATION
CAUTION
There are 3rd speed and 4th speed synchronizer rings, if
NOTCH the wrong one is installed it will effect the shift feeling.
1. Ascertain whether or not there are identification notches on
the synchronizer ring.
Two notches: 3rd speed synchronizer ring
No notches: 4th speed synchronizer ring
2. Install the synchronizer ring so that it does not angle to the
cone of the gear.
