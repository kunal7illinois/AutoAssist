13A-142 MULTIPORT FUEL INJECTION (MFI) <2.4L>
MULTIPORT FUEL INJECTION (MFI) DIAGNOSIS
STEP 1. Check connector B-17 at heated oxygen sensor
(front) and connector C-60 at ECM <M/T> or connector C-
57 at PCM <A/T> for damage.
Q:Is the connector in good condition?
CONNECTOR: B-17
YES : Go to Step 2.
NO : Repair or replace it. Refer to GROUP 00E, Harness
Connector Inspection P.00E-2. Then go to Step 3.
