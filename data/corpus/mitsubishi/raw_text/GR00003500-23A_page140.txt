STEP 1. Using scan tool MB991502, check data list item 61:
Park/Neutral Position Switch.
CAUTION
To prevent damage to scan tool MB991502, always turn the
ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position before con-
necting or disconnecting scan tool MB991502.
(1) Connect scan tool MB991502 to the data link connector.
(2) Turn the ignition switch to the "ON" position.
(3) Set scan tool MB991502 to data reading mode for item 61:
Park/Neutral Position Switch.
• Move the selector lever to "P," "R," "N," "D," "3," "2," "L"
and sport mode positions to confirm whether shown on
the scan tool. (Vehicles with sport mode is indicated as
"D" on the scan tool.)
