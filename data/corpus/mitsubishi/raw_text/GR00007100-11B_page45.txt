OIL SEAL
1. Check the oil seal lip for wear and damage. Replace the oil
seal if necessary.
2. Check the oil seal lip for deterioration. Replace the oil seal if
necessary.

COUNTERBALANCE SHAFT
1. Check the oil holes for clogging and clean if necessary.
2. Check the journal for seizure, damage and contact with
bearing. If there is anything wrong with the journal, replace
the counterbalance shaft, bearing or front case assembly if
required.
