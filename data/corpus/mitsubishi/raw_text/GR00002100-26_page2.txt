FRONT AXLE
The front axle consists of a knuckle, front hub, unit
bearing and drive shaft. The unit bearing is press-fit-
ted to the front hub and bolted to the knuckle. Also,
the unit bearing utilizes a double row angular contact
ball bearing. The drive shaft has a Tripod joint (TJ)
on the transaxle side and a Birfield joint (BJ) on the
wheel side. A center bearing and an inner shaft have
been adopted in 3.0L engine.
