IGNITION SYSTEM
SPARK PLUG CHECK AND CLEANING
M1163004300148
<3.0L ENGINE − without VARIABLE INDUCTION
CONTROL (VIC) system>
CAUTION
• Do not attempt to adjust the gap of the platinum plug.
<3.0L ENGINE – WITHOUT VIC>
• Cleaning of the platinum plug may result in damage to
the platinum tips. Therefore, if carbon deposits must be
removed, use a plug cleaner and complete cleaning
within 20 seconds to protect the electrode. Do not use a
wire brush.
Check the plug gap and replace if the limit is exceeded.
PLATINUM Standard value: 1.0 − 1.1 mm (0.039 − 0.043 inch)
TIPS
Limit: 1.3 mm (0.051 inch)
AAKKXX0000338833AG
<3.0L ENGINE − with VARIABLE INDUCTION
CONTROL (VIC) system>
CAUTION
• Do not attempt to adjust the gap of the iridium plug.
<3.0L ENGINE – WITH VIC>
• Cleaning of the iridium plug may result in damage to
the iridium and platinum tips. Therefore, if carbon
deposits must be removed, use a plug cleaner and
complete cleaning within 20 seconds to protect the
IRIDIUM
electrode. Do not use a wire brush.
TIP
Check the plug gap and replace if the limit is exceeded.
PLATINUM Standard value: 0.7 − 0.8 mm (0.028 − 0.031 inch)
TIP
Limit: 1.0 mm (0.039 inch)
