.<<A>> CYLINDER HEAD BOLT REMOVAL
Using special tool MD998051, loosen the cylinder head bolts.
Loosen each bolt evenly, little by little, by two or three steps.

<<B>> RETAINER LOCK REMOVAL
1. Set special tool MD998772 as illustrated to compress the
valve spring. Remove the retainer locks.
2. Relieve the spring tension and remove the valve, retainer,
spring, etc.
Store removed valves, springs and other parts, tagged to
indicate their cylinder number and location for assembly.
