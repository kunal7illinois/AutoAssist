M1141006100120
1. Remove condenser fan motor connector.
2. Start the engine and run it at idle.
FAN CONTROLLER
3. Turn the A/C switch to ON and maintain the coolant
temperature at 80°C (176°F) or less.
4. When measuring the voltage between the fan controller-side
connector terminals, check that the value changes
repeatedly as indicated by (1) − (3) below.
(1) 0 V
(2) 8.2 ± 2.6 V
AC000274AC (3) Battery positive voltage ± 2.6 V
5. If the voltage does not repeatedly change as indicated,
replace the radiator fan motor and the fan controller
assembly.
