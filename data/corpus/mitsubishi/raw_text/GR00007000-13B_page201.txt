STEP 1. Check connector B-24 at left bank heated oxygen
sensor (front) and connector C-62 at ECM <M/T> or
connector C-59 at PCM <A/T> for damage.

YES : Go to Step 2.
NO : Repair or replace it. Refer to GROUP 00E, Harness
Connector Inspection P.00E-2. Then go to Step 3.
