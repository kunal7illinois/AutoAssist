Q:Are there any leaks?
AC000198
There are leaks at the fuel pump module or the fuel tank
differential pressure sensor section. : After reassemble
the leaked parts, check again that there are no leaks.
Then reinstall the fuel tank. (Refer to GROUP 13C,
Fuel Tank P.13C-11.) Then go to Step 26.
There are leaks at the fuel tank. : Replace the fuel tank,
reinstall the fuel tank. (Refer to GROUP 13C, Fuel
Tank P.13C-11.) Then go to Step 26.
There are no leaks. : Then go to Step 15.
STEP 15. Visually check for cracks in the fuel tank filler
neck.
(1) Visually check for cracks in the fuel tank filler neck.
Q:Is the fuel filler neck assembly in good condition?
YES : Reinstall the fuel tank and fuel filler neck assembly.
(Refer to GROUP 13C, Fuel Tank P.13C-11.) Then go
to Step 26.
NO : Replace the fuel tank filler neck assembly and
reinstall the fuel tank. (Refer to GROUP 13C, Fuel
Tank P.13C-11.) Then go to Step 26.
