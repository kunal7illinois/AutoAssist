AUTOMATIC TRANSAXLE 23A-17
AUTOMATIC TRANSAXLE DIAGNOSIS
INSPECTION USING SCAN TOOL, ROAD TEST
AND DATA LIST
Required Special Tool:
MB991502: Scan Tool (MUT-II)
CAUTION
To prevent damage to scan tool MB991502, always turn the
ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position before con-
necting or disconnecting scan tool.
1. Connect the scan tool to data link connector.
2. Turn the ignition switch to the "ON" position.
3. Carry out inspection by means of the Road Test and Data
List function. If there is an abnormality, check and repair the
chassis harnesses and components. Refer to P.23A-19,
Road Test. Refer to P.23A-323, Data List Reference Table.
4. Re-check using scan tool and confirm that the abnormal

input and output have returned to normal because of the
repairs.

5. Check for and inspect any diagnostic trouble code(s) that

trouble code(s) when finished checking.
6. Turn the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position.
7. Disconnect the scan tool from the data link connector.
8. Start the engine again and do a test drive to confirm that the
problem is eliminated.
