QUARTER WINDOW GLASS AND REGULATOR ASSEMBLY
STEP 3. Check the power window main switch continuity.
(1) Exchange the power window main switch with one in good
condition to check that the quarter window glass operates
properly. (Refer to P.42-54.)
(2) If normal operates can not be seen, replace the convertible
top control module. (Refer to P.42-138.)
Q:Is the power window main switch damaged?
YES : Replace it, then go to Step 4.
NO : Go to Step 4.
STEP 4. Check symptoms.
Q:Does the quarter windows open and close normally?
YES : This diagnosis is complete.
NO : Return to Step 1.
