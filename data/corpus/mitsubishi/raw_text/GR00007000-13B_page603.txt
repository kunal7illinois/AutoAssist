• Malfunction of the A/C control system.

• ECM <M/T> or PCM <A/T> failed.
DIAGNOSIS
STEP 1. Check connector C-58 at ECM <M/T> or connector
C-55 at PCM <A/T> for damage.
Q:Is the connector in good condition?
YES : Go to Step 2.
NO : Repair or replace it. Refer to GROUP 00E, Harness
Connector Inspection P.00E-2. Then confirm that the
malfunction symptom is eliminated.

connector C-58 <M/T> or PCM harness side connector C-
55 <A/T>.

measure at the harness side.
(2) Start the engine and run at idle.
