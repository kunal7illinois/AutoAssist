Use care not to let engine oil enter the fuel rail.

>>C<< FUEL PRESSURE REGULATOR INSTALLATION
CAUTION
Do not let engine oil enter the fuel rail.
1. Apply clean engine oil to the O-ring.
2. Insert the fuel pressure regulator into the fuel rail.
3. Make sure the regulator rotates smoothly. If not, remove the
fuel pressure regulator to check the O-ring for damage, and
replace the O-ring if necessary. Then reinsert the fuel
pressure regulator and check that it rotates smoothly.
4. Tighten the two bolts to the specified torque.
