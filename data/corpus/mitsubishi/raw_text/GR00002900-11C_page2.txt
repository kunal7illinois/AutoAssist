The 6G72 (3.0L) engine is a six-cylinder engine. The cylinder numbers are assigned as 1 − 3 − 5 for the right
bank and 2 − 4 − 6 for the left bank from the front of the engine (timing belt side). This engine is fired in the
order of the 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6 cylinders.
