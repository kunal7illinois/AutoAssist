MULTIPORT FUEL INJECTION (MFI) DIAGNOSIS
STEP 10. Check for harness damage between manifold
differential pressure sensor connector B-04 terminal 3 and
ECM connector C-58 terminal 42 <M/T> or PCM connector
C-55 terminal 46 <A/T>.
Q:Is the harness wire in good condition?
CONNECTOR : B-04
YES : Go to Step 11.
NO : Repair it. Then go to Step 12.
