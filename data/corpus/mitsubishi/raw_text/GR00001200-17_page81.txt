Auto-cruise Control Setting
1. Switch "ON" the main switch.
2. Drive at the desired speed, above approximately 40 km/h.
(25 mph)
3. Push the auto-cruise control switch in the direction of arrow.
4. Check to be sure that when the switch is released the speed
is the desired constant speed.
NOTE: If the vehicles speed decreases to approximately 15
km/h (9 mph) below the set speed because of climbing a hill
for example, the auto-cruise control will be cancelled.
