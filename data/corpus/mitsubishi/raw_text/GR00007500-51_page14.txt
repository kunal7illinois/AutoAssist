REAR PILLAR GARNISH INSTALLATION
1. Tear off the double-sided tape backing paper.
NOTE: If you attach the adhesive tape to the edge of the
backing paper, if will be easy to tear off.
2. Install the rear pillar garnish.
NOTE: If the double-sided tape is difficult to affix in cold
temperature, etc., warm the bonding surfaces of the body
ADHESIVE TAPE
and rear pillar garnish to about 40 − 60°C (104 − 140°F)
BACKING PAPER before affixing the tape.
3. Firmly press in the rear pillar garnish.
