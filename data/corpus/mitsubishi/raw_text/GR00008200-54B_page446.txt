STEP 10. Check the input signal by using "DATA LIST" of
the SWS monitor.
Satisfy the following conditions to check the driver’s and front
passenger’s door switches.
• Driver’s door: Open (driver’s door switch is on)
However, the door should be closed when checking the
front passenger’s door switch.
• Front passenger’s door: Open (front passenger’s door
switch is on)
However, the door should be closed when checking the
driver’s door switch.

MB991502.
