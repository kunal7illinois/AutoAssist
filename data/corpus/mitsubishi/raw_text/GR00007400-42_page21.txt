.<<A>> QUARTER WINDOW GLASS REMOVAL
1. Apply cloth tape to protect quarter window glass.
CLOTH TAPE AC002057AB
CAUTION
Putting glass adhesive knife too deeply into windshield
adhesive may damage quarter window glass.
2. Use glass adhesive knife to cut away adhesive.
Keep glass adhesive knife at right angles with body flange
(from inside the vehicle), and put the blade at body flange.
Then cut away adhesive along the body flange.
NOTE: If glass adhesive knife does not reach the body
flange, refer to WINDSHIELD P.42-14.

4. Use special tool MB990480 to remove the quarter window
glass.
