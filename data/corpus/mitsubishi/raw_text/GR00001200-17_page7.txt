AUTO-CRUISE CONTROL
AUTO-CRUISE CONTROL
GENERAL DESCRIPTION
M1172000100151
By using the auto-cruise control, the driver can drive
at the desired speed [in a range of approximately 40
− 200 km/h (25 − 124 mph)] without depressing the
accelerator pedal.
