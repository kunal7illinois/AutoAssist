RADIO AND TAPE PLAYER
STEP 43. Check the DIN cable between amplifier and radio
and CD player.
Q: Is the DIN cable in good condition?
YES : Repair or replace the amplifier or radio and CD
player. The tweeter (LH) should sound.
NO : Repair or replace the DIN cable. The tweeter (LH)
should sound.
STEP 44. Check the tweeter (RH).
(1) Remove the tweeter (RH).
(2) Check that the tweeter (RH) generates noise when a five-
volt voltage is applied on the tweeter (RH) terminal.
Q:Is the tweeter (RH) generating noise?
YES : Go to Step 45.
NO : Replace the tweeter (RH). The tweeter (RH) should
5V sound.
