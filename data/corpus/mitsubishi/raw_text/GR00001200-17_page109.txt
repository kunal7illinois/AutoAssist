ENGINE AND EMISSION CONTROL 17-109
EMISSION CONTROL
EGR SOLENOID CHECK
M1173003100120
1. Disconnect the vacuum hose from the EGR solenoid.
NOTE: When disconnecting the vacuum hose, always make
sure that it can be reconnected at its original position.
2. Disconnect the harness connector.
3. Connect a hand vacuum pump to nipple (A) of the EGR
A solenoid. (Refer to the illustration at left.)
4. As described in the chart below, check airtightness by
applying a vacuum with voltage applied directly from the
battery to the EGR solenoid and without applying voltage.
B BATTERY POSITIVE NORMAL CONDITION
VOLTAGE
Not applied Vacuum leaks
AKX00351AB Applied Vacuum maintained
5. Measure the resistance between the terminals of the EGR
solenoid.
Standard value: 29 − 35 Ω [at 20°C (68°F)]
6. Replace the solenoid if resistance is out of specification.
