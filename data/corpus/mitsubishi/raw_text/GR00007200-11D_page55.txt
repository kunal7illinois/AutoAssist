>>F<< OIL SEAL CASE ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
1. Apply sealant Mitsubishi Genuine Part number MD970389
or equivalent to the gasket surface of oil seal case.
NOTE: Be sure to install the case quickly while the sealant is
wet (within 15 minutes).
2. Apply engine oil to the lip of the oil seal, and then install the
oil seal case onto the cylinder block.
NOTE: After installation, keep the sealed area away from
the oil for approximately one hour.
