1. Check to be sure that the interlock switch is as shown in the
illustration when the clutch pedal is depressed at its full
stroke 130 mm (5.1 inches). If not at the specified
dimension, loosen the clutch interlock switch 1/4 turn
counterclockwise. Then slide the switch to the specified
dimension, and turn the switch 1/4 turn clockwise to lock.

2. Connect an ohmmeter to the interlock switch connector, and
then check for continuity when the clutch pedal is fully
depressed and when it is released outward. If the interlock
switch is not as it should be, replace it.
