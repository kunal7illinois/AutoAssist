SUNROOF ASSEMBLY
>>B<< SUNROOF MOTOR ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
If the sunroof motor assembly is out of its fully closed position,
adjust the motor position by the following steps before install-
ment.
1. Remove the sunroof motor assembly without disconnecting
the sunroof motor assembly connector.
2. Close the sunroof lid glass fully.
3. Push the sunroof close switch until the sunroof motor
assembly stops turning. Repeat this operation until the
motor does not turn at all. Then install the motor assembly to
the roof.
NOTE: When the sunroof motor assembly does not turn at
all, the sunroof-ECU will return to its default condition.
