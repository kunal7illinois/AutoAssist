STEP 27. Check the DIN cable between amplifier and radio
and CD player.
Q:Is the DIN cable in good condition?
YES : Repair or replace the amplifier or radio and CD
player. The front door speaker (RH) should sound.
NO : Repair or replace the DIN cable. The front door
speaker (RH) should sound.
STEP 28. Check the rear speaker (LH).
(1) Remove the rear speaker (LH).
(2) Check that the rear speaker (LH) generates noise when a
five-volt voltage is applied on the rear speaker (LH)
terminal.
Q:Is the rear speaker (LH) generating noise?
YES : Go to Step 29.
5V NO : Replace the rear speaker (LH). The rear speaker (LH)
should sound.
