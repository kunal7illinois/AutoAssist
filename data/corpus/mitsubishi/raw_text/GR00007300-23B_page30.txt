ASSEMBLY
CAUTION
• Do not reuse the gasket, O-ring, oil seal. Always
replace with a new one when assembling.
• Do not use grease, use petroleum jelly (i.e. Vaseline).
• Apply ATF to friction components, rotating parts, and
sliding parts before installation. Immerse a new clutch
discs or brake discs in ATF for at least two hours
before assembling them.
• When replacing a bushing, replace the assembly which
it belongs to.
• Do not use cloth gloves or shop towels during assem-
bly. Use nylon cloth or other lint-free material.
