DIAGNOSIS
STEP 1. Check the headlight (high beam) operation.
Q:Do the headlights illuminate at high beam normally?
YES : Go to Step 2.
NO : Refer to Inspection Procedure J-3 "Headlights (high-
beam) do not illuminate normally P.54B-274."
STEP 2. Replace the ECU.
(1) Replace the ETACS-ECU.
(2) The headlights should flash at high beam when the theft-
alarm system is triggered.
Q:Do the headlights flash at high beam when the theft-
alarm system is triggered?
YES : No action to be taken.
NO : Replace the front-ECU. The headlights should flash at
high beam when the theft-alarm system is triggered.
