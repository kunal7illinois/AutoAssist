22A-14 MANUAL TRANSAXLE
TRANSAXLE CONTROL
>>C<< SHIFT CABLE AND SELECT CABLE ASSEMBLY/
SHIFT CABLE CONNECTION/SELECT CABLE
CONNECTION
1. Set the transaxle side shift lever and the passenger
compartment side shift lever to the neutral position.
2. For the transaxle side, the white and yellow paint marks on
the shift and select cable ends should face the snap pins.
3. Move the shift lever to all positions and check that the
operation is smooth.
