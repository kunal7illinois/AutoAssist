DTC SET CONDITIONS
If the resistance value for a solenoid valve circuit is
greater than 3.5 Ω for 4 seconds or less than 2.6 Ω
for 4 seconds, it is judged that there is a short circuit
or an open circuit in the solenoid valve and the diag-
nostic trouble code number "33" is displayed. The
transmission is locked into 3rd gear as a fail-safe
measure, and the "N" range light flashes once per
second.
