Required Special Tools:
MB991502: Scan Tool (MUT-II)
CAUTION
To prevent damage to scan tool MB991502, always turn the
ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position before con-
necting or disconnecting scan tool MB991502.
NOTE: If the battery positive voltage is low, diagnostic trouble
codes will not be output. Check the battery if the scan tool can-
not display.
NOTE: If the battery is disconnected or if the powertrain con-
trol module connector is disconnected, the diagnostic trouble
codes will be erased. Do not disconnect the battery or power-
train control module before the diagnostic trouble codes have
been read.
1. Connect the scan tool to the data link connector.
2. Turn the ignition switch to the "ON" position.
3. Record the diagnostic trouble codes for A/T.
4. Refer to the Diagnostic Trouble Code Chart.
5. Turn the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) and then back
to "ON" again.
6. Erase the diagnostic trouble code by selecting DTC erase
