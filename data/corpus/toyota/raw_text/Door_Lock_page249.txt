10. REMOVE FRONT DOOR LOCK ASSEMBLY
(a) Disconnect the connector.
(b) Using a "torx" socket wrench (T30), remove the 3
screws.
(c) Slide the front door lock assembly downward and
pull out the front door lock open rod from the outside
handle frame. Remove the front door lock assembly
and cables as a unit.
(d) Remove the front door lock open rod from the front
door lock assembly.
(e) Remove the door lock wiring harness seal from the
front door lock assembly.
B136428
(f) Remove the front door lock remote control cable
assembly.
