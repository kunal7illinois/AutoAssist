2. Automatic Tensioner
(cid:1) The automatic tensioner consists of an idler pulley, an arm, and a tensioner. The idler pulley maintains belt
tension by the force of the spring that is located in the tensioner.
(cid:1) Due to the different suppliers used, the tensioner comes in two types, although their basic operation remain
the same and they are interchangeable.
