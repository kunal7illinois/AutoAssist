BRAKE CONTROL – VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM (for BOSCH Made)
2. SENSOR SIGNAL CHECK BY TEST MODE (SIGNAL
CHECK) (INTELLIGENT TESTER)
HINT:
If the ignition switch is turned from on (IG) to on (ACC) or
off during Test Mode (signal check), DTCs recorded
during the signal check function will be erased.
(a) Procedure to enter Test Mode.
Intelligent Tester
(1) Turn the ignition switch off.
(2) Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.
(3) Check that the steering wheel is centered and
move the shift lever to the P position (Automatic
Transaxle) or apply the parking brake (Manual
Transaxle).
(4) Turn the ignition switch on (IG).
