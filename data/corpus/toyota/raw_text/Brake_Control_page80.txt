BC–80
BRAKE CONTROL – ANTI-LOCK BRAKE SYSTEM
ABS Warning Light Remains ON
DESCRIPTION
The skid control ECU sends the indicator signals to the combination meter assembly via the CAN
communication system.
If any of the following is detected, the ABS warning light remains on.
• The skid control ECU connectors are disconnected from the skid control ECU.
• There is a malfunction in the skid control ECU internal circuit.
• There is an open in the wire harness between the combination meter and the skid control ECU.
• The ABS and/or EBD is defective.
HINT:
The intelligent tester may not be used when the skid control ECU is abnormal.
