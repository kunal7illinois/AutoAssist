INTERIOR – ROOF HEADLINING
18. INSTALL ROOF HEADLINING ASSEMBLY (w/ Sliding
Roof)
(a) Pull the roof headlining assembly into the vehicle
through the rear left door.
(b) Engage the hook, 4 clips, and 2 fasteners.
(c) Connect the sliding roof drive gear connector.
(d) Connect the antenna cord sub-assembly connector
and engage the clamp to the rear pillar RH.
(e) Connect the antenna cord sub-assembly connector
and engage each clamp to the front pillar RH.
B139561
(f) Install the radio setting condenser to the front pillar
RH with the bolt.
(g) Connect the No. 1 roof wire connector and engage
each clamp to the front pillar LH.
