2. Air Cleaner
(cid:1) A nonwoven, full-fabric type air cleaner element is used.
(cid:1) A carbon filter, which adsorbs the HC that accumulates in the intake system when the engine is stopped,
is used in the air cleaner case in order to reduce evaporative emissions. This filter is maintenance-free.
(cid:1) Along with the use of the air intake control system, an air intake control valve is provided on the air cleaner
case.
(cid:1) Resonators have been provided to reduce the amount of intake air sound.

3. Throttle Body
(cid:1) A link-less type throttle body in which the throttle position sensor and the throttle control motor are
integrated is used. It realizes excellent throttle valve control. For details, see page EG-118.
(cid:1) In the throttle control motor, a DC motor with excellent response and minimal power consumption is used.
The ECM performs the duty ratio control of the direction and the amperage of the current that flows to
the throttle control motor in order to regulate the throttle valve angle.
