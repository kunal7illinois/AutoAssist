7. INSTALL NO. 3 BRAKE PISTON
(a) Coat the contact surface of the transaxle case with
ATF.
(b) Coat the lip seal of the No. 3 brake piston with ATF.
C137432
(c) Temporarily install the pawl stopper plate to the
transaxle case with the 2 bolts.
C137431
(d) Press the return spring installation surface to install
the No. 3 brake piston to the transaxle case.
NOTICE:
• Make sure that the lip of the No. 3 brake
piston is not twisted and does not get caught
in the transaxle case.
• After installing, make sure that the
protrusions on the No. 3 brake piston and the
grooves on the pawl stopper plate are
aligned.
C136679
(e) Install the return spring to the transaxle case.
