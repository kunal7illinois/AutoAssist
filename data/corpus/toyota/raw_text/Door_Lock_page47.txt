DL–47
DOOR LOCK – POWER DOOR LOCK CONTROL SYSTEM
Luggage Compartment Door Courtesy Switch Circuit
DESCRIPTION
The door courtesy switch turns on when the door is open and turns off when the door is closed.
The main body ECU detects the condition of the door courtesy switch.
