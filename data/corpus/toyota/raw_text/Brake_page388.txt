BC–377
BRAKE CONTROL – VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM (for BOSCH Made)
DTC C1300/62 Skid Control ECU Malfunction
DESCRIPTION
The skid control ECU is housed in the brake actuator assembly.
The skid control ECU judges the vehicle driving condition based on the signals from each sensor, and
controls the brake actuator assembly.
DTC No. DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
C1300/62 Internal failure of the skid control ECU control unit Brake actuator assembly (skid control ECU)
