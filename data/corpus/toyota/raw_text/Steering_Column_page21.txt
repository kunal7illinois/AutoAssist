STEERING COLUMN – ELECTRIC STEERING LOCK
2. ACTIVE TEST
HINT:
Performing the ACTIVE TEST is one of the methods to
save labor time. It is possible to display the DATA LIST
during the ACTIVE TEST.
(a) Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.
(b) Turn the engine switch on (IG) and turn the
intelligent tester on.
(c) Following the display on the intelligent tester,
perform the ACTIVE TEST.
