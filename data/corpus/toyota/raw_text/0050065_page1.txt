ES–393
2AZ-FE ENGINE CONTROL SYSTEM – MASS AIR FLOW METER
REMOVAL
1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY
TERMINAL
CAUTION:
Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the
cable from the negative (-) battery terminal to
prevent airbag and seat belt pretensioner activation.
2. REMOVE MASS AIR FLOW METER
(a) Disconnect the mass air flow meter connector.
(b) Remove the 2 screws and mass air flow meter.
ES
A124546E01
INSTALLATION
1. INSTALL MASS AIR FLOW METER
(a) Install the mass air flow meter with the 2 screws.
NOTICE:
Make sure that the O-ring is not cracked or
jammed when installing it.
(b) Connect the mass air flow meter connector.
2. CONNECT CABLE TO BATTERY NEGATIVE
TERMINAL
