TIRE AND WHEEL – TIRE PRESSURE WARNING SYSTEM
Tire Pressure Warning Reset Switch Circuit
DESCRIPTION
Receiving a signal from the tire pressure warning reset switch, the tire pressure warning ECU and
receiver indicates the initialization of the pressure warning system.
