12. REMOVE REAR DOOR OUTSIDE STRIPE
(a) Using a heat light, heat the rear door outside stripe.
Heating temperature
Item Temperature
Vehicle Body 40 to 60°C (104 to 140°F)
NOTICE:
Do not heat the vehicle body excessively.
(b) Pull back an edge of the rear door outside stripe and
pull it parallel to the vehicle body to pull it off.
