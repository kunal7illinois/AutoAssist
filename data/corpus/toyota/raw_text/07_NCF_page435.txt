BODY ELECTRICAL - SMART KEY SYSTEM BE-101
Pattern B: OFF (cid:1) ACC (cid:1) IG ON (cid:1) OFF
1) OFF (cid:1) ACC
Step System Operation
a) The driver has the key in their possession and enters the vehicle.
When the driver presses the engine switch once with the following conditions satisfied, the
main body ECU recognizes the engine switch signal and transmits the key certification
request to the certification ECU.
b)
(cid:3) Shift position is P.
(cid:3) Brake pedal is not depressed.
(cid:3) Power source is OFF.
Due to the brake pedal not being depressed, the main body ECU will turn ON the amber
c)
indicator light of the engine switch.
The rest of the system operation is the same as d) to h) in pattern A. For details, see page
d)
BE-98.

Step System Operation
