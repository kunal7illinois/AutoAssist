SUSPENSION – REAR SHOCK ABSORBER
INSTALLATION
1. INSTALL REAR SHOCK ABSORBER WITH COIL
SPRING
(a) Install the rear shock absorber with coil spring to the
rear axle carrier assembly and insert the 2 bolts
from the rear of the vehicle.
(b) Slowly jack up the vehicle using a wooden block
and install the rear shock absorber with coil spring
(upper side) to the vehicle.
