SE–18
SEAT – FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY (for Manual Seat)
DISASSEMBLY
NOTICE:
Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the seat frame
and adjuster may injure your hands.
1. REMOVE VERTICAL ADJUSTER COVER LH (for
Driver Seat)
(a) Using a screwdriver, disengage the 2 claws and
remove the vertical adjuster cover LH.
HINT:
Tape the screwdriver tip before use.
SE
B139103
2. REMOVE VERTICAL ADJUSTING HANDLE NO. 2 (for
Driver Seat)
(a) Remove the 2 screws and vertical adjusting handle.
B139104
3. REMOVE RECLINING ADJUSTER RELEASE
HANDLE
(a) Lift the reclining adjuster release handle.
(b) Using a screwdriver, disengage the claw and
remove the reclining adjuster release handle.
HINT:
Tape the screwdriver tip before use.
B139105
4. REMOVE FRONT SEAT CUSHION SHIELD
(a) Disengage the 2 hooks.
