BLUETOOTH HANDS-FREE SYSTEM
(cid:4)DESCRIPTION
(cid:3) Bluetooth is a short-distance, high-speed wireless data communication system that uses the 2.4 GHz
frequency band prescribed by the Bluetooth SIG (Special Interest Group).
(cid:3) This system enables drivers to place or receive phone calls using a cellular phone without releasing their
hands from the steering wheel.
(cid:3) The Bluetooth hands-free system is installed on both the ’07 Camry with the navigation with AV system
and the ’07 Camry without the navigation with AV system as optional equipment*.
(cid:3) The Bluetooth hands-free system of the ’07 Camry with the navigation with AV system can be operated
by touching icons indicated on the radio and player with display.
(cid:3) The Bluetooth hands-free system of the ’07 Camry without the navigation with AV system can be operated
by turning or pressing the control knob of the audio head unit.
