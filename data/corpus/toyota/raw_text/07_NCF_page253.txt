1. General
(cid:3) Models with the brake control system consisting of ABS with EBD and Brake Assist use a mechanical
type brake assist mechanism, which is integrated into the brake booster.
(cid:3) Models with the brake control system consisting of ABS with EBD, Brake Assist, TRAC and VSC use
an electrical type brake assist mechanism, which effects brake assist control through the brake actuator.
(cid:3) Due to the difference of the production site, the brake actuator, used on ABS with EBD, Brake Assist,
TRAC and VSC models, is supplied from two different suppliers.

Service Tip
(cid:3) To ensure the performance and reliability of the plunger type master cylinder, it must not be
disassembled. If it malfunctions, replace the entire assembly.
(cid:3) Before removing the plunger type master cylinder from the brake booster, discharge the vacuum
from the brake booster. Otherwise, the piston of the master cylinder may be left inside the brake
booster.
For details, see the 2007 Camry Repair Manual (Pub. No. RM0250U).
