EG-34 ENGINE - 2AZ-FE ENGINE
System Outline
Engine Immobilizer Prohibits fuel delivery and ignition if an attempt is made to start the engine
with an invalid key.
Diagnosis When the ECM detects a malfunction, the ECM diagnoses and memorizes
[See page EG-72] the failed section.
Fail-Safe When the ECM detects a malfunction, the ECM stops or controls the engine
[See page EG-73] according to the data already stored in the memory.
