INSPECTION PROCEDURE
1 READ VALUE OF DATA LIST (STP SIGNAL)
HINT:
According to the DATA LIST displayed on the intelligent
tester, you can read the value of switches, sensors, actuators
and other items without removing any parts. Reading the
DATA LIST as the first step in troubleshooting is one method
to save labor time.
(a) Turn the ignition switch off.
(b) Connect the intelligent tester together with the CAN VIM
(Controller Area Network Vehicle Interface Module) to
the DLC3.
(c) Turn the ignition switch on (IG).
(d) Turn on the tester.
(e) Enter the following items: "DIAGNOSIS / OBD/MOBD /
ECT / DATA LIST".
(f) According to the display on the tester, read "DATA LIST".
