Service Tip
Spray type washer nozzles cannot be adjusted because of their structure. Do not attempt to adjust the
nozzles as it could damage them.
If adjustment is necessary, adjust the nozzles after replacing them with those selected from five part
numbers with different spray angles. For details, see the 2007 Camry Repair Manual (Pub. No.
RM0250U).
