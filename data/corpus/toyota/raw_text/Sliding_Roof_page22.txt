RF–22
SLIDING ROOF – SLIDING ROOF SYSTEM
DTC B2342 Switch Failure
DESCRIPTION
This DTC is output when the sliding roof drive gear sub-assembly (sliding roof control ECU) detects that
the OPEN, CLOSE, UP or DOWN switch in the roof console box assembly (sliding roof control switch) is
stuck for 30 seconds or more.
