BRAKE CONTROL – VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM (for ADVICS Made)
DTC C1231/31 Steering Angle Sensor Circuit Malfunction
DESCRIPTION
The steering angle sensor signal is sent to the skid control ECU via the CAN communication system.
When there is a malfunction in the CAN communication system, it will be detected by the steering angle
sensor zero point malfunction diagnostic function.
