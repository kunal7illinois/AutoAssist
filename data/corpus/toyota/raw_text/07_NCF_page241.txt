CHASSIS - U660E AUTOMATIC TRANSAXLE CH-77
(cid:4)SHIFT CONTROL MECHANISM
1. General
(cid:3) A gate type shift lever is used in conjunction with the 6-speed automatic transaxle. With the gate type lever,
the shift lever button and the overdrive switch of the straight type shift lever are discontinued. Similar
functions are achieved through a single-shift operation (fore-aft and side-to-side).
(cid:3) The shift control cable with a length adjustment mechanism is used.
(cid:3) Shift pattern is provided with the S mode position on the side of the D position.
(cid:3) A shift lock system is used.
: The shift lever can be moved only
with the power source IG-ON and
the brake pedal depressed.
: The shift lever can be moved at
anytime.

Service Tip
The shift control cable is fixed by the lock piece of the adjustment mechanism. Adjustment of the shift
control cable is possible by releasing the lock piece from the cable. For details, see the 2007 Camry
Repair Manual (Pub. No. RM0250U).
