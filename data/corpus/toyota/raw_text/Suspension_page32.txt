SP–30
SUSPENSION – FRONT LOWER BALL JOINT
INSTALLATION
1. INSTALL FRONT LOWER BALL JOINT
(a) Install the front lower ball joint to the steering
knuckle with the nut.
Torque:123 N*m (1,250 kgf*cm, 91 ft.*lbf)
NOTICE:
Prevent oil from adhering to the screw and
tapered parts.
(b) Install a new cotter pin.
NOTICE:
If the holes for the cotter pin are not aligned,
tighten the nut further up to 60°.
