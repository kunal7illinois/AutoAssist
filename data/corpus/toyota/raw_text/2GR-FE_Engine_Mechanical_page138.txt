41. INSTALL IDLE SPROCKET ASSEMBLY
(a) Apply a light coat of engine oil to the rotating surface
of the No. 1 idle gear shaft.
(b) Temporarily install the No. 1 idle gear shaft and idle
sprocket with the No. 2 idle gear shaft while aligning
the knock pin of the No. 1 idle gear with the knock
pin groove of the cylinder block.
NOTICE: EM
Knock Pin Be careful of the idle gear direction.
HINT:
Check that no foreign objects are on the idle gear
shafts No. 1 and No. 2.
(c) Using a 10 mm hexagon wrench, tighten the No. 2
idle gear shaft.
Torque:60 N*m (612 kgf*cm, 44 ft.*lbf)
A132222E01 HINT:
After installing the idle sprocket assembly, check
that the idle sprocket turns smoothly.
