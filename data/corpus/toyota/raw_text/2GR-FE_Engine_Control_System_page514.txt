2GR-FE ENGINE CONTROL SYSTEM – THROTTLE BODY
ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION
1. INSPECTION THROTTLE BODY
(a) Inspect the throttle control motor for operating
sounds.
(1) Turn the ignition switch on (IG).
(2) When turning the accelerator pedal position
sensor lever, check for running sounds of the
motor. The motor should be running smoothly
without friction sounds.
