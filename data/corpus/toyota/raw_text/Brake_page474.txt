BRAKE – BRAKE SYSTEM
• Disconnect and connect the flexible hose and brake
tube:
Separating: (a) Hold the flexible hose with a wrench and
disconnect the brake tube with a union nut wrench
without deforming the tube.
(b) Remove the clip.
(c) Install a new clip.
(d) Connect the brake tube with a union nut wrench
without deforming the tube.
Connecting:
BR
C102779E06
• Connect the brake tube and way:
(a) Support the way to prevent deformation of the
brake tube and connect the brake tube to the way
with a union nut wrench.
(b) Support the way to prevent deformation of the
brake tube and install the bolt to fix the way to the
body.
