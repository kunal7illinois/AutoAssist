DESCRIPTION
The front passenger side - side squib circuit consists of the center airbag sensor assembly and the front
seat side airbag assembly RH.
The circuit instructs the SRS to deploy when deployment conditions are met.
These DTCs are recorded when a malfunction is detected in the front passenger side - side squib circuit.
