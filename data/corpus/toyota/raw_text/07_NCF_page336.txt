(cid:1)INTERNAL TRUNK RELEASE HANDLE
In case a person is inadvertently locked inside the trunk and needs to get out, an internal trunk release handle
is included inside the luggage room.
The handle is made of phosphorescent plastic, so that it is visible in the luggage room for a while even after
the luggage room door has been closed.

CRS (Child Restrain System) lower anchorage for securing child seats has been provided behind the seat
