REMOVAL

2. REMOVE FRONT WHEEL OPENING EXTENSION
PAD LH (See page ED-6)
3. REMOVE FRONT FENDER LINER (See page ED-6)

(a) Disconnect the front speed sensor connector and
clamp.
Clamp
