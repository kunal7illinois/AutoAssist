DL–246
DOOR LOCK – LUGGAGE COMPARTMENT DOOR OPENER OUTER SWITCH
REMOVAL
1. REMOVE LUGGAGE COMPARTMENT DOOR COVER
(See page ET-61)
2. REMOVE LUGGAGE DOOR OPENING SWITCH
ASSEMBLY
(a) Disconnect the connector.
(b) Remove the 2 screws and the luggage door opening
switch assembly.
INSPECTION
1. INSPECT LUGGAGE KEY ELECTRICAL SWITCH
(a) Disconnect the switch connector.
(b) Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in
the table below.
