BODY ELECTRICAL - SMART KEY SYSTEM BE-95
5. Construction and Operation
Engine Switch
The engine switch consists of a momentary type switch, two color (Amber, Green) LEDs, and transponder
key amplifier.
(cid:3) The amber and green LEDs are for the indicator light.
(cid:3) The driver can determine the present power source and check whether the engine can start or not in
accordance with the illumination state of the indicator light.
(cid:3) When the main body ECU detects an abnormality with the smart key system, it makes the amber indicator
light flash. If the engine is stopped in this state, it might not be possible to restart it.
