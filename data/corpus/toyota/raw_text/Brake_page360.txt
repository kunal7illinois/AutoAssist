BC–349
BRAKE CONTROL – VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM (for BOSCH Made)
DTC C1201/51 Engine Control System Malfunction
DESCRIPTION
If a malfunction in the engine control system is detected, the operations of VSC and TRAC are prohibited
by the fail safe function (See page BC-306).
When the signals from the engine are input normally, the fail safe is cancelled and the DTC is not stored.
