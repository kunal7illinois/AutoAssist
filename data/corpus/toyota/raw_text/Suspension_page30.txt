SP–28
SUSPENSION – FRONT LOWER BALL JOINT
REMOVAL
HINT:
• Use the same procedures for the RH side and the LH side.
• The procedures listed below are for the LH side.
1. REMOVE FRONT WHEEL
2. REMOVE FRONT AXLE HUB NUT (See page DS-7)
3. SEPARATE FRONT SPEED SENSOR (See page DS-7)
4. SEPARATE FRONT DISC BRAKE CALIPER
ASSEMBLY (See page AH-6)
5. REMOVE FRONT DISC (See page BR-49)
6. SEPARATE TIE ROD END SUB-ASSEMBLY (See page
SP DS-8)
7. SEPARATE FRONT SUSPENSION LOWER NO. 1
ARM (See page DS-8)
8. REMOVE FRONT AXLE ASSEMBLY (See page DS-8)
9. REMOVE FRONT WHEEL NO. 1 BEARING DUST
DEFLECTOR (See page AH-7)
10. REMOVE FRONT AXLE HUB HOLE SNAP RING (See
page AH-7)
11. REMOVE FRONT AXLE HUB (See page AH-7)
12. REMOVE FRONT DISC BRAKE DUST COVER (See
page AH-8)
13. REMOVE FRONT AXLE HUB BEARING (See page
AH-8)
14. REMOVE FRONT LOWER BALL JOINT
(a) Secure the steering knuckle in a vise using
aluminum plates.
(b) Remove the cotter pin and nut.
(c) Using SST, remove the front lower ball joint.
SST 09628-62011
NOTICE:
• Do not damage the dust cover of the ball
joint.
• Do not damage the steering knuckle.
