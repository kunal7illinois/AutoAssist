35. REMOVE PARKING BRAKE PEDAL ASSEMBLY
(a) Using needle-nose pliers, remove the clip from the
No. 1 parking brake cable assembly.
(b) Remove the bolt, nut, and the No. 1 parking brake
cable clamp.
