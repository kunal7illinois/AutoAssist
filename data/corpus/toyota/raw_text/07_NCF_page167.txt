CHASSIS - CLUTCH CH-3
(cid:2)CLUTCH START SYSTEM
1. General
This system prevents the starter from cranking unless the clutch pedal is depressed. This prevents the engine
from being started in gear, and ensures safety by preventing the vehicle from moving unintentionally.
