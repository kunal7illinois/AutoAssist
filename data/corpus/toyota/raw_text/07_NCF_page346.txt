BE-12 BODY ELECTRICAL - MULTIPLEX COMMUNICATION
(cid:3)AVC-LAN
1. General
The AVC-LAN is used to transmit data only between the audio head unit and the stereo amplifier on models
with the JBL premium sound system.

3. Diagnosis
(cid:3) If a malfunction occurs in the AVC-LAN communication line, the audio head unit stores a DTC
(Diagnostic Trouble Code) in its memory.
(cid:3) The DTC of models with the navigation with AV system can be read on the diagnosis menu display on
the audio head unit.
(cid:3) The DTC of models without the navigation with AV system can be read on the LCD of the audio head unit.
For details, see the 2007 Camry Repair Manual (Pub. No. RM0250U).
