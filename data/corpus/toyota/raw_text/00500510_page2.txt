ES–402
2AZ-FE ENGINE CONTROL SYSTEM – CRANKSHAFT POSITION SENSOR
REMOVAL
1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY
TERMINAL
CAUTION:
Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the
cable from the negative (-) battery terminal to
prevent airbag and seat belt pretensioner activation.
2. REMOVE GENERATOR ASSEMBLY
(a) Remove the generator assembly from the vehicle
(see page CH-9).
3. REMOVE CRANKSHAFT POSITION SENSOR
ES (a) Disconnect the sensor connector.
(b) Remove the connector clamp.
(c) Remove the wire harness from the wire harness
clamp bracket.
(d) Remove the wire harness clamp.
(e) Remove the bolt and sensor.
A097797E01
INSPECTION
1. INSPECT CRANKSHAFT POSITION SENSOR
(a) Measure the resistance of the sensor.
Standard resistance
Tester Connection Specified Condition
1 (NE+) - 2 (NE-) 985 to 1,600 Ω at Cold
1 (NE+) - 2 (NE-) 1,265 to 1,890 Ω at Hot
NOTICE:
A097451E01
Cold and Hot mean the temperature of the coils
themselves. Cold is from -10 to 50°C (14 to
122°F) and Hot is from 50 to 100°C (122 to
212°F).
If the result is not as specified, replace the
crankshaft position sensor.
INSTALLATION
1. INSTALL CRANKSHAFT POSITION SENSOR
NOTICE:
Make sure that the O-ring is not cracked or jammed
when installing it.
(a) Apply a light coat of engine oil to the O-ring of the
sensor.
