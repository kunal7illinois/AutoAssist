DL–70
DOOR LOCK – WIRELESS DOOR LOCK CONTROL SYSTEM (w/ Smart Key System)
Function Operation
The buzzer sounds when LOCK is pressed when any of the doors are ajar. When all the
Door ajar warning doors are closed or when UNLOCK is pressed, the buzzer will stop after it sounds for 10
seconds continuously.
When all the doors are locked, pressing the UNLOCK switch causes the interior light to
Illuminated Entry
come on simultaneously with the unlock operation.
