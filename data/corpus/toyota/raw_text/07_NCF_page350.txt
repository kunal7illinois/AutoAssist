BE-16 BODY ELECTRICAL - POWER DISTRIBUTOR
POWER DISTRIBUTOR
(cid:2)DESCRIPTION
(cid:1) The power distributor is built into the engine room relay block, and uses a small mechanical relay and
semiconductor relay for a compact and lightweight design.
(cid:1) The power distributor has a headlight control function. This function dims the headlights (Hi beam) in
accordance with operation signals from the main body ECU while the daytime running light system is
operating. For details, see page BE-19.
