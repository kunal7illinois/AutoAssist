EXTERIOR – NAME PLATE
2. INSTALL NO. 3 LUGGAGE COMPARTMENT DOOR
NAME PLATE
(a) Clean the vehicle body surface.
(1) Using a heat light, heat the vehicle body
surface.
(2) Remove the double-sided tape from the vehicle
body.
(3) Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with
cleaner.
(b) Clean the name plate. (If reusing the name plate)
(1) Using a heat light, heat the name plate.
(2) Remove the double-sided tape from the name
plate.
(3) Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with
cleaner.
ET
(4) Apply new double-sided tape to the name
plate.
(c) Install the name plate.
(1) Using a heat light, heat the vehicle body and
name plate.
(2) Remove the release paper from the name
plate.
HINT:
After removing the release paper, keep the
exposed adhesive free from foreign matter.
(3) Install the name plate.
