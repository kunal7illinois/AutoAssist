3 REPLACE DOOR CONTROL RECEIVER
(a) Reconnect the E6 main body ECU connector.
(b) Replace the door control receiver with a normal one.
(c) Perform the registration procedures (See page DL-101).
HINT:
If a normally functioning door control receiver is
available, connect it and check if the wireless door lock
function is normal or DTCs are output. If the alternative
receiver functions normally, replace the original door
control receiver.
