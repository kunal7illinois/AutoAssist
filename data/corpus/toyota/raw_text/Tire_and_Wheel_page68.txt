TW–65
TIRE AND WHEEL – TIRE PRESSURE WARNING SYSTEM
DTC C2191/91 Vehicle Speed Signal Error (Test Mode DTC)
DESCRIPTION
The tire pressure warning ECU receives a speed signal from the combination meter. This DTC is stored
upon entering test mode, and cleared when a vehicle speed signal of 12 mph (20 km/h) is detected for 3
seconds or more. This DTC is output only in test mode.

INSPECTION PROCEDURE
NOTICE:
• When replacing the tire pressure warning ECU, read the IDs stored in the ECU using the
intelligent tester and write them down before removal.
• It is necessary to perform initialization (See page TW-18) after registration (See page TW-15) of
the transmitter IDs into the tire pressure warning ECU after the ECU have been replaced.
