ET–34
EXTERIOR – REAR SPOILER
(c) Install the rear spoiler.
(1) Using a heat light, heat the vehicle body and
rear spoiler.
(2) Remove the release paper from the rear
spoiler.
HINT:
After removing the release paper, keep the
exposed adhesive free from foreign matter.
(3) Engage the 4 clips and install the rear spoiler
with the 2 nuts.
