BC–282
BRAKE CONTROL – VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM (for BOSCH Made)
(c) BA (Brake Assist)
The primary purpose of the brake assist system is to
provide auxiliary brake force to assist a driver who
cannot generate a large enough brake force during
emergency braking. This system helps to maximize
the brake performance of the vehicle.
