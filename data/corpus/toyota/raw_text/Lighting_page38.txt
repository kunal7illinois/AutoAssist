LI–38
LIGHTING – LIGHTING SYSTEM
Headlight Relay Circuit
DESCRIPTION
The main body ECU receives a light control switch HEAD signal from the headlight dimmer switch
assembly, and turns the low beam headlights on. The headlight relay is installed in the power distributor
(engine room J/B assembly).
