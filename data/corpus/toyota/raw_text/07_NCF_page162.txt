12. Diagnosis
(cid:1) When the ECM detects a malfunction, the ECM makes a diagnosis and memorizes the failed section.
Furthermore, the MIL (Malfunction Indicator Lamp) in the combination meter illuminates or blinks to
inform the driver.
(cid:1) The ECM will also store the DTC (Diagnostic Trouble Code) of the malfunctions. The DTC can be
accessed by using the hand-held tester.
(cid:1) For details, see the 2007 Camry Repair Manual (Pub. No. RM0250U).
Service Tip

(cid:1) To clear the DTC that is stored in the ECM, use a hand-held tester, disconnect the battery terminal
or remove the EFI No.1 fuse and ETCS fuse for 1 minute or longer.
