DESCRIPTION
The driver side front pretensioner squib circuit consists of the center airbag sensor assembly and the front
seat outer belt assembly LH.
This circuit instructs the SRS to deploy when deployment conditions are met.
These DTCs are recorded when a malfunction is detected in the front pretensioner squib circuit.
