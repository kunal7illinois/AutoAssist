Multiplex Communication System – CAN
System Outline
CAN has two lines as a pair which make communication with operating voltage. CAN has excellent data speed and error
detecting capacity. It consists of vehicle control systems such as engine control module, yaw rate sensor, skid control ECU
with actuator, steering sensor, data link connector 3, A/C amplifier, transmission control ECU, airbag sensor assembly center,
combination meter, certification ECU and main body ECU.
