1. ADJUST FRONT DOOR
HINT:
(cid:1) Before adjusting the door position for vehicles equipped with side airbags and curtain shield
airbags, be sure to disconnect the battery. After adjustment, inspect the SRS warning light, the
side airbag system, and the curtain shield airbag system for normal operation. Then initialize
both airbag systems.
(cid:1) Use the same procedures for the RH side and LH side.
(cid:1) The procedures listed below are for the LH side.
(cid:1) Centering bolts are used to mount the door hinge to the vehicle body and door. The door cannot
be adjusted with the centering bolts on. Substitute the centering bolts with standard bolts (with
washers) when making adjustment.
