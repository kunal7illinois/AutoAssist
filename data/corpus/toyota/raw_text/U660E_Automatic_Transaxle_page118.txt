DESCRIPTION
ATF pressure switch No. 2 is installed in the shift solenoid valve ATF output passage. This switch is used
to identify the malfunctioning part when a fail-safe operation is performed. If the shift solenoid valve
malfunctions when the ATF pressure switch has a malfunction, a fail-safe operation will be performed.
This fail-safe operation is different from the fail-safe operation performed when the solenoid valve
malfunctions and the ATF pressure switch is normal.
