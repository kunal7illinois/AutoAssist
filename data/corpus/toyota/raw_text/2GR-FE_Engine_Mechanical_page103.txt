2GR-FE ENGINE MECHANICAL – ENGINE UNIT
28. INSPECT CAMSHAFT OIL CLEARANCE
(a) Clean the bearing caps, camshaft housing and
camshaft journals.
(b) Place the camshafts on the camshaft housing.
(c) Lay a strip of Plastigage across each of the
camshaft journals.
(d) Install the bearing caps (See page EM-113).
NOTICE:
Do not turn the camshaft.
(e) Remove the bearing caps (See page EM-64).
