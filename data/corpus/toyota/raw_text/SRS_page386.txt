SUPPLEMENTAL RESTRAINT SYSTEM – DRIVER SIDE KNEE AIRBAG ASSEMBLY
(h) Dispose of the driver side knee airbag assembly.
CAUTION:
• The driver side knee airbag assembly
becomes extremely hot when the airbag is
deployed, so do not touch it for at least 30
minutes after deployment.
• Use gloves and safety glasses when handling
a driver side knee airbag assembly with a
deployed airbag.
• Do not apply water, etc. to a driver side knee
C122413
airbag assembly with a deployed airbag.
• Always wash your hands with water after
completing the operation.
(1) Remove the driver side knee airbag assembly
from the tire.
(2) Place the driver side knee airbag assembly in a
plastic bag, tie it tightly and dispose of it as
other general part disposal.
