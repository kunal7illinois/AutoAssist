SP–32
SUSPENSION – FRONT STABILIZER BAR
REMOVAL
1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY
TERMINAL
2. PLACE FRONT WHEELS FACING STRAIGHT AHEAD
3. REMOVE FRONT WHEELS
4. SEPARATE STEERING INTERMEDIATE SHAFT
ASSEMBLY (See page SR-42)
5. SEPARATE TIE ROD END SUB-ASSEMBLY (See page
PS-40)
6. REMOVE FRONT STABILIZER LINK ASSEMBLY LH
(a) Remove the 2 nuts and the front stabilizer link
assembly LH.
SP
HINT:
If the ball joint turns together with the nut, use a
hexagon wrench (6 mm) to hold the stud.
C132425
7. REMOVE FRONT STABILIZER LINK ASSEMBLY RH
(a) Remove the 2 nuts and the front stabilizer link
assembly RH.
HINT:
If the ball joint turns together with the nut, use a
hexagon wrench (6 mm) to hold the stud.
8. REMOVE ENGINE ASSEMBLY WITH TRANSAXLE
HINT:
Refer to the instructions for removal of the engine
C139012 assembly (See page EM-100 for 2AZ-FE, EM-29 for
2GR-FE).
9. REMOVE FRONT NO. 1 STABILIZER BRACKET LH
(a) Remove the 2 bolts and the front No. 1 stabilizer
bracket LH from the front frame assembly.
C132433
