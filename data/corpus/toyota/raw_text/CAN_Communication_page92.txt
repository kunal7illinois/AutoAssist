CA–92
CAN COMMUNICATION – CAN COMMUNICATION SYSTEM
Short to B+ in CAN Bus Line
DESCRIPTION
A short to B+ is suspected in the CAN bus wire when the resistance between terminals 6 (CANH) and 16
(BAT), or terminals 14 (CANL) and 16 (BAT) of the DLC3 is below 6 kΩ.
Symptom Trouble Area
• Short to B+ in CAN bus wire
• Brake actuator assembly (Skid control ECU)
• Steering angle sensor
• Yaw rate sensor
