7 CHECK FOR SPARKS AND IGNITION
(a) Remove the ignition coil from the cylinder head.
(b) Install the spark plug onto the ignition coil.
(c) Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.
(d) Turn the ignition switch ON and turn the tester ON.
(e) Select the following menu items: DIAGNOSIS /
ENHANCED OBD II / ACTIVE TEST / FUEL CUT ALL.
(f) Attach the spark plug assembly to the cylinder head.
(g) Perform the FUEL CUT ALL operation (press the RIGHT
or LEFT button to change the ON).
