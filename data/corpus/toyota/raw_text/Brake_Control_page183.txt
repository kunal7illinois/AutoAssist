BC–177
BRAKE CONTROL – VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM (for ADVICS Made)
DTC C0226/21 SFR Solenoid Circuit
DTC C0236/22 SFL Solenoid Circuit
DTC C0246/23 SRR Solenoid Circuit
DTC C0256/24 SRL Solenoid Circuit
DTC C1225/25 SM Solenoid Circuit
DESCRIPTION
These solenoids turn on when signals are received from the skid control ECU and they control the
pressure acting on the wheel cylinders to control the braking force.
