8 CHECK DTC
(a) Connect the connectors to the occupant classification
ECU and the 4 occupant classification sensors.
(b) Connect the negative (-) terminal cable to the battery.
(c) Turn the ignition switch on (IG), and wait for at least 60
seconds.
(d) Clear the DTCs stored in the center airbag sensor
assembly (See page RS-41).
(e) Clear the DTCs stored in the occupant classification
RS ECU (See page RS-251).
(f) Turn the ignition switch off.
(g) Turn the ignition switch on (IG), and wait for at least 60
seconds.
(h) Check the DTCs (See page RS-251).
OK:
DTC is not output.

9 REPLACE OCCUPANT CLASSIFICATION ECU
(a) Turn the ignition switch off.
(b) Disconnect the negative (-) terminal cable from the
battery.
(c) Replace the occupant classification ECU (See page RS-
469).
HINT:
Perform the inspection using parts from a normal vehicle
if possible.
