CA–107
CAN COMMUNICATION – CAN COMMUNICATION SYSTEM
Short to GND in CAN Bus Line
DESCRIPTION
A short to GND is suspected in the CAN bus wire when the resistance between terminals 4 (CG) and 6
(CANH), or terminals 4 (CG) and 14 (CANL) of the DLC3 is below 200 Ω.
Symptoms Trouble Area
• Short to GND in CAN bus wire
• ECM
• Brake actuator assembly (Skid control ECU)
• Steering angle sensor
