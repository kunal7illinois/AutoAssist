CA–72
CAN COMMUNICATION – CAN COMMUNICATION SYSTEM
Open in CAN Main Bus Line
DESCRIPTION
There may be an open circuit in the CAN main bus wire and/or the DLC3 branch wire when the resistance
between terminals 6 (CANH) and 14 (CANL) of the DLC3 is 69 Ω or more.
