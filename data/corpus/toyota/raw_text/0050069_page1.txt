ES–410
2AZ-FE ENGINE CONTROL SYSTEM – THROTTLE BODY
ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION
1. CHECK THROTTLE BODY
(a) Check the throttle control motor operating sounds.
(1) Turn the ignition switch ON.
(2) When pressing the accelerator pedal, check
the operating sound of the running motor. Make
sure that no friction noises emit from the motor.
If friction noise exists, replace the throttle body.
(b) Check the throttle position sensor.
Intelligent Tester
(1) Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.
(2) Turn the ignition switch ON.
(3) Push the intelligent tester main switch ON.
ES
(4) Enter the following menus: DIAGNOSIS /
ENHANCED OBD II / DATA LIST / THROTTLE
POS.
(5) Depress the accelerator pedal. When the
throttle valve is fully opened, check that the
value of "THROTTLE POS" is within the
DLC3 specification.
Standard throttle valve opening percentage:
60% or more
CAN VIM NOTICE:
When checking the standard throttle valve
B127989E01 opening percentage, the shift lever should
be in the N position.
If the percentage is less than 60%, replace the
throttle body.
