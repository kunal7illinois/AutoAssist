(cid:3)AIRBAG SENSOR ASSEMBLY
(cid:1) It reaches a deploy judgment to deploy the dual stage driver’s and front passenger’s airbags, driver’s knee
airbag, and pretensioners based on the signals received from the front airbag sensor and the airbag sensor
assembly. In addition, it can reach a deploy judgment to deploy the SRS side airbags and SRS curtain shield
airbags based on signals received from the side & curtain shield airbag sensors and curtain shield airbag
sensors. Furthermore, it is equipped with a diagnosis function to perform self-diagnosis in case of system
malfunctions.
(cid:1) Each signal is transmitted as follows:
Target ECU Signal Communication path
ECM Fuel Cut Signal CAN communication circuit
SRS Warning Light ON Demand Signal
Combination Meter Driver Seat Belt Remainder Light ON Demand CAN communication circuit
