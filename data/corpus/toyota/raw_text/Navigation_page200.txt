NS–195
NAVIGATION – MULTI-DISPLAY
REMOVAL
1. EJECT MAP DISC
(a) Turn the ignition switch on (ACC).
(b) Push the "CLOSE" button.
(c) Touch "MAP EJECT" on the navigation screen to
eject the map disc.
2. REMOVE SHIFT LEVER KNOB SUB-ASSEMBLY (for
Automatic Transaxle) (See page IP-24)
3. REMOVE SHIFT LEVER KNOB SUB-ASSEMBLY (for
Manual Transaxle) (See page IP-24)
4. REMOVE NO. 1 INSTRUMENT CLUSTER FINISH
PANEL GARNISH (See page IP-24)
5. REMOVE NO. 2 INSTRUMENT CLUSTER FINISH
PANEL GARNISH (See page IP-25)
6. REMOVE FLOOR SHIFT POSITION INDICATOR
HOUSING SUB-ASSEMBLY (for Automatic
Transaxle) (See page IP-25)
7. REMOVE UPPER CONSOLE PANEL (for Manual
Transaxle) (See page IP-25)
8. REMOVE UPPER CONSOLE REAR PANEL SUB-
ASSEMBLY (for Automatic Transaxle) (See page IP-
26)
9. REMOVE UPPER CONSOLE REAR PANEL SUB-
ASSEMBLY (for Manual Transaxle) (See page IP-26)
10. REMOVE UPPER CONSOLE PANEL SUB-ASSEMBLY
(See page IP-27)
11. REMOVE INSTRUMENT PANEL NO. 2 REGISTER
ASSEMBLY (See page IP-27)
12. REMOVE NAVIGATION RECEIVER WITH HEATER
CONTROL PANEL ASSEMBLY
(a) Remove the 4 bolts.
(b) Pull the navigation receiver with heater control
panel assembly toward the rear of the vehicle and
disengage the 4 clips.
(c) Disconnect each connector and remove the
navigation receiver with heater control panel
assembly.
