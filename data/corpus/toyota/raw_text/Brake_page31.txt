BRAKE CONTROL – ANTI-LOCK BRAKE SYSTEM
DATA LIST / ACTIVE TEST
1. DATA LIST
HINT:
According to the DATA LIST displayed by the intelligent
tester, the value of the switch, sensor, actuator and other
items can be read without removing any parts. Reading
the DATA LIST as a first step in troubleshooting is one of
the methods to save labor time.
(a) Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.
Intelligent Tester
(b) Turn the ignition switch on (IG).
(c) According to the display on the tester, read the
"DATA LIST".
