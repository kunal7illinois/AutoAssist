BODY ELECTRICAL - SLIDING ROOF SYSTEM BE-177
3. Sliding Roof Open Warning
When the ignition switch/engine switch* is changed from IG-ON to OFF and the driver door is opened when
the sliding roof is open, the siding roof ECU sounds the buzzer in the combination meter. Then, a warning
message appears on the multi-information display.
The warning is activated if all of the following conditions are met:
(cid:3) Sliding roof is not fully closed.
Warning Condition
(cid:3) Ignition switch/engine switch* is “OFF”
(cid:3) Driver door is opened.
