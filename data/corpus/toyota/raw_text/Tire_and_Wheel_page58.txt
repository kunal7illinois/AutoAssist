TW–57
TIRE AND WHEEL – TIRE PRESSURE WARNING SYSTEM
DTC C2171/71 Transmitter ID not Registered
DESCRIPTION
The IDs of each tire pressure warning valve and transmitters are registered to the tire pressure warning
ECU.
When the ECU detects that an ID is not the one of registered IDs, or cannot receive an ID signal, a DTC is
output.
