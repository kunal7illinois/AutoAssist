2AZ-FE EXHAUST – EXHAUST PIPE
REMOVAL
1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY
TERMINAL
CAUTION:
Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the
cable from the negative (-) battery terminal to
prevent airbag and seat belt pretensioner activation.
2. REMOVE HEATED OXYGEN SENSOR (for Bank 1
Sensor 2) (See page EC-24)
3. REMOVE TAILPIPE ASSEMBLY
(a) Remove the 2 bolts and 2 compression springs.
(b) Disconnect the 2 exhaust pipe supports, and
remove the tailpipe.
(c) Remove the gasket from the center exhaust pipe.
