CAN COMMUNICATION – CAN COMMUNICATION SYSTEM
6. HOW TO DISTINGUISH THE CAN J/C CONNECTOR
(a) In the CAN communication system, the shape of all
connectors connected to the CAN J/C No. 1 is the
same. The connectors connected to the CAN J/C
No. 1 can be connecting side of the junction
connector.
HINT:
See "TERMINALS OF ECU" (See page CA-10) for
bus wire color or the type of connecting surface.
