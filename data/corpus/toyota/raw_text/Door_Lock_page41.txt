DL–41
DOOR LOCK – POWER DOOR LOCK CONTROL SYSTEM
Rear Door Lock Motor LH Circuit
DESCRIPTION
The rear left door lock motor is built into the rear left door lock assembly.
The main body ECU controls the rear left door lock motor to lock/unlock the rear left door. This ECU
applies current from terminal ACT+ to terminal ACT- to operate the motor to lock the door. It reverses the
direction of the current flow to operate the motor to unlock the door.
