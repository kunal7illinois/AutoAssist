RS–448
SUPPLEMENTAL RESTRAINT SYSTEM – FRONT AIRBAG SENSOR
INSTALLATION
HINT:
• Use the same procedures for the RH side and LH side.
• The procedures listed below are for the LH side.
1. INSTALL FRONT AIRBAG SENSOR
(a) Check that the ignition switch is off.
(b) Check that the battery negative (-) cable is
disconnected.
CAUTION:
Wait for 90 seconds after disconnecting the
cable to prevent airbag deployment.
(c) Connect the connector.
(d) Install the front airbag sensor with the bolt.
Torque:17.5 N*m (179 kgf*cm, 13 ft.*lbf)
NOTICE:
• If the front airbag sensor has been dropped,
or there are any cracks, dents or other
defects in the case, bracket or connector,
replace it with a new one.
• When installing the front airbag sensor, be
careful that the SRS wiring does not interfere
with other parts and that it is not pinched
between other parts.
(e) Check that there is no looseness in the installation
parts of the front airbag sensor.
