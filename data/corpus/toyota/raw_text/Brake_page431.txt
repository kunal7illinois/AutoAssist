BC–420
BRAKE CONTROL – VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM (for BOSCH Made)
TC and CG Terminal Circuit
DESCRIPTION
DTC output mode is set by connecting terminals TC and CG of the DLC3.
DTCs are indicated by the blinking pattern of the ABS warning light.

HINT:
When warning lights continue to blink, a ground short in the wiring of terminal TC of the DLC3 or an
internal ground short in one or more ECUs is suspected.
