EI–92
ENGINE IMMOBILISER – ENGINE IMMOBILISER SYSTEM (w/o Smart Key System)
Security Indicator Light Circuit
DESCRIPTION
When the transponder key is registered, the transponder key ECU assembly outputs the key registration
condition by lighting up, blinking or turning off the security indicator.
