5. INSPECT CAMSHAFT
(a) Inspect the camshaft for runout.
(1) Place the camshaft on V-blocks.

Maximum circle runout:
0.03 mm (0.0012 in.)
If the circle runout is greater than the
maximum, replace the camshaft.
