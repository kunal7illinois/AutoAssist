variation between the atmospheric pressure, before
and after conduction of the purge flow monitor, is
measured by the ECM.

Required Sensors/Components EVAP VSV and pump module
Frequency of Operation Once per driving cycle
Duration Within 15 minutes
MIL Operation 2 driving cycles

Typical Enabling Conditions: ES
EVAP key-off monitor runs when all of following conditions are met: -
Atmospheric pressure 70 to 110 kPa (525 to 825 mmHg)
Battery voltage 10.5 V or more
Vehicle speed Below 2.5 mph (4 km/h)
Engine switch OFF
Time after key off 5, 7 or 9.5 hours
EVAP pressure sensor malfunction (P0450, P0451, P0452 and/or
Not detected
P0453)
EVAP VSV Not operated by scan tool
Vent valve Not operated by scan tool
