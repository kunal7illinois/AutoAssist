BC–414
BRAKE CONTROL – VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM (for BOSCH Made)
Slip Indicator Light does not Come ON
DESCRIPTION
The skid control ECU is connected to the combination meter assembly via CAN communication.
The SLIP indicator light blinks during VSC and/or TRAC operation.
When the system fails, the SLIP indicator light comes on to warn the driver (See page BC-278).
