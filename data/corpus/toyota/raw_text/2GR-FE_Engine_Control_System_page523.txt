ES–511
2GR-FE ENGINE CONTROL SYSTEM – KNOCK SENSOR
21. REMOVE KNOCK CONTROL SENSOR
(a) Disconnect the 2 knock control sensor connectors.
(b) Remove the 2 bolts and 2 knock control sensors.
A129615
INSPECTION
1. INSPECT KNOCK CONTROL SENSOR
(a) Using an ohmmeter, measure the resistance
between the terminals. ES
Standard resistance:
120 to 280 kΩ at 20°C (68°F)
If the result is not as specified, replace the sensor.
