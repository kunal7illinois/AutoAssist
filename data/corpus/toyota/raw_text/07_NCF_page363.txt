BODY ELECTRICAL - METER BE-29
4. Multi-information Display
General
(cid:3) The multi-information display has three modes:
Mode Outline
(cid:3) Five types of information can be displayed: outside temperature, driving
Cruise Information range, average fuel consumption since refueling, distance driven since
(See page BE-31) engine start, and average speed since engine start.
(cid:3) The display can be switched by using the DISP switch.
Warning Interrupts the multi-information display immediately when a warning
(See page BE-32) occurs.
Diagnosis DTC (Diagnostic Trouble Code) for the brake control system (TRAC and
(See page CH-137) VSC) can be displayed.
