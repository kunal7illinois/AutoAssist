ET–33
EXTERIOR – REAR SPOILER
INSTALLATION
1. INSTALL REAR SPOILER
HINT:
When installing the spoiler, heat the vehicle body and
spoiler using a heat light.
Heating temperature
Item Temperature
Vehicle Body 40 to 60°C (104 to 140°F)
Spoiler 20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)
NOTICE:
Do not heat the vehicle body and rear spoiler
excessively.
(a) Clean the vehicle body surface.
ET
(1) Using a heat light, heat the vehicle body
surface.
(2) Remove the double-sided tape from the vehicle
body.
(3) Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with
cleaner.
(b) Clean the rear spoiler. (If reusing the rear spoiler)
(1) Using a heat light, heat the rear spoiler.
(2) Remove the double-sided tape from the rear
spoiler.
(3) Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with
cleaner.
(4) Apply new double-sided tape to the rear
spoiler.
Double-sided Tape
1.2 mm
