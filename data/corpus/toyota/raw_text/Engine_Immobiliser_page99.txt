ENGINE IMMOBILISER – ENGINE IMMOBILISER SYSTEM (w/o Smart Key System)
Door Courtesy Switch Circuit
DESCRIPTION
When an additional transponder key is registered, the transponder key ECU assembly detects the front
door courtesy light switch open/close condition, and enters the key registration mode.
