(h) Precautions for the entry unlock mode switching
function:
(1) Perform the entry unlock mode switching
function within a 1 m (3.3 ft) area around the

cannot be performed within 5 seconds of the
previous mode change. Wait 5 seconds or more
before performing a mode change.
