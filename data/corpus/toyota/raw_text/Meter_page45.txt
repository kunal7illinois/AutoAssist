ME–45
METER – METER / GAUGE SYSTEM
Entire Combination Meter does not Operate
DESCRIPTION
This circuit is the power source circuit for the meter. This circuit provides two types of power sources; one
is a constant power source mainly used as a backup power source, and the other is a power source
mainly used for signal transmission. The constant power source is mainly used as a backup power source
of the meter CPU, however, it is also used for communication. If a voltage of 12 V is not applied to
terminal IG+ when turn the ignition switch on (IG), the indicator will not operate.
