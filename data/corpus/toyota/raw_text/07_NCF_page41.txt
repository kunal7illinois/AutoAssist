5. Chain Tensioner
(cid:1) The chain tensioner uses a spring and oil pressure to maintain proper chain tension at all times.
The chain tensioner suppresses noise generated by the chain. A ratchet type non-return mechanism is also
used.
(cid:1) To improve serviceability, the chain tensioner is constructed so that it can be removed and installed from
the outside of the timing chain cover.
