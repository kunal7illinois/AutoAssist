CC–39
CRUISE CONTROL – CRUISE CONTROL MAIN SWITCH
REMOVAL
1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY
TERMINAL
CAUTION:
Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the
cable from the negative (-) battery terminal to
prevent airbag and seat belt pretensioner activation
(See page RS-1).
2. REMOVE LOWER NO. 3 STEERING WHEEL COVER
(See page RS-349)
3. REMOVE LOWER NO. 2 STEERING WHEEL COVER
(See page RS-349)
4. REMOVE STEERING PAD (See page RS-350)
5. REMOVE CRUISE CONTROL MAIN SWITCH
(a) Disconnect the connector.
(b) Remove the 2 screws and the cruise control main
switch.
