RS–246
SUPPLEMENTAL RESTRAINT SYSTEM – OCCUPANT CLASSIFICATION SYSTEM
• When the sensitivity deviates from the
standard value, retighten the bolts of the front
passenger seat without deforming the seat
rail. After performing this procedure, if the
sensitivity is not within the standard value,
replace the front seat assembly RH.
• When zero point calibration has not finished
normally, replace the front seat assembly RH.
