DATA LIST / ACTIVE TEST
1. DATA LIST
HINT:
By accessing the DATA LIST displayed on the intelligent
tester, it is possible to perform such functions as reading
the values of switches and sensors without removing any
parts. Reading the DATA LIST is the first step in
troubleshooting and is one method to save labor time.
(a) Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.
(b) Turn the engine switch on (IG) and turn the
intelligent tester on.
(c) Operate the intelligent tester according to the steps
on the display and select DATA LIST.
