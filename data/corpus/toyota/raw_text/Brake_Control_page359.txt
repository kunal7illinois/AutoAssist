5 RECONFIRM DTC
(a) Clear the DTCs (See page BC-302).
(b) Start the engine.
(c) At a speed of 18 mph (30 km/h) or more, drive the
vehicle, turn the steering wheel, and decelerate (depress
the brake pedal) the vehicle.
(d) Turn the ignition switch on (IG) again and check that no
CAN communication system DTC is output (See page
BC-302).
HINT:
Reinstall the sensors, connectors, etc. and restore the
vehicle to its prior condition before rechecking for DTCs.
