1. General

(cid:1) The intake manifold runner valve assembly is used in the intake manifold on the models for California
package.

control, For details of throttle body, refer to page EG-49.
(cid:1) The double wall structure compact exhaust manifold is used on the models for California package.
(cid:1) 2-way exhaust control system is provided to reduce noise and vibration in the main muffler.
