System Outline
1. Manual Down or Up Operation
When the power window master SW is pushed one step, the motor rotates to open the window.
When the power window master SW is pulled up one step, the motor rotates in the opposite direction, to close the window.
All the other windows can be opened/closed as well, by the operation of the power window master SW or respective power
window SW.
2. Auto Down or Up Operation
When the power window master SW is pushed two steps, the motor rotates to open the window automatically.
When the power window master SW is pulled up two steps, the motor rotates to close the window automatically. (w/ jam
protection)
3. Jam Protection Function
When any foreign object gets caught during power window UP operation, the motor rotates in the opposite direction to open
the window.
