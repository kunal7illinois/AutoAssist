BRAKE CONTROL – VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM (for ADVICS Made)
8 INSPECT BRAKE FLUID LEVEL WARNING SWITCH
(a) Remove the reservoir tank cap and strainer.
Brake Fluid Level Warning Switch:
(b) Disconnect the brake fluid level warning switch
connector.
(c) Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the
table below.
HINT:
A float is located inside the reservoir. Its position can be
changed by increasing or decreasing the level of brake
fluid.
Standard resistance
Tester Connection Condition Specified Condition
1 - 2 Float up (Switch OFF) 1.9 to 2.1 kΩ
1 - 2 Float down (Switch ON) Below 1 Ω
