CA–122
CAN COMMUNICATION – CAN COMMUNICATION SYSTEM
Open in One Side of CAN Branch Line
DESCRIPTION
If 2 or more ECUs and/or sensors do not appear on the intelligent tester "Communication Bus Check"
screen, one side of the CAN branch line may be open. (One side of the CAN-H [branch line] / CAN-L
[branch line] of the ECU and/or sensor is open.)
Symptom Trouble Area
• One side of the CAN branch line is open
• Steering angle sensor
• Skid control ECU
• ECM
2 or more ECUs and/or sensors do not appear on the intelligent tester
• Yaw rate sensor
"Communication Bus Check" screen.
• Main body ECU
• A/C amplifier
• Center airbag sensor
• Combination meter
