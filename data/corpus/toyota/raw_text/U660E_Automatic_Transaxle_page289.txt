11. INSPECT ONE-WAY CLUTCH ASSEMBLY
(a) Temporarily install the one-way clutch assembly to
the planetary ring gear.
(b) Rotate the one-way clutch assembly to check the
rotating direction for lock or free operation.
If the one-way clutch assembly does not operate
normally, replace it.

13. INSPECT NO. 2 BRAKE DISC
(a) Check if the sliding surfaces of the discs, plates,
and flange are worn or burnt. If necessary, replace
them.
