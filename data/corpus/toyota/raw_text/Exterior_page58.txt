12. REMOVE REAR DOOR FRONT WINDOW FRAME
MOULDING
HINT:
When removing the rear door front window frame
moulding, heat the vehicle body and rear door front
window frame moulding using a heat light.
B129819 Heating temperature
Item Temperature
Vehicle Body 40 to 60°C (104 to 140°F)
Moulding 20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)
NOTICE:
Do not heat the vehicle body and moulding
excessively.
(a) Using a heat light, heat the rear door front window
frame moulding.
