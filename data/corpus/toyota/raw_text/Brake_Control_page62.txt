BC–62
BRAKE CONTROL – ANTI-LOCK BRAKE SYSTEM
DTC C1237/37 Speed Sensor Rotor Faulty
DESCRIPTION
The skid control ECU measures the speed of each wheel by receiving signals from the speed sensor.
These signals are used for recognizing that all 4 wheels are operating properly.
Therefore, all wheel signals must be equal.
DTC No. DTC Detecting Condition Trouble Area
• Brake actuator assembly (skid control ECU)
• Speed sensor rotor (Front)
When any of the following is detected:
• Rear axle hub and bearing assembly
• Wheel speed difference between the wheels.
C1237/37 • Speed sensor
• Wheel sensor signal failure.
• Speed sensor circuit
• ABS control continues for 60 sec. or more.
• Tire and wheel size
• Tire deformation
