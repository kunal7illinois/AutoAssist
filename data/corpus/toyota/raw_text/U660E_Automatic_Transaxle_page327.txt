1. INSPECT FRONT OIL PUMP AND GEAR BODY SUB-

2. INSPECT CLEARANCE OF FRONT OIL PUMP AND
GEAR BODY SUB-ASSEMBLY
(a) Push the driven gear to one side of the body.
(b) Using a feeler gauge, measure the clearance.
Standard clearance:
0.08 to 0.15 mm (0.0031 to 0.0059 in.)
Maximum clearance:
0.15 mm (0.0059 in.)
If the clearance is greater than the maximum,
replace the oil pump body sub-assembly.
