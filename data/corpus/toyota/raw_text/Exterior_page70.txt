15. REMOVE FRONT DOOR STRIPE
(a) Using a heat light, heat the front door stripe.

Item Temperature
Vehicle Body 40 to 60°C (104 to 140°F)
NOTICE:
Do not heat the vehicle body excessively.

parallel to the vehicle body to pull it off.
