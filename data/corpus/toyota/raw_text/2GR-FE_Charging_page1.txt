2GR-FE CHARGING – CHARGING SYSTEM
CHARGING SYSTEM
PRECAUTION
1. Check that the battery cables are connected to the
correct terminals.
2. Disconnect the battery cables when the battery is
given a quick charge.
3. Do not perform tests with a high voltage insulation
resistance tester.
4. Never disconnect the battery while the engine is
running.
5. Check that the charging cable nut is tightened on
terminal B of the generator and the engine room R/B.
