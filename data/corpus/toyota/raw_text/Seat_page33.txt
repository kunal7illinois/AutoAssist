SEAT – FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY (for Power Seat)
REMOVAL
1. REMOVE FRONT SEAT HEADREST ASSEMBLY
2. REMOVE SEAT TRACK COVER
(a) Operate the power seat switch knob and move the
seat to the position farthest forward.
(b) Using a screwdriver, detach the 4 claws and remove
the seat track cover.
HINT:
Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

3. REMOVE INNER SEAT TRACK BRACKET COVER
(a) Using a screwdriver, detach the 4 claws and remove
the inner seat track bracket cover.
4. REMOVE FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY
(a) Operate the power seat switch knob and move the
seat to the rearmost position.
