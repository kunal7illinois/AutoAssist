DESCRIPTION
The driver side knee airbag squib circuit consists of the center airbag sensor assembly and the driver side
knee airbag assembly.
This circuit instructs the SRS to deploy when deployment conditions are met.
These DTCs are recorded when a malfunction is detected in the driver side knee airbag squib circuit.
