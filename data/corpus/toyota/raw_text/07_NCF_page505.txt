SEAT HEATER SYSTEM
(cid:4)DESCRIPTION
(cid:3) This system is optional equipment on the driver and front passenger seats of leather seat models.
(cid:3) A seat heater switch with a built-in indicator light for checking the heater operation is provided.
(cid:3) The output temperature of the seat heater is controlled by thermostat that are enclosed in the cushion.
