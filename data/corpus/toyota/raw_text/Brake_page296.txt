BC–285
BRAKE CONTROL – VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM (for BOSCH Made)
(1) Operation description
The skid control ECU detects wheelspin
condition by receiving signals from each speed
sensor and the ECM via CAN communication.
The skid control ECU controls engine torque
with the ECM via CAN communication and
brake hydraulic pressure through the pump and
solenoid valve.
The SLIP indicator light blinks when the system
is operating. If a malfunction occurs in the TRAC
system, both master caution and SLIP indicator
lights will come on and the DTC will be displayed
on the multi-information display.
