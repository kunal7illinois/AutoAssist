INSPECTION
1. INSPECT POWER STEERING RACK
(a) Using a dial indicator, check for runout of the
steering rack.
Maximum runout:
0.3 mm (0.0118 in.)
NOTICE:
Make sure that the steering rack is placed
horizontally.
C132328
If runout exceeds the maximum, replace the power
steering link assembly.
(b) Check the rack surface for wear and damage.
2. INSPECT TIE ROD ASSEMBLY LH
(a) Secure the tie rod assembly LH in a vise.
(b) Install the nut to the stud bolt.
(c) Flip the ball joint back and forth 5 times or more.
(d) Using a torque wrench, turn the nut continuously at
a rate of 3 to 5 seconds per turn and take the torque
reading on the 5th turn.
Torque:Turning torque
0.98 to 3.92 N*m (10 to 40 kgf*cm, 8.7 to
34.7 in.*lbf)
C132329
If turning torque is not within the specified range,
replace the tie rod assembly LH.
