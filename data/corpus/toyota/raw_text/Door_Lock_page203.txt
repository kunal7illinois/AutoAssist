DL–193
DOOR LOCK – SMART KEY SYSTEM
Luggage Compartment Door Open Switch Circuit
DESCRIPTION
If the certification ECU detects that the luggage compartment open switch is pressed when the driver is
carrying the electrical key or when the electrical key is locked in the luggage compartment door, the
certification ECU sends a luggage compartment door open motor drive request signal to the main body
ECU.
