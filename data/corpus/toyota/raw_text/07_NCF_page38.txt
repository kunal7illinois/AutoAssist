(cid:2)VALVE MECHANISM
1. General
(cid:1) Each cylinder is equipped with 2 intake valves and 2 exhaust valves. Intake and exhaust efficiency has been
increased due to the larger total port areas.
(cid:1) The valves are directly opened and closed by 2 camshafts.
(cid:1) The intake and exhaust camshafts are driven by a chain. The VVT-i system used for the intake camshaft
is used to increase fuel economy, engine performance and reduce exhaust emissions.
(cid:1) The shimless type valve lifter is used.
