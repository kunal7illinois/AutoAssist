DL–53
DOOR LOCK – POWER DOOR LOCK CONTROL SYSTEM
Luggage Compartment Door Key Cylinder Switch Circuit
DESCRIPTION
When the luggage compartment door lock cylinder is turned to unlock the door by key, there is continuity
between terminal TSW of the main body ECU and body ground.
