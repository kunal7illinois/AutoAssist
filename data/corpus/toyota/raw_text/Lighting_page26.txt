LIGHTING – LIGHTING SYSTEM
DTC B1244 Light Sensor Circuit Malfunction
DESCRIPTION
The automatic light control sensor detects ambient light, converts it into an electrical signal, and outputs it
to the main body ECU. The main body ECU turns on or off the headlights and taillights according to the
signal.
