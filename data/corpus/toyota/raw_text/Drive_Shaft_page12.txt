11. REMOVE FRONT DRIVE SHAFT ASSEMBLY RH DS
(a) Using a screwdriver, remove the bearing bracket
hole snap ring.
(b) Remove the bolt and front drive shaft assembly RH
from the drive shaft bearing bracket.
NOTICE:
Do not damage the boot and oil seal.
C080304E03
12. SECURE FRONT AXLE HUB SUB-ASSEMBLY
(a) Secure the front axle hub bearing.
SST
SST 09608-16042 (09608-02021, 09608-02041)
NOTICE:
The hub bearing may be damaged if it is
subjected to the vehicle's full weight, such as
moving the vehicle with the drive shaft removed.
If it is necessary to place the vehicle's weight on
the hub bearing, first support it with SST.
