SR–34
STEERING COLUMN – ELECTRIC STEERING LOCK
Power Source Circuit
DESCRIPTION
This circuit supplies voltage from the battery to terminal B of the steering lock ECU. This circuit is used as
power source for the steering lock CPU, motor, communication, and peripheral circuits.
