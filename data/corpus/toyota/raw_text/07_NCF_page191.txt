CHASSIS - U250E AUTOMATIC TRANSAXLE CH-27
Transmission Control Switch and Park/Neutral Position Switch
The ECM uses these switches to detect the shift lever position.
(cid:3) The park/neutral position switch sends the P, R, N, D, 3 and 2 position signals to the ECM.
(cid:3) The transmission control switch is installed inside the shift lever assembly. This switch sends the 4 and
L position signals to the ECM.
(cid:3) The ECM sends these shift position signals to the combination meter (meter ECU) through CAN
communication. The combination meter (meter ECU) controls the shift position indicator light based on
these signals.
