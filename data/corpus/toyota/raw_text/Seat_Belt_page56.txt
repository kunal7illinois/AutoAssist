SB–52
SEAT BELT – REAR SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY
5. INSTALL REAR SEAT SHOULDER BELT COVER
(a) Engage the 4 claws and install the rear seat
shoulder belt cover.
HINT:
Use the same procedures for the RH side and the
LH side.
B132012
6. CONNECT REAR SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY
Protruding Parts (for LH Side)
(a) Connect the floor end of the rear seat outer belt
assembly with the bolt.
Torque:42 N*m (428 kgf*cm, 31 ft.*lbf)
NOTICE:
Do not allow the anchor part of the rear seat
outer belt assembly to overlap protruding parts
of the floor panel.
(b) Check if the ELR locks.
B132008E01
NOTICE:
The check should be performed with the outer
belt assembly installed.
(1) With the belt assembly installed, check that the
belt locks when it is pulled out quickly.
7. CONNECT REAR SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY
Protruding Parts (for RH Side)
(a) Connect the floor end of the rear seat outer belt
assembly with the bolt.
SB Torque:42 N*m (428 kgf*cm, 31 ft.*lbf)
NOTICE:
Do not allow the anchor part of the rear seat
outer belt assembly to overlap protruding parts
of the floor panel.
(b) Check if the ELR locks.
B132009E01
NOTICE:
The check should be performed with the outer
belt assembly installed.
(1) With the belt assembly installed, check that the
belt locks when it is pulled out quickly.
