BC–73
BRAKE CONTROL – ANTI-LOCK BRAKE SYSTEM
DTC C1249/49 Open in Stop Light Switch Circuit
DESCRIPTION
This circuit recognizes brake operation by sending a stop light signal to the skid control ECU.
The skid control ECU has an open detection circuit, which outputs this DTC when detecting an open in the
stop light signal input line (STP terminal) or the ground line of the stop light circuit with the stop light switch
off (brake pedal not depressed).
