ENGINE IMMOBILISER SYSTEM
(w/o Smart Key System)
PRECAUTION
1. PRECAUTIONS FOR THE KEY
(a) The key is a precision instrument. Be sure to
observe the following:
(1) Do not drop or strike the key.
(2) Do not allow the key to be kept in a high
temperature place for a long time.
(3) Do not use an ultrasonic washing machine to
clean the key.
(4) Keep the key away from magnets or magnetized
items during use.
(5) Do not attach any stickers to the key.
