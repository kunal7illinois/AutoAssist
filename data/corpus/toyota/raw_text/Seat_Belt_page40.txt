SEAT BELT – REAR SEAT INNER BELT ASSEMBLY
27. DISCONNECT REAR SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY
(for LH Side)
(a) Remove the bolt and disconnect the floor end of the
rear seat outer belt assembly.

28. DISCONNECT REAR SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY
(for RH Side)
(a) Remove the bolt and disconnect the floor end of the
rear seat outer belt assembly.
B132007
29. REMOVE REAR SEAT SHOULDER BELT COVER
(a) Using a screwdriver, disengage the 4 claws and
remove the rear seat shoulder belt cover.
HINT:
• Tape the screwdriver tip before use.
• Use the same procedures for the RH side and
the LH side.
