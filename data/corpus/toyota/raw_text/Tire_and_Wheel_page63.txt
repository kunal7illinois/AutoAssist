TIRE AND WHEEL – TIRE PRESSURE WARNING SYSTEM
DTC C2177/77 Initialization not Completed
DESCRIPTION
Initialization is necessary after replacing any of the ECU, tires with different tire pressure, or tire pressure
warning valve and transmitter, after rotating the tires or when a new vehicle is delivered.
