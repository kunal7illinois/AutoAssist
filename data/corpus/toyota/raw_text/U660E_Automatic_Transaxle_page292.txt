19. INSPECT CLEARANCE OF NO. 2 CLUTCH DISC
(a) Install the direct multiple disc clutch assembly onto
the transaxle rear cover.
AX (b) Using a dial indicator, measure the No. 2 clutch
pack clearance while applying and releasing
compressed air (200 kPa, 2.0 kgf/cm2, 29 psi).
HINT:
Measure the clearance at 3 points where the
diameter of the No. 2 direct multiple clutch piston is
approximately 152 mm (5.98 in.) and calculate the
average.
Pack clearance:
0.544 to 0.744 mm (0.0214 to 0.0293 in.)
If the pack clearance is not as specified, inspect the
discs, plates and flange.
HINT:
There are 6 different thicknesses of flanges
available.
