Shift Lock Mechanism
If the brake pedal is depressed with the power SW is pushed to IG ON position (Power SW type), or the ignition SW is turned
to ON (Ignition SW type), the shift lock control ECU is activated, allowing the driver to change the shift lever to a position
other than the P position.
