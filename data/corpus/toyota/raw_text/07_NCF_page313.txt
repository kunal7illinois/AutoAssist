(cid:3)STEERING COLUMN
1. Tilt and Power Telescopic Steering Column
(cid:3) The manual tilt and telescopic mechanism mainly consists of a tilt lever, steering column tube, breakaway
bracket, tilt lever lock bolt, and tilt steering stoppers.
(cid:3) The tilt lever controls the tilt and the telescope motion.
(cid:3) With the tilt adjustment range of 3.2(cid:5) (stepless) and the telescopic adjustment range of 40 mm (1.57 in.),
the steering column can be adjusted to a position selected by the driver.
(cid:3) When the tilt and telescopic mechanism is in its locked state, the tilt lever at B position causes the cam
of the tilt steering stoppers to tighten the steering column tube.
(cid:3) When the tilt and telescopic mechanism is in its free state, the tilt lever at A position causes the cam of
the tilt steering stoppers to loosen the steering column tube.
