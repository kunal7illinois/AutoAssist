2GR-FE ENGINE CONTROL SYSTEM – CAMSHAFT TIMING OIL CONTROL VALVE
ES–485
ASSEMBLY
2. INSTALL CAMSHAFT TIMING OIL CONTROL VALVE
(for Bank 2 Exhaust Side)
New O-Ring
(a) Apply a light coat of engine oil to a new O-ring and
install it to the camshaft timing oil control valve.
A093611E01
(b) Install the camshaft timing oil control valve with the
bolt.
Torque:10 N*m (102 kgf*cm, 7 ft.*lbf)
NOTICE:
ES
Make sure that the O-ring is not cracked or
jammed when installing the oil control valve.
(c) Connect the camshaft timing oil control valve
connector.
A135696
