INSPECTION
1. INSPECT FRONT DRIVE SHAFT ASSEMBLY
(a) Check that there is no excessive play in the
outboard joint.
(b) Check that the inboard joint slides smoothly in the
thrust direction.
(c) Check that there is no excessive play in the radial
directions of the inboard joint.
(d) Check the boots for damage.
