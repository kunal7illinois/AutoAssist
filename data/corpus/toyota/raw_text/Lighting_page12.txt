LIGHTING – LIGHTING SYSTEM
CUSTOMIZE PARAMETERS
HINT:
The followings are the possible items to be customized.
NOTICE:
• Before attempting to customize vehicle settings,
confirm whether it is possible to make the change that
the customer has requested.
• Be sure to record the current value before
customizing.
• In case of performing the troubleshooting, pay
attention because there is a possibility that the
function has been disabled by customizing. (Example:
In case of the symptom in which "The wireless
operation does not function", check that the wireless
operation has not been disabled by customizing, then
perform the troubleshooting.)
