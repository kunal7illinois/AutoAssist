2AZ-FE CHARGING – CHARGING SYSTEM
CHARGING SYSTEM
PRECAUTION
1. Check that the battery cables are connected to the
correct terminals.
2. Disconnect the battery cables if a quick charge is
given to the battery.
3. Do not perform tests with a high voltage insulation
resistance tester.
4. Never disconnect the battery while the engine is
running.
5. Check that the charging cable is tightly connected
onto terminal B of the generator and the fuse box.
6. Do not check whether the generator generates
current or not while terminal F is connected to the
other terminals.
