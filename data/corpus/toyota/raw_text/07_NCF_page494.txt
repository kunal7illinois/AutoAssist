CRUISE CONTROL SYSTEM
(cid:4)DESCRIPTION
(cid:3) The cruise control system is optional equipment on all models.
(cid:3) When the system is set to a desired vehicle speed, the throttle valve position is adjusted automatically to
maintain the vehicle speed without the driver having to depress the accelerator pedal.
(cid:3) This system effects control through the ETCS-i (Electronic Throttle Control System-intelligent).
