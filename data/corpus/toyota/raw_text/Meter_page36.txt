2. ACTIVE TEST
Performing the ACTIVE TEST using the intelligent tester
allows the meters, indicators and so on to operate
without parts removal. Performing the ACTIVE TEST as
the first step of troubleshooting is one way to shorten
labor time.
It is possible to display the DATA LIST on the intelligent
tester during the ACTIVE TEST.
(a) Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.
(b) Turn the ignition switch on (IG).
(c) Turn the tester ON.
(d) Enter following menus: DIAGNOSIS / OBD/MOBD /
ME METER / ACTIVE TEST.
(e) From the display on the tester, performing the
"ACTIVE TEST".
