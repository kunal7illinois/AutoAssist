ENGINE - 2AZ-FE ENGINE EG-15
(cid:4)LUBRICATION SYSTEM
1. General
(cid:3) The lubrication circuit is fully pressurized and oil passes through an oil filter.
(cid:3) The trochoidal type oil pump is chain-driven by the crankshaft.
(cid:3) The oil filter is attached downward from the crankcase to improve serviceability.
(cid:3) Along with the adoption of the VVT-i system, the cylinder head is provided with a VVT-i controller and
a camshaft timing oil control valve. This system operates using the engine oil.
