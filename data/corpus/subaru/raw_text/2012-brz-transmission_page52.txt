Manual transmission/transaxle - Manual transmission ASSY
39. Install the back-up light switch ASSY.
(1) Install the back-up light switch ASSY and a new gasket.
Standard value: T=32.3N•m {329.4kgf•cm} {23.8ft⋅lbf}
<Caution>
When a torque wrench extended with a union nut wrench is used, the
actual tightening torque becomes over-torque if the part is tightened
until the torque wrench reading reaches the specified tightening
torque.

clamps in the order from the rearmost, and connect the back-up
light switch ASSY wire and neutral switch wire.
